# Websites

## Canonical Links 
### Translations (Pāli Nikāyas & related)
- [**SuttaCentral**](https://suttacentral.net/{{suttacode}}?view=normal)
	- Main hub for early Buddhist texts with multiple translations, parallels, segmented Pāli, and tools.
- [**The Buddha’s Words**](http://thebuddhaswords.net/{{nikayacode}}/{{suttacode}}.html)
	- Clean HTML versions of many Pāli suttas in English and Pāli, organised by Nikāya.
- [**Dhamma.gift**](https://dhamma.gift/read/?q={{suttacode}})
	- Lightweight reader that aggregates sutta texts from multiple sources; good for quick reading.
- [**Dhamma Talks (Thanissaro Bhikkhu)**](https://www.dhammatalks.org/suttas/)
	- Large collection of sutta translations, books, and essays from the Thai Forest tradition.
- [**Ancient Buddhist Texts**](https://ancient-buddhist-texts.net/Buddhist-Texts/index.htm)
	- Pāli and English texts, metrical editions, and study material by Ānandajoti Bhikkhu.
- [**Pa-Auk Tipiṭaka Project**](http://tipitaka.paauksociety.org/)
	- Online Pāli Canon from the Pa-Auk tradition, with some supporting material.
- [**SuttaFriends**](https://suttafriends.org/sutta/)
	- Accessible English translations of Pāli suttas, with some Pāli text and topic-based navigation.
- [**Dharmafarer (Sutta Discovery)**](https://www.themindingcentre.org/dharmafarer/sutta-titles-2)
	- Annotated translations and in-depth study notes by Piya Tan; strong focus on early suttas.
- [**Hillside Hermitage – Suttas**](https://suttas.hillsidehermitage.org/)
	- Suttas and talks from Ajahn Nyanamoli and the Hillside Hermitage community, practice-focused.
- [**Colombo Dhamma Friends / “Serene Colombo”**](https://www.serenecolombo.org/category/suttas/)
	- Additional English sutta translations and teachings, often overlapping with SuttaFriends.
- [**Access to Insight (archive)**](https://www.accesstoinsight.org/)
	- Classic collection of Theravāda sutta translations and essays (archived but still widely referenced).
- [**ReadingFaithfully.org**](https://readingfaithfully.org/)
	- Resources to build a regular sutta reading practice: guides, reading plans, and study tips.
- [**Buddha Dust**](https://buddhadust.net/backmatter/indexes/sutta/sutta_toc.htm)
	- Extensive index pointing to sutta translations across several websites and collections.
- [**A Buddhist View**](https://abuddhistview.com/)
	- Essays and reflections grounded in early Buddhist teachings and practice.
- [**Ancient-Buddhist-Texts.net**](https://ancient-buddhist-texts.net/)
	- Comprehensive, freely available online collection of early and medieval Buddhist texts in Pāḷi, Sanskrit‑Prākṛt, and Classical Sanskrit 


### Pāli text & study tools
- [**BuddhaNexus – Pāli**](https://buddhanexus.net/pli/text/{{suttacode}})
	- Pāli text viewer with powerful parallel search across Pāli Nikāyas and Chinese Āgamas.
- [**Tipitaka.org**](https://www.tipitaka.org)
	- Online Pāli Canon in multiple scripts, with line-by-line text navigation.
- [**Digital Pāli Reader (web)**](https://www.digitalpalireader.online/_dprhtml/index.html)
	- Browser-based version of the classic DPR: instant word lookup and dictionary integration.
- [**Tipitaka Pali Reader**](https://americanmonk.org/tipitaka-pali-reader/)
	- Reader app with integrated dictionaries and search, designed for Pāli Canon study.
- [**Tipitaka.app**](https://tipitaka.app/index.html)
	- Mobile-friendly web app for browsing the Pāli Canon.
- [**Tipitaka Sutta (sutta.org)**](https://tipitaka.sutta.org)
	- Alternative front-end to Pāli texts with its own navigation structure.
- [**Digital Pāli Dictionary (DPDict)**](https://dpdict.net/)
	- Modern, searchable Pāli–English dictionary, useful alongside any Pāli text.
- [**PTS Converter (Dhamma-Dāna)**](https://pts.dhamma-dana.de)
	- Converts between PTS page references and standard sutta references for the main Nikāyas.
- [**Open.tipitaka.lk**]([https://pts.dhamma-dana.de](https://open.tipitaka.lk/))
	- Sri Lankan Tipitaka in various scipts and include audio for all pali texts.

### Āgamas & other early canons
- [**Dharma Pearls**](https://canon.dharmapearls.net/)
	- High-quality English translations of Chinese Āgama collections and related early Buddhist texts.
- [**BDK America (Bukkyō Dendō Kyōkai)**](https://www.bdkamerica.org/tripitaka-list/)
	- Translations from the Chinese canon, including some Āgama and early Mahāyāna texts.
- [**Dharmamitra**](https://dharmamitra.org/)
	- Extensive collection of English translations from the Chinese Buddhist Canon by Bhikshu Dharmamitra, covering Mahāyāna Sūtras, Vinaya, and Śāstras.

### Audio – listening to the suttas
- [**PaliAudio.com**](https://www.paliaudio.com/{{suttacode}})
	- Professional readings of Pāli Nikāya suttas (often based on SuttaCentral translations).
- [**Sutta Readings**](https://www.suttareadings.net/)
	- Curated audio readings of suttas from various translators and sources.
- [**SuttaCentral Voice**](https://www.sc-voice.net/?src=sc#/wiki/toc)
	- SuttaCentral’s audio front-end: listen to Pāli and translations with navigation synced to segments.
- [**Audio Tipitaka (Audtip)**](http://audtip.org/)
	- Long-term project offering audio versions of sutta translations; open to volunteers.
- [**Dharma Audiobooks**](http://dharmaaudiobooks.com/)
	- Professionally produced audiobooks of suttas, talks, and classic Buddhist writings.

### Helper tools & research
-  Simsapa Dhamma Reader https://simsapa.github.io/ 
	- Excelent sutta reader and Pali dictionary application.

- [**ReadingFaithfully – Sutta Tools Lab**](https://readingfaithfully.org/lab/)

- [**Awesome SuttaCentral (developer tools)**](https://suttacentral.github.io/awesome/)

## AI Tools
- [NORBU](https://norbu-ai.org/en/norbu) AI guide of the foundational principles of Buddhist teachings, help clarify key terms and concepts
- [NORBU Early Buddhist Texts](https://norbu-ai.org/en/norbu_ebt) contains only suttas from the Pali Tipitaka, Chinese Agamas, Sanskrit fragments as well as some sutras preserved in Tibetan translation.

#### [Notebook LM](https://notebooklm.google/)
It is a great AI tool that is limited only by the selected resources. It’s good for finding needed references. There are a few useful collections available:

- [Early Suttas](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/6a9818aa-3045-4f35-8afb-ec5f13571d40) - mūla of EBT*
    
- [Sutta Piṭaka + Commentaries](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/68815e7f-c5b6-4b12-a66e-9f80729b9015) - Second basket of the Tipiṭaka, mūla + atthakathā
    
- [Vinaya](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/d5bdd9b7-1b25-4e9c-b3ea-f19bb93c9081) - First basket of the Tipiṭaka, mūla part
    
- [Vinaya + Commentaries](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/118e3516-95d1-4f8d-9f8b-371ee078f621) - everything from the Tipiṭaka related to Vinaya - mūla + atthakathā + ṭīkā + aññā
    
- [Early Suttas + Vinaya](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/62ff61c6-b9e1-4524-8447-82b00ca6f6ff) - mūla: EBT* and Vinaya
    
- [Pātimokkha Pali Study](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/36c7a255-1b16-455c-bcde-14574d8f0dd2) - sources include: Bhikkhu Vibhaṅga Pāli–English (Ven. Brahmali); Pātimokkha Analysis — normal and extended versions (Ven. Ñāṇatusita); Buddhist Monastic Code I and II (Ven. Ṭhānissaro); SBS Pātimokkha Analysis
    
- [Anupubbasikkhā Gradual Illumination](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/317a21a5-267c-4153-9543-b5bcfdcc6625?artifactId=3dcc3a5a-9b0b-488b-aa00-aa60764f6340) - everything from Tipiṭaka (mūla, atthakathā, ṭīkā) related to the Gradual Training, compiled by Ā Virāga.
    
- [Pāli Tipiṭaka](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/8e21e964-8804-437d-a355-ed93666058ca) - Tipiṭaka mūla
    
- [Visuddhimagga](https://notebooklm.google.com/notebook/13187f85-d540-42c1-8ea8-5f723c5afab3)

## Buddhist Education / Dharma Blogging
- [**Digital Dictionary of Buddhism**](http://buddhism-dict.net/)
	- Large, scholarly online dictionary of Buddhist terms, texts, and people (requires login/guest access).
- [**La Parole du Bouddha**](http://www.buddha-vacana.org/fr/index.html)
	- French version of Buddha-Vacana offering Pāli suttas side-by-side with French translations.
- [**Abhidhamma.com**](https://abhidhamma.com/)
	- Dedicated to the Theravāda Abhidhamma with systematic explanations and digital translations.
- [**Agama (Buddhason)**](https://agama.buddhason.org/)
	- Chinese-language digital library providing Āgama sūtras alongside parallel Pāli texts.
- [**Virtual Autopsy (Australian Museum)**](https://autopsy2021.australian.museum/)
	- Interactive exhibit of a forensic autopsy; useful for contemplating the body and impermanence.
- [**Buddhist Publication Society (BPS)**](https://bps.lk/)
	- Sri Lankan publisher offering a vast library of downloadable Theravāda books and booklets.
- [**Buddhist eLibrary**](https://buddhistelibrary.org/)
	- Multi-tradition repository of downloadable eBooks, audio, and study materials.
- [**Buddhist University**](https://buddhistuniversity.net/)
	- Free online platform offering structured courses, syllabuses, and a digital library for early Buddhist studies.
- [**Critical Pāli Dictionary (Online)**](https://cpd.uni-koeln.de/)
	- Comprehensive, scholarly lexicon of the Pāli language maintained by the University of Cologne.
- [**Dhamma Dāna (German)**](https://dhamma-dana.de/)
	- Project offering free German-language Buddhist books, audio lectures, and study materials.
- [**Fake Buddha Quotes**](https://fakebuddhaquotes.com/)
	- Site investigating and debunking quotes falsely attributed to the Buddha.
- [**AnkiWeb Forums**](https://forums.ankiweb.net/)
	- Community support for Anki users; useful for troubleshooting flashcards for memorization.
- [**Gandhari.org**](https://gandhari.org/)
	- Scholarly collection of early Buddhist texts, inscriptions, and manuscripts in the Gāndhārī language.
- [**Jātaka Stories**](https://jatakastories.div.ed.ac.uk/)
	- Database of Jātaka tales with Pāli/Sanskrit text, English translations, and visual artwork.
- [**Leigh Brasington**](https://leighb.com/)
	- Teachings, articles, and resources on jhāna and insight practices from a senior Theravāda teacher.
- [**SC Citation Helper**](https://mini-sc.netlify.app/)
	- Simple tool to generate standardized citations (e.g., DN.1) for Pāli texts.
- [**NORBU (AI)**](https://norbu-ai.org/en/norbu_ebt)
	- Buddhist AI assistant designed to answer Dhamma and Vinaya questions ethically.
- [**Old Buddhist Objects (OBO)**](https://obo.genaud.net/)
	- Web library of selected Pāli Canon texts and suttas available in readable PDF formats.
- [**Dictionary of Pāḷi Roots**](https://obo.genaud.net/backmatter/glossology/pali_roots.htm)
	- Lists Pāḷi verbal roots with meanings and Sanskrit cognates for deep language study.
- [**Pali Translation Project**](https://palitranslation.org/)
	- Project aiming to produce and host high-quality, searchable English translations of the Pāli Canon.
- [**Pariyatti**](https://pariyatti.org/)
	- Non-profit bookstore and resource hub for Theravāda literature and Vipassana teachings.
- [**Pitaka.lk**](https://pitaka.lk/main/)
	- The Pāli Tipiṭaka available online with parallel Sinhala translations.
- [**Stanford Encyclopedia: Buddha**](https://plato.stanford.edu/entries/buddha/)
	- Peer-reviewed philosophical article covering the Buddha’s life, metaphysics, and epistemology.
- [**Schatztruhe Pali-Kanon**](https://schatztruhe-palikanon.net/)
	- German website providing structured explanations and study resources for the Pāli Canon.
- [**Sobhana.net**](https://sobhana.net/)
	- Audio-visual library of Dhamma talks, sutta chants, and texts in Pāli and English.
- [**TipitakaPali.org**](https://tipitakapali.org/)
	- Platform for the Chaṭṭha Saṅgāyana Tipiṭaka with integrated dictionaries and search tools.
- [**Buddha-Vacana**](https://www.buddha-vacana.org/)
	- Pāli learning platform with word-by-word glossaries for selected suttas.
- [**BuddhaNet**](https://www.buddhanet.net/)
	- Long-standing directory of Buddhist centers, eBooks, and global resources.
- [**Dhamma.ru**](https://www.dhamma.ru/)
	- Russian-language website offering Theravāda translations, books, and meditation guidance.
- [**Dhamma Wheel**](https://www.dhammawheel.com/)
	- Large English-language discussion forum focused on Theravāda Buddhism.
- [**DhammaWiki**](https://www.dhammawiki.com/index.php?title=Main_Page)
	- Encyclopedia of Buddhist teachings and terminology, focused on early Buddhism.
- [**OCBS Courses**](https://www.ocbs-courses.org/)
	- Self-paced academic Pāli language courses from the Oxford Centre for Buddhist Studies.
- [**Oxford Bibliographies: Buddhism**](https://www.oxfordbibliographies.com/browse?module_0=obo-9780195393521)
	- Curated academic research guides and reading lists on Buddhist topics.
- [**Palikanon.com**](https://www.palikanon.com/)
	- German and English collection of Buddhist texts, dictionaries, and meditation manuals.
- [**Words of the Buddha**](https://www.wordsofthebuddha.org/)
	- Side-by-side Pāli-English corpus with contextual tooltips and notes.
