## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses
## 6: The Book of the Sixes
### AN 6.74: Dutiyatajjhānasutta — First jhāna (2nd)

1"Cha, bhikkhave, dhamme appahāya abhabbo paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharituṁ. Katame cha? Kāmavitakkaṁ, byāpādavitakkaṁ, vihiṁsāvitakkaṁ, kāmasaññaṁ, byāpādasaññaṁ, vihiṁsāsaññaṁ – ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhamme appahāya abhabbo paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharituṁ.

2Cha, bhikkhave, dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharituṁ. Katame cha? Kāmavitakkaṁ, byāpādavitakkaṁ, vihiṁsāvitakkaṁ, kāmasaññaṁ, byāpādasaññaṁ, vihiṁsāsaññaṁ – ime kho, bhikkhave, cha dhamme pahāya bhabbo paṭhamaṁ jhānaṁ upasampajja viharitun"ti.

Dasamaṁ.


> 3Anāgāmi arahaṁ mittā,  
> saṅgaṇikārāmadevatā;  
> Samādhisakkhibhabbaṁ balaṁ,  
> tajjhānā apare duveti.
