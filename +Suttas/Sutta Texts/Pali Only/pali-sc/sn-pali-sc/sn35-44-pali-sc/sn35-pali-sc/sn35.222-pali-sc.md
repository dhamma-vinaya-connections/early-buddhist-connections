## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.222: The Interior as Impermanent
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are impermanent.

Seeing this … They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”

1"Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ … pe … jivhā aniccā … pe … mano anicco.

Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.
