## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.192-194: The Interior as Not-Self in the Three Times
#### B. Sujato


“bhikkhus, in the past … future … present the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are not-self …”

"Cakkhu, bhikkhave, anattā atītaṁ anāgataṁ paccuppannaṁ … pe … jivhā anattā … pe … mano anattā atīto anāgato paccuppanno. Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.
