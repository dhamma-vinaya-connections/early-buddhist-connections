## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.187: The Interior Will Be Impermanent in the Future
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, in the future the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind will be impermanent …”

1"Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṁ anāgataṁ … pe … jivhā aniccā anāgatā … pe … mano anicco anāgato. Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.
