## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.227: The Exterior as Not-Self
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and thoughts are not-self.

Seeing this … They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”

1"Rūpā, bhikkhave, anattā. Saddā … gandhā … rasā … phoṭṭhabbā … dhammā anattā.

Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.


Saṭṭhipeyyālo samatto.

2Chandenaṭṭhārasa honti,  
atītena ca dve nava;  
Yadaniccāṭṭhārasa vuttā,  
tayo ajjhattabāhirā;  
Peyyālo saṭṭhiko vutto,  
buddhenādiccabandhunāti.

Suttantāni saṭṭhi.
