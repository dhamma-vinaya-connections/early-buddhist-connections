## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.224: The Interior as Not-Self
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are not-self.

Seeing this … They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”

1"Cakkhu, bhikkhave, anattā … pe … jivhā anattā … pe … mano anattā.

Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.
