## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.226: The Exterior as Suffering
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and thoughts are suffering.

Seeing this … They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”

1"Rūpā, bhikkhave, dukkhā. Saddā … gandhā … rasā … phoṭṭhabbā … dhammā dukkhā.

Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.
