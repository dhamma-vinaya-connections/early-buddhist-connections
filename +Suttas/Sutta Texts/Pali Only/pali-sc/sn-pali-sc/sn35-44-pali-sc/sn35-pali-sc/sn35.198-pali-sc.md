## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.198-200: The Exterior as Suffering in the Three Times
#### B. Sujato


“bhikkhus, in the past … future … present sights, sounds, smells, tastes, touches, and thoughts are suffering …”

"Rūpā, bhikkhave, dukkhā atītā anāgatā paccuppannā. Saddā … gandhā … rasā … phoṭṭhabbā … dhammā dukkhā atītā anāgatā paccuppannā. Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.
