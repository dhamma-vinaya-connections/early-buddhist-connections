## 54. Ānāpānasaṁyutta: On Breath Meditation

### II. The Second Chapter — SN54.17: Giving Up the Fetters
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, when immersion due to mindfulness of breathing is developed and cultivated it leads to giving up the fetters …”

1"Ānāpānassatisamādhi, bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulīkato saṁyojanappahānāya saṁvattati … pe … .
