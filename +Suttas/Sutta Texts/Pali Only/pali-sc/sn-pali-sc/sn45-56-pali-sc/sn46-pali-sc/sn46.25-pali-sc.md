## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 46. Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta: On the Awakening Factors

### III. With Udāyī — SN46.25: Non-decline
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, I will teach you seven principles that guard against decline. Listen …

And what are the seven principles that guard against decline? They are the seven awakening factors. What seven? The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, immersion, and equanimity. These are the seven principles that guard against decline.”

1"Satta vo, bhikkhave, aparihāniye dhamme desessāmi; taṁ suṇātha.

Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta aparihāniyā dhammā? Yadidaṁ – satta bojjhaṅgā. Katame satta? Satisambojjhaṅgo … pe … upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo – ime kho, bhikkhave, satta aparihāniyā dhammā"ti.

Pañcamaṁ.
