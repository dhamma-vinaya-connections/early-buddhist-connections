## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 51. Iddhipādasaṁyutta: On the Bases of Psychic Power

### III. The Iron Ball — SN51.24: Plain Version
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, there are these four bases of psychic power. What four? It’s when a bhikkhu develops the ![[Iddhipadas]].”

1"Cattārome, bhikkhave, iddhipādā. Katame cattāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu chandasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṁ iddhi pāda ṁ bhāveti, vīriyasamādhi … pe … cittasamādhi … vīmaṁsāsamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgataṁ iddhi pāda ṁ bhāveti – ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro iddhipādā"ti.

Catutthaṁ.
