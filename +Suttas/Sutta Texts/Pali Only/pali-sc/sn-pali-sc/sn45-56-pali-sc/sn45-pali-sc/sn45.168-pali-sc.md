## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 45. Maggasaṁyutta: On the Path

### XIII. Searches — SN45.168: Troubles
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, there are these three troubles. What three? Greed, hate, and delusion. These are the three troubles.

The noble eightfold path should be developed for the direct knowledge, complete understanding, finishing, and giving up of these three troubles.”

1"Tayome, bhikkhave, nīghā. Katame tayo? Rāgo nīgho, doso nīgho, moho nīgho – ime kho, bhikkhave, tayo nīghā. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, tiṇṇannaṁ nīghānaṁ abhiññāya pariññāya parikkhayāya pahānāya … pe … ayaṁ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo"ti.

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
