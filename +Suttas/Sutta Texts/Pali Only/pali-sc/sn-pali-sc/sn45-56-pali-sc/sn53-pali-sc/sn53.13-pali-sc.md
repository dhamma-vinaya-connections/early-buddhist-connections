## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 53. Jhānasaṁyutta: On jhānas

### II. Diligence — SN53.13-22: Diligence
#### B. Sujato


1(To be expanded as in the chapter on diligence at SN45.139-148.)

2The Realized One, footprint, roof peak,  
roots, heartwood, jasmine,  
monarch, sun and moon,  
and cloth is the tenth.

1(Appamādavaggo vitthāretabbo.)

2Tathāgataṁ padaṁ kūṭaṁ,  
mūlaṁ sāro ca vassikaṁ;  
Rājā candimasūriyā,  
vatthena dasamaṁ padanti.
