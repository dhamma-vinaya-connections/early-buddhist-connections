## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 48. Indriyasaṁyutta: On the Faculties

### III. The Six Faculties — SN48.22: The Life Faculty
#### B. Sujato


1“bhikkhus, there are these three faculties. What three? The faculties of femininity, masculinity, and life. These are the three faculties.”

1"Tīṇimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni tīṇi? Itthindriyaṁ, purisindriyaṁ, jīvitindriyaṁ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, tīṇi indriyānī"ti.

Dutiyaṁ.
