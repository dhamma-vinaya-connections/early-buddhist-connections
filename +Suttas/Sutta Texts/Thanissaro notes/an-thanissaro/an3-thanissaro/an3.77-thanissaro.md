1. Notice that the Buddha, instead of giving a definition of becoming _(bhava)_ in response to this question, simply notes that becoming occurs on three levels. Nowhere in the suttas does he define the term _becoming,_ but a survey of how he uses the term in different contexts suggests that it means a sense of identity in a particular world of experience: your sense of what you are, focused on a particular desire, in your personal sense of the world as related to that desire. In other words, it is both a psychological and a cosmological concept. For more on this topic, see _The Paradox of Becoming,_ Introduction and Chapter One.
