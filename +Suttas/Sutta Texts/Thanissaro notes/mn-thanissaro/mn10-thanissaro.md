_Satipaá¹­á¹­hÄnaâ€”the establishing_ (upaá¹­á¹­hÄna) _of mindfulness_ (sati)_â€”is a meditative technique for training the mind to keep mindfulness firmly established in a particular frame of reference in all its activities. The role of mindfulness is to keep the mind properly focused in frames of reference that will give it guidance in what present events to develop, and which ones to abandon, so as to keep it on the path. To make an analogy, awakening is like a mountain on the horizon, the destination to which you are driving a car. Mindfulness is what remembers to keep attention focused on the road to the mountain, rather than letting it stay focused on glimpses of the mountain or get distracted by other paths leading away from the road._

_Satipaá¹­á¹­hÄna plays a role in many formulations of the path to awakening. In the noble eightfold path, it is the seventh factor, following on right effort and leading to right concentration.
