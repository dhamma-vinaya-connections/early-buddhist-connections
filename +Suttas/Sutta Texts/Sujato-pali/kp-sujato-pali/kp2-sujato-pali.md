## The Ten Training Rules

  

1I undertake the training rule of refraining from killing living creatures

2I undertake the training rule of refraining from taking what has not been given

3I undertake the training rule of refraining from unchastity

4I undertake the training rule of refraining from false speech

5I undertake the training rule of refraining from liquor, wines, or intoxicants which cause heedlessness.

6I undertake the training rule of refraining from eating at the wrong time

7I undertake the training rule of refraining from dances, songs, music, and watching shows

8I undertake the training rule of refraining from adorning or ornamenting myself by wearing garlands, scents, or ointments

9I undertake the training rule of refraining from lofty or grand beds

10I undertake the training rule of refraining from accepting gold or money
