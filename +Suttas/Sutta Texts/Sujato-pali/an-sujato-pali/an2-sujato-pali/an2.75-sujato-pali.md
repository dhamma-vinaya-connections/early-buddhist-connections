## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 2: The Book of the Twos

### 75
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“There are, bhikkhus, these two kinds of happiness. What two? Happiness that relies on pleasure and happiness that relies on equanimity. These are the two kinds of happiness. The better of these two kinds of happiness is happiness that relies on equanimity.”

1 "Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sātārammaṇañca sukhaṁ upekkhārammaṇañca sukhaṁ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, imesaṁ dvinnaṁ sukhānaṁ yadidaṁ upekkhārammaṇaṁ sukhan"ti.
