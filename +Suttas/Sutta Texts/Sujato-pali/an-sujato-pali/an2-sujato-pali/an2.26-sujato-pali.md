## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 2: The Book of the Twos

### 26
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, when you hide your misdeeds, you can expect one of two destinies: hell or the animal realm.

2When you don’t hide your misdeeds, you can expect one of two destinies: as a god or a human.”

1"Paṭicchannakammantassa, bhikkhave, dvinnaṁ gatīnaṁ aññatarā gati pāṭikaṅkhā – nirayo vā tiracchānayoni vāti.

2Appaṭicchannakammantassa, bhikkhave, dvinnaṁ gatīnaṁ aññatarā gati pāṭikaṅkhā – devā vā manussā vā"ti.
