## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 2: The Book of the Twos

### 64
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“There are, bhikkhus, these two kinds of happiness. What two? The happiness of laypeople, and the happiness of renunciates. These are the two kinds of happiness. The better of these two kinds of happiness is the happiness of renunciates.”

1"Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Gihisukhañca pabbajitasukhañca. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, imesaṁ dvinnaṁ sukhānaṁ yadidaṁ pabbajitasukhan"ti.
