## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 2: The Book of the Twos

### 68
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“There are, bhikkhus, these two kinds of happiness. What two? Material happiness and spiritual happiness. These are the two kinds of happiness. The better of these two kinds of happiness is spiritual happiness.”

1"Dvemāni, bhikkhave, sukhāni. Katamāni dve? Sāmisañca sukhaṁ nirāmisañca sukhaṁ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dve sukhāni. Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, imesaṁ dvinnaṁ sukhānaṁ yadidaṁ nirāmisaṁ sukhan"ti.
