## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 2: The Book of the Twos

### 58
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“These two, bhikkhus, are not startled by a crack of thunder. What two? A bhikkhu who has ended defilements; and a thoroughbred horse. These are the two who are not startled by a crack of thunder.”

1"Dveme, bhikkhave, asaniyā phalantiyā na santasanti. Katame dve? Bhikkhu ca khīṇāsavo, assājānīyo ca. Ime kho, bhikkhave, dve asaniyā phalantiyā na santasantī"ti.
