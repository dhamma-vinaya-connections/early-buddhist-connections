## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 5: The Book of the Fives

### XXI. With Kimbila — AN 5.204: Powers
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these five powers. What five? Faith, conscience, prudence, energy, and wisdom. These are the five powers.”

1"Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhābalaṁ, hiribalaṁ, ottappabalaṁ, vīriyabalaṁ, paññābalaṁ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañca balānī"ti.

Catutthaṁ.
