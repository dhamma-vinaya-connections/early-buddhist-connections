## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 5: The Book of the Fives

### XXV. Bad Conduct — AN 5.246: Bad Bodily Conduct (2nd)
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these five drawbacks in bad bodily conduct … benefits in good bodily conduct …”

1"Pañcime, bhikkhave, ādīnavā kāyaduccarite … pe … ānisaṁsā kāyasucarite … pe … .

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
