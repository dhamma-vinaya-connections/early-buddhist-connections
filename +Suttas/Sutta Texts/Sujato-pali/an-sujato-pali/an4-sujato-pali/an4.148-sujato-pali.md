## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 4: The Book of the Fours

### XV. Brightness — AN 4.148: Bad Conduct
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these four kinds of bad conduct by way of speech. What four? Speech that’s false, divisive, harsh, or nonsensical. These are the four kinds of bad conduct by way of speech.”

1"Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, vacīduccaritāni. Katamāni cattāri? Musāvādo, pisuṇā vācā, pharusā vācā, samphappalāpo – imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri vacīduccaritānī"ti.

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
