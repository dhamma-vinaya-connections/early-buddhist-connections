## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 4: The Book of the Fours

### XVI. Faculties — AN 4.154: The Power of Mindfulness
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these four powers. What four? The powers of mindfulness, immersion, blamelessness, and inclusiveness. These are the four powers.”

1"Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Satibalaṁ, samādhibalaṁ, anavajjabalaṁ, saṅgahabalaṁ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī"ti.

Catutthaṁ.
