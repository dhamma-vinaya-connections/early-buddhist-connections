## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 4: The Book of the Fours

### II. Walking — AN 4.17: Prejudice (1st)
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these four ways of making prejudiced decisions. What four? Making decisions prejudiced by favoritism, hostility, stupidity, and cowardice. These are the four ways of making prejudiced decisions.


2If you act against the teaching  
out of favoritism, hostility, cowardice, or stupidity,  
your fame fades away,  
like the moon in the waning fortnight.”

1"Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, agatigamanāni. Katamāni cattāri? Chandāgatiṁ gacchati, dosāgatiṁ gacchati, mohāgatiṁ gacchati, bhayāgatiṁ gacchati – imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri agatigamanānīti.


2Chandā dosā bhayā mohā,  
yo dhammaṁ ativattati;  
Nihīyati tassa yaso,  
kāḷapakkheva candimā"ti.

Sattamaṁ.
