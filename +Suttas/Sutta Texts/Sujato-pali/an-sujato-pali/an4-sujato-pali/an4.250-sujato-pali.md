## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 4: The Book of the Fours

### XXV. Perils of Offenses — AN 4.250: Expressions (1st)
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these four ignoble expressions. What four?

Saying you’ve seen, heard, thought, or known something, but you haven’t. These are the four ignoble expressions.”

1"Cattārome, bhikkhave, anariyavohārā. Katame cattāro?

Adiṭṭhe diṭṭhavāditā, asute sutavāditā, amute mutavāditā, aviññāte viññātavāditā – ime kho, bhikkhave, cattāro anariyavohārā"ti.

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
