## Aṅguttara Nikāya – The Numerical Discourses

## 1: The Book of the Ones

### XXVII. Ways of Performing Deeds — AN 4.265: Stealing
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, someone with four qualities is cast down to hell. What four? They themselves steal … Someone with four qualities is raised up to heaven. …

They don’t themselves steal …
