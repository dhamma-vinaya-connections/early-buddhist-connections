## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 4: The Book of the Fours

### XVI. Faculties — AN 4.152: The Power of Faith
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these four powers. What four? The powers of faith, energy, mindfulness, and immersion. These are the four powers.”

1"Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Saddhābalaṁ, vīriyabalaṁ, satibalaṁ, samādhibalaṁ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī"ti.

Dutiyaṁ.
