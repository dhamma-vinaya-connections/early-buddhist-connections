## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 4: The Book of the Fours

### XII. With Kesi — AN 4.119: Perils (1st)
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these four perils. What four? The perils of rebirth, old age, sickness, and death.

These are the four perils.”

1"Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, bhayāni. Katamāni cattāri? Jātibhayaṁ, jarābhayaṁ, byādhibhayaṁ, maraṇabhayaṁ – 

imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri bhayānī"ti.

Navamaṁ.
