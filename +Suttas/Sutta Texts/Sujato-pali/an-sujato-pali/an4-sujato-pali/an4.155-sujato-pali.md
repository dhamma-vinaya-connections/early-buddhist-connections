## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 4: The Book of the Fours

### XVI. Faculties — AN 4.155: The Power of Reflection
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these four powers. What four? The powers of reflection, development, blamelessness, and inclusiveness. These are the four powers.”

1"Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, balāni. Katamāni cattāri? Paṭisaṅkhānabalaṁ, bhāvanābalaṁ, anavajjabalaṁ, saṅgahabalaṁ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri balānī"ti.

Pañcamaṁ.
