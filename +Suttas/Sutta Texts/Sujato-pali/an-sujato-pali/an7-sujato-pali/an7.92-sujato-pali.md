## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 7: The Book of the Sevens

### IX. An Ascetic — AN 7.92: A Perfected One
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, it’s by being far from seven things that you become a perfected one. What seven?

Identity view, doubt, misapprehension of precepts and observances, greed, hate, delusion, and conceit. It’s because of being far from these seven things that you become a perfected one.”

1"Ārakattā arahā hoti. Katamesaṁ sattannaṁ?

Sakkāyadiṭṭhi ārakā hoti, vicikicchā ārakā hoti, sīlabbataparāmāso ārako hoti, rāgo ārako hoti, doso ārako hoti, moho ārako hoti, māno ārako hoti. Imesaṁ kho, bhikkhave, sattannaṁ dhammānaṁ ārakattā arahā hotī"ti.

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
