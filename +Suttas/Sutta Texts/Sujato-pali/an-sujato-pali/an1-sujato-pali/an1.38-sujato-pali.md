## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 1: The Book of the Ones

### 1. Obsession of the Mind - 38
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, I do not see a single thing that, when it is restrained, is so very beneficial as the mind. A restrained mind is very beneficial.”

1"Nāhaṁ, bhikkhave, aññaṁ ekadhammampi samanupassāmi yaṁ evaṁ saṁvutaṁ mahato atthāya saṁvattati yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, cittaṁ. Cittaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁvutaṁ mahato atthāya saṁvattatī"ti.

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
