## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 1: The Book of the Ones

### 1. Obsession of the Mind - 235–247
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“The foremost of my nun disciples in seniority is Mahāpajāpatī Gotamī.

2 … with great wisdom is Khemā.

3 … with psychic power is Uppalavaṇṇā.

4 … who have memorized the texts on monastic training is Paṭācārā.

5 … who speak on the teaching is Dhammadinnā.

6 … who practice jhāna is Nandā.

7 … who are energetic is Soṇā.

8 … with clairvoyance is Sakulā.

9 … with swift insight is Bhaddā Kuṇḍalakesā.

10 … who recollect past lives is Bhaddā Kāpilānī.

10 … who have attained great insight is Bhaddakaccānā.

12 … who wear coarse robes is Kisāgotamī.

13 … who are strong in faith is Siṅgālakamātā.”


_v. Fifth Subchapter_

1"Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, mama sāvikānaṁ bhikkhunīnaṁ rattaññūnaṁ yadidaṁ mahāpajāpatigotamī. (1)

2…   Mahāpaññānaṁ yadidaṁ khemā. (2)

3…   Iddhimantīnaṁ yadidaṁ uppalavaṇṇā. (3)

4…   Vinayadharānaṁ yadidaṁ paṭācārā. (4)

5…   Dhammakathikānaṁ yadidaṁ dhammadinnā. (5)

6…   Jhāyīnaṁ yadidaṁ nandā. (6)

7…   Āraddhavīriyānaṁ yadidaṁ soṇā. (7)

8…   Dibbacakkhukānaṁ yadidaṁ bakulā. (8)

9…   Khippābhiññānaṁ yadidaṁ bhaddā kuṇḍalakesā. (9)

10…   Pubbenivāsaṁ anussarantīnaṁ yadidaṁ bhaddā kāpilānī. (10)

11…   Mahābhiññāpattānaṁ yadidaṁ bhaddakaccānā. (11)

12…   Lūkhacīvaradharānaṁ yadidaṁ kisāgotamī. (12)

13…   Saddhādhimuttānaṁ yadidaṁ siṅgālakamātā"ti. (13)


Vaggo pañcamo.

Pañcamavagga
