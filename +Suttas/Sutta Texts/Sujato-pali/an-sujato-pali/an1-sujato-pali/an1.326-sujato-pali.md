## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 1: The Book of the Ones

### 1. Obsession of the Mind - 326
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, in a poorly explained teaching and training a lazy person lives happily. Why is that? Because the teaching is poorly explained.”

1"Durakkhāte, bhikkhave, dhammavinaye yo kusīto so sukhaṁ viharati. Taṁ kissa hetu? Durakkhātattā, bhikkhave, dhammassā"ti.
