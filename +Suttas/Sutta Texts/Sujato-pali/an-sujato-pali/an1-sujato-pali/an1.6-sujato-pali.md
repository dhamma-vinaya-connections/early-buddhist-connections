## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 1: The Book of the Ones

### 1. Obsession of the Mind - 6
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, I do not see a single sight that occupies a woman’s mind like the sight of a man. The sight of a man occupies a woman’s mind.”

1"Nāhaṁ, bhikkhave, aññaṁ ekarūpampi samanupassāmi yaṁ evaṁ itthiyā cittaṁ pariyādāya tiṭṭhati yathayidaṁ, bhikkhave, purisarūpaṁ. Purisarūpaṁ, bhikkhave, itthiyā cittaṁ pariyādāya tiṭṭhatī"ti.

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
