## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 1: The Book of the Ones

### 1. Obsession of the Mind - 198–208
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“The foremost of my bhikkhu disciples in creating a mind-made body is Cūḷapanthaka.

2 … who are skilled in the evolution of consciousness is Cūḷapanthaka.

3 … who are skilled in the evolution of perception is Mahāpanthaka.

4 … who live without conflict is Subhūti.

5 … who are worthy of a religious donation is Subhūti.

6 … who stay in the wilderness is Revata of the Acacia Wood.

7 … who practice jhāna is Kaṅkhārevata.

8 … who are energetic is Soṇa Koḷivisa.

9 … who are good speakers is Soṇa Kuṭikaṇṇa.

10 … who receive many possessions is Sīvali.

11 … who are strong in faith is Vakkalī.”


_ii. Second Subchapter_

1"Etadaggaṁ, bhikkhave, mama sāvakānaṁ bhikkhūnaṁ manomayaṁ kāyaṁ abhinimminantānaṁ yadidaṁ cūḷapanthako. (1)

2…   Cetovivaṭṭakusalānaṁ yadidaṁ cūḷapanthako. (2)

3…   Saññāvivaṭṭakusalānaṁ yadidaṁ mahāpanthako. (3)

4…   Araṇavihārīnaṁ yadidaṁ subhūti. (4)

5…   Dakkhiṇeyyānaṁ yadidaṁ subhūti. (5)

6…   Āraññakānaṁ yadidaṁ revato khadiravaniyo. (6)

7…   Jhāyīnaṁ yadidaṁ kaṅkhārevato. (7)

8…   Āraddhavīriyānaṁ yadidaṁ soṇo koḷiviso. (8)

9…   Kalyāṇavākkaraṇānaṁ yadidaṁ soṇo kuṭikaṇṇo. (9)

10…   Lābhīnaṁ yadidaṁ sīvali. (10)

11…   Saddhādhimuttānaṁ yadidaṁ vakkalī"ti. (11)

Vaggo dutiyo.


Dutiyavagga
