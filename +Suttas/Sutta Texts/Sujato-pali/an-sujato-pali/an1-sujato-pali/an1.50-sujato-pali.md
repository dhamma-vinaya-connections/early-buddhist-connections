## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 1: The Book of the Ones

### 1. Obsession of the Mind - 50
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“This mind, bhikkhus, is radiant. And it is freed from passing corruptions.”

1"Pabhassaramidaṁ, bhikkhave, cittaṁ. Tañca kho āgantukehi upakkilesehi vippamuttan"ti.

Dasamaṁ.

Paṇihitaacchavaggo pañcamo.
