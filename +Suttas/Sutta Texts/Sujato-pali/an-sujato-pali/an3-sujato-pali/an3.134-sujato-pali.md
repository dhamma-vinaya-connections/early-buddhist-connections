## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 3: The Book of the Threes

### XIV. A Warrior — AN 3.134: Assemblies
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are these three assemblies. What three? An assembly educated in fancy talk, an assembly educated in questioning, and an assembly educated to the fullest extent. These are the three assemblies.”

1"Tisso imā, bhikkhave, parisā. Katamā tisso? Ukkācitavinītā parisā, paṭipucchāvinītā parisā, yāvatāvinītā parisā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso parisā"ti.

Dutiyaṁ.
