## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 10: The Book of the Tens

### XIII. Purified — AN 10.126: Fourth
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, these ten things don’t culminate in the removal of greed, hate, and delusion apart from the Holy One’s training. What ten? Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom. These are the ten things that don’t culminate in the removal of greed, hate, and delusion apart from the Holy One’s training.”

1"Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā rāgavinayapariyosānā honti dosavinayapariyosānā honti mohavinayapariyosānā honti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi … pe … sammāvimutti – ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā rāgavinayapariyosānā honti dosavinayapariyosānā honti mohavinayapariyosānā honti, nāññatra sugatavinayā"ti.

Catutthaṁ.
