## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 10: The Book of the Tens

### XIII. Purified — AN 10.133: Eleventh
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, there are ten right ways. What ten? Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom. These are the ten right ways.”

1"Dasayime, bhikkhave, sammattā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi, sammāñāṇaṁ, sammāvimutti – ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa sammattā"ti.

Ekādasamaṁ.

Parisuddhavaggo tatiyo.
