## Aṅguttara Nikāya - The Numerical Discourses

## 10: The Book of the Tens

### XIII. Purified — AN 10.123: First
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, these ten things are not purified and cleansed apart from the Holy One’s training. What ten? Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom. These ten things are not purified and cleansed apart from the Holy One’s training.”

1"Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi, sammāñāṇaṁ, sammāvimutti – ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā, nāññatra sugatavinayā"ti.

Paṭhamaṁ.
