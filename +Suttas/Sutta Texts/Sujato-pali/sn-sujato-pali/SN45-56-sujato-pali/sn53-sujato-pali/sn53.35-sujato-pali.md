## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 53. Jhānasaṁyutta: On jhānas

### IV. Searches — SN53.35-44: Searches
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1(To be expanded as in the chapter on searches at SN45.161-170.)

2Searches, discriminations, defilements,  
states of existence, three kinds of suffering,  
barrenness, stains, and troubles,  
feelings, craving, and thirst.

1(Esanāvaggo vitthāretabbo.)

2Esanā vidhā āsavo,  
Bhavo ca dukkhatā tisso;  
Khilaṁ malañca nīgho ca,  
Vedanā taṇhā tasinā cāti.
