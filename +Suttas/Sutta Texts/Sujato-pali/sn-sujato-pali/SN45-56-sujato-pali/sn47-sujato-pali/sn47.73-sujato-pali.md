## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 47. Satipaṭṭhānasaṁyutta: On Mindfulness Meditation

### VI. Abbreviated Texts on the Ganges — SN47.73-84: Hard Work
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, all the hard work that gets done depends on the earth and is grounded on the earth. …”

To be expanded as in SN45.149-160.


2Hard work, seeds, and dragons,  
a tree, a pot, and a spike,  
the sky, and two on clouds,  
a ship, a guest house, and a river.

1"Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, ye keci balakaraṇīyā kammantā karīyantī"ti vitthāretabbaṁ.

Balakaraṇīyavaggo aṭṭhamo.


2Balaṁ bījañca nāgo ca,  
rukkho kumbhena sūkiyā;  
Ākāsena ca dve meghā,  
nāvā āgantukā nadīti.
