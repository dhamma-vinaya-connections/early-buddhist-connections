## 48. Indriyasaṁyutta: On the Faculties

### I. Plain Version — SN48.1: Plain Version
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1At Sāvatthī.

There the Buddha said:

“bhikkhus, there are these five faculties. What five? The faculties of faith, energy, mindfulness, immersion, and wisdom. These are the five faculties.”

#### Pali

1Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

Tatra kho bhagavā etadavoca.

"Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṁ, vīriyindriyaṁ, satindriyaṁ, samādhindriyaṁ, paññindriyaṁ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī"ti.

Paṭhamaṁ.
