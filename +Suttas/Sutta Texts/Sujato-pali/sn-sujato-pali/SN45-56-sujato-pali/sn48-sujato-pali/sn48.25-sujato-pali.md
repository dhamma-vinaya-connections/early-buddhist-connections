## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 48. Indriyasaṁyutta: On the Faculties

### III. The Six Faculties — SN48.25: Plain Version
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, there are these six faculties. What six? The faculties of the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind. These are the six faculties.”

1"Chayimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni cha? Cakkhundriyaṁ, sotindriyaṁ, ghānindriyaṁ, jivhindriyaṁ, kāyindriyaṁ, manindriyaṁ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, cha indriyānī"ti.

Pañcamaṁ.
