## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 46. Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta: On the Awakening Factors

### XI. Hard Work — SN46.99-110: Hard Work, Etc.
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, all the hard work that gets done depends on the earth and is grounded on the earth. …”

To be expanded as in SN45.149-160.

1"Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, ye keci balakaraṇīyā kammantā karīyan"ti vitthāretabbaṁ.

Balakaraṇīyavaggo ekādasamo.

2Balaṁ bījañca nāgo ca,  
rukkho kumbhena sūkiyā;  
Ākāsena ca dve meghā,  
nāvā āgantukā nadīti.

3Balakaraṇīyavaggo bojjhaṅgasaṁyuttassa bojjhaṅgavasena vitthāretabbo.
