## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 46. Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta: On the Awakening Factors

### XIII. Floods — SN46.121-129: Floods
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, there are these four floods. What four? The floods of sensuality, desire to be reborn, views, and ignorance.”

To be expanded as in SN45.171-179.

1"Cattārome, bhikkhave, oghā. Katame cattāro? Kāmogho, bhavogho, diṭṭhogho, avijjogho"ti vitthāretabbaṁ.
