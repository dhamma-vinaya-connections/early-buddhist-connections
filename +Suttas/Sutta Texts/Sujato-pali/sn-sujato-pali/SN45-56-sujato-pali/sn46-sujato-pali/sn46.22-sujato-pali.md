## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 46. Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta: On the Awakening Factors

### III. With Udāyī — SN46.22: A Teaching on the Awakening Factors
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, I will teach you the seven awakening factors. Listen …

And what are the seven awakening factors? The awakening factors of mindfulness, investigation of principles, energy, rapture, tranquility, immersion, and equanimity. These are the seven awakening factors.”

1"Satta vo, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅge desessāmi; taṁ suṇātha.

Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā? Satisambojjhaṅgo … pe … upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo – ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā"ti.

Dutiyaṁ.
