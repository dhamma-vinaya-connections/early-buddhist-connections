## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 46. Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta: On the Awakening Factors

### VII. Breathing — SN46.66: Breathing
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, when mindfulness of breathing is developed and cultivated it’s very fruitful and beneficial. …”

1"Ānāpānassati, bhikkhave, bhāvitā … pe … dasamaṁ.

Ānāpānavaggo sattamo.

2Aṭṭhikapuḷavakaṁ vinīlakaṁ,  
Vicchiddakaṁ uddhumātena pañcamaṁ;  
Mettā karuṇā muditā,  
Upekkhā ānāpānena te dasāti.
