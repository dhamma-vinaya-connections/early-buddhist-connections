## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 52. Anuruddhasaṁyutta: With Anuruddha

### II. A Thousand — SN52.21: jhānas, Etc.
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“… And it’s because of developing and cultivating these four kinds of mindfulness meditation that I truly understand corruption, cleansing, and emergence regarding the jhānas, liberations, immersions, and attainments.”

1"Imesañca panāhaṁ, āvuso, catunnaṁ satipaṭṭhānānaṁ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā jhanavimokkhasamādhisamāpattīnaṁ saṅkilesaṁ vodānaṁ vuṭṭhānaṁ yathābhūtaṁ pajānāmī"ti.

Ekādasamaṁ.
