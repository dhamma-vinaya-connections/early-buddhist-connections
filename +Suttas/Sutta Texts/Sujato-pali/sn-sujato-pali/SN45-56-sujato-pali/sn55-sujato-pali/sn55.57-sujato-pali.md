## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 55. Sotāpattisaṁyutta: On Stream-Entry

### VI. A Wise Person — SN55.57: The Fruit of Non-Return
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1“bhikkhus, when four things are developed and cultivated they lead to the realization of the fruit of non-return. …”

"

1 … Anāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya … saṁvattantī"ti.

Sattamaṁ.
