## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 55. Sotāpattisaṁyutta: On Stream-Entry

### VI. A Wise Person — SN55.61: The Increase of Wisdom
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, when four things are developed and cultivated they lead to the increase of wisdom. …”

1" … . Paññāvepullāya … saṁvattantī"ti.

Ekādasamaṁ.

Sappaññavaggo chaṭṭho.

2Sagāthakaṁ vassaṁvutthaṁ,  
dhammadinnañca gilānaṁ;  
Caturo phalā paṭilābho,  
vuddhi vepullatāya cāti.
