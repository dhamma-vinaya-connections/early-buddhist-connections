## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 55. Sotāpattisaṁyutta: On Stream-Entry

### VI. A Wise Person — SN55.58: The Fruit of Perfection
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, when four things are developed and cultivated they lead to the realization of the fruit of perfection. …”

1" … Arahattaphalasacchikiriyāya … saṁvattantī"ti.

Aṭṭhamaṁ.
