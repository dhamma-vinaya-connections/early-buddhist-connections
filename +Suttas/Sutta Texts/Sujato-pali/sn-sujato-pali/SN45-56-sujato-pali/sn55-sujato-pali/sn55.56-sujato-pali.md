## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 55. Sotāpattisaṁyutta: On Stream-Entry

### VI. A Wise Person — SN55.56: The Fruit of Once-Return
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, when four things are developed and cultivated they lead to the realization of the fruit of once-return. …”

1"Cattārome, bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā sakadāgāmiphalasacchikiriyāya saṁvattanti. Katame cattāro? … pe … Saṁvattantī"ti.

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
