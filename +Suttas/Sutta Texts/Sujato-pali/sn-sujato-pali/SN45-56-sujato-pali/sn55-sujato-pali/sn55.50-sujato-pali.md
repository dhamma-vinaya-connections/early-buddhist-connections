## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 55. Sotāpattisaṁyutta: On Stream-Entry

### V. Overflowing Merit, With Verses — SN55.50: Factors
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, there are these four factors of stream-entry. What four? Associating with good people, listening to the true teaching, proper attention, and practicing in line with the teaching. These are the four factors of stream-entry.”

1"Cattārimāni, bhikkhave, sotāpattiyaṅgāni. Katamāni cattāri? Sappurisasaṁsevo, saddhammassavanaṁ, yonisomanasikāro, dhammānudhammappaṭipatti – imāni kho, bhikkhave, cattāri sotāpattiyaṅgānī"ti.

Dasamaṁ.  
Sagāthakapuññābhisandavaggo pañcamo.

2Abhisandā tayo vuttā,  
Duve mahaddhanena ca;  
Suddhaṁ nandiyaṁ bhaddiyaṁ,  
Mahānāmaṅgena te dasāti.
