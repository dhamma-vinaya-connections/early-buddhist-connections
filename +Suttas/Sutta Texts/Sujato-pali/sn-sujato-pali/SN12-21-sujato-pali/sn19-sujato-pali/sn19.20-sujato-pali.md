## 19. Lakkhaṇasaṁyutta: With Lakkhaṇa

### II. The Second Chapter — SN19.20: A Bad Novice bhikkhu
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“I saw a novice bhikkhu flying through the air. His outer robe was burning …” …

“He used to be a bad novice bhikkhu …”

1“Addasaṁ sāmaṇeraṁ vehāsaṁ gacchantaṁ. Tassa saṅghāṭipi ādittā …pe…

pāpasāmaṇero ahosi …pe….
