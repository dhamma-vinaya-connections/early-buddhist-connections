## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 15. Anamataggasaṁyutta: On the Unknowable Beginning

### II. The Second Chapter — SN15.15: Father
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1At Sāvatthī.

“bhikkhus, transmigration has no known beginning. … It’s not easy to find a sentient being who in all this long time has not previously been your father. … This is quite enough for you to become disillusioned, dispassionate, and freed regarding all conditions.”

#### Pali

1Sāvatthiyaṁ viharati.

“Anamataggoyaṁ, bhikkhave, saṁsāro … pe … na so, bhikkhave, satto sulabharūpo yo napitābhūtapubbo iminā dīghena addhunā. … alaṁ vimuccitun”ti.
