## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 24. Diṭṭhisaṁyutta: On View

### I. Stream-Entry — SN24.11: The World Is Finite
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1At Sāvatthī.

“bhikkhus, when what exists, because of grasping what and insisting on what, does the view arise: ‘The world is finite’?” …

#### Pali

1Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

"Kismiṁ nu kho, bhikkhave, sati, kiṁ upādāya, kiṁ abhinivissa evaṁ diṭṭhi uppajjati: ‘antavā loko’"ti? "Bhagavaṁmūlakā no, bhante, dhammā … pe … niyato sambodhiparāyano"ti.

Ekādasamaṁ.
