## 22. Khandhasaṁyutta: On the Aggregates

### II. Impermanence — SN22.13: Suffering
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1At Sāvatthī.

“bhikkhus, form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness are suffering.

Seeing this … They understand: ‘… there is no return to any state of existence.’”

#### Pali

1Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

“Rūpaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ, vedanā dukkhā, saññā dukkhā, saṅkhārā dukkhā, viññāṇaṁ dukkhaṁ.

Evaṁ passaṁ … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī”ti.
