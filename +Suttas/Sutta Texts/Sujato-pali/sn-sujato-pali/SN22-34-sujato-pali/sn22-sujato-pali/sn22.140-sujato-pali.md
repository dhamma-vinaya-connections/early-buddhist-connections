## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 22. Khandhasaṁyutta: On the Aggregates

### XIV. Burning Chaff — SN22.140: Suffering
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1At Sāvatthī.

“bhikkhus, you should give up desire for what is suffering. …”

#### Pali

1Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

"Yaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ; tatra vo chando pahātabbo … pe … yaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ; tatra vo chando pahātabbo"ti.

Pañcamaṁ.
