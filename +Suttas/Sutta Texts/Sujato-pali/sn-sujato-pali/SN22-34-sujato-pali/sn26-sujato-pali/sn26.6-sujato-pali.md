## 26. Uppādasaṁyutta: On Arising

### I. Arising — SN26.6: Perception
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1At Sāvatthī.

“bhikkhus, the arising of perception of sights … perception of thoughts … is the manifestation of old age and death.

The cessation of perception of sights … perception of thoughts … is the ending of old age and death.”

#### Pali

1Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

"Yo kho, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya uppādo ṭhiti … pe … jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo … pe … yo dhammasaññāya uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṁ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo.

Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya nirodho … pe … jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo … pe … yo dhammasaññāya nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṁ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo"ti.

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
