## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 27. Kilesasaṁyutta: On Corruptions

### I. Corruptions — SN27.6: Perception
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato. 

1At Sāvatthī.

“bhikkhus, desire and greed for perception of sights, perception of sounds, perception of smells, perception of tastes, perception of touches, or perception of thoughts is a corruption of the mind. …”

#### Pali

1Sāvatthinidānaṁ.

"Yo, bhikkhave, rūpasaññāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yo saddasaññāya … yo gandhasaññāya … yo rasasaññāya … yo phoṭṭhabbasaññāya … yo dhammasaññāya chandarāgo, cittasseso upakkileso. Yato kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno … pe … abhiññā sacchikaraṇīyesu dhammesū"ti.

Chaṭṭhaṁ.
