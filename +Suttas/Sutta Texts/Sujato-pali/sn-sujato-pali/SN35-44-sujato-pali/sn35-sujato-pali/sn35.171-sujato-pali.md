## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.171-173: Desire, Etc. for the Suffering Interior
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

“bhikkhus, you should give up desire … greed … desire and greed for what is suffering. And what is suffering?

The eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are suffering …”

"Yaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ, tatra vo chando pahātabbo, rāgo pahātabbo, chandarāgo pahātabbo. Kiñca, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ?

Cakkhu, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ; tatra vo chando pahātabbo, rāgo pahātabbo, chandarāgo pahātabbo … pe … jivhā dukkhā … pe … mano dukkho; tatra vo chando pahātabbo, rāgo pahātabbo, chandarāgo pahātabbo. Yaṁ, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ tatra vo chando pahātabbo, rāgo pahātabbo, chandarāgo pahātabbo"ti.
