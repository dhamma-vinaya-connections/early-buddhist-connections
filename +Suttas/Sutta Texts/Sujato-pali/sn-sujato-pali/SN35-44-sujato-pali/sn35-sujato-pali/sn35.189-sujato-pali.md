## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 35. Saḷāyatanasaṁyutta: On the Six Sense Fields

### XVII. Sixty Abbreviated Texts — SN35.189-191: The Interior as Suffering in the Three Times
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

“bhikkhus, in the past … future … present the eye, ear, nose, tongue, body, and mind are suffering …”

"Cakkhu, bhikkhave, dukkhaṁ atītaṁ anāgataṁ paccuppannaṁ … pe … jivhā dukkhā atītā anāgatā paccuppannā … pe … mano dukkho atīto anāgato paccuppanno. Evaṁ passaṁ, bhikkhave … pe … nāparaṁ itthattāyāti pajānātī"ti.
