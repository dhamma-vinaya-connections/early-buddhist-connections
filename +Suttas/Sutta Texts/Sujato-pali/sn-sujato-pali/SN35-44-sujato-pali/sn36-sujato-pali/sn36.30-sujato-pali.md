## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 36. Vedanāsaṁyutta: On Feelings

### III. The Explanation of the Hundred and Eight — SN36.30: Plain Version
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“bhikkhus, there are these three feelings. What three? Pleasant, painful, and neutral feelings. These are the three feelings.”

1"Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanā"ti.

Dasamaṁ.
