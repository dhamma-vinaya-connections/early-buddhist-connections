## Saṁyutta Nikāya — The Linked Discourses

## 43. Asaṅkhatasaṁyutta: On the Unconditioned

### I. The First Chapter — SN43.3: thinking and considering
#### B. Sujato


- © Translated from the Pali by Bhante Sujato.  

1“And what is the path that leads to the unconditioned? Immersion with thinking and considering. Immersion without placing the mind, but just keeping it connected. Immersion without placing the mind or keeping it connected. …”

1"Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Savitakkasavicāro samādhi, avitakkavicāramatto samādhi, avitakkaavicāro samādhi – ayaṁ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo … pe … .

Tatiyaṁ.
