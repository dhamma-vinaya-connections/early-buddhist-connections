## Chapter 11: The Non-Returner

6. Heart (2)
1. Thus I have heard:<a id="fnref1"></a>[^1] One time, the Buddha was staying at Anāthapiṇḍada’s Park in Jeta’s Grove of Śrāvastī.
2. It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the bhikkhus, “From the start, I do not see in this assembly a single thing that’s easily controlled and which brings a good result when it’s easily controlled. That thing is the heart.
3. “[Therefore,] bhikkhus, one should discern their heart and be well aware of its good roots. Thus, bhikkhus, you should train yourselves.”
4. When the bhikkhus heard what the Buddha taught, they rejoiced and approved.

---

### Notes

[^1]: For the source text, cf. [T125.2.566c16-21]. This and the previous _sūtra_ are similar to those found in [AN 1.21-30].
