### Translator: Charles Patton

## [2. The Sense Fields](index.html)

80 (207). Truly Knowing (2)
1. Thus I have heard:<a id="fnref1"></a>[^1] One time, the Buddha was staying in Doctor Jīvaka Kumāra’s Mango Park of Vaiśālī.
2. It was then that the Bhagavān addressed the bhikkhus, “You should cultivate a measureless _samādhi_ and earnestly tie your thoughts to it. Why is that? Having cultivated a measureless _samādhi_ and earnestly tied one’s thoughts to it, then things appear as they really are. What truly appears? The eye truly appears … _and so on as in [previous sūtra](SA2_79.html#p2)_ … These things truly appear as impermanent and created.”
3. After the Buddha spoke this _sūtra_, the bhikkhus who heard what the Buddha taught rejoiced and approved.
