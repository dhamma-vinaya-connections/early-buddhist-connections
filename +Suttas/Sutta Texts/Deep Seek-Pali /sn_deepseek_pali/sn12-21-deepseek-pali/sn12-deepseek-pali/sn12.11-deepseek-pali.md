"Monks, what is the source of these four nutriments, what is their origin, what is their birth, what is their arising? These four nutriments have craving as their source, craving as their origin, they are born from craving, they arise from craving. And what is the source of this craving, monks, what is its origin, what is its birth, what is its arising? Craving has feeling as its source, feeling as its origin, it is born from feeling, it arises from feeling. And what is the source of this feeling, monks, what is its origin, what is its birth, what is its arising?
