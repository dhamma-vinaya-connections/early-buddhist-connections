110.At Savatthi. "Monks, there are these five aggregates of clinging. Which five? They are: the form aggregate of clinging... the consciousness aggregate of clinging. When, monks, a monk has truly understood the arising, the passing away, the gratification, the danger, and the escape in regard to these five aggregates of clinging, and is liberated through non-clinging, then, monks, that monk is called an arahant, one whose taints are destroyed, who has lived the life, done what had to be done, laid down the burden, attained the true goal, destroyed the fetters of being, and is liberated by right knowledge."
