669.At Savatthi. "Monks, there are these four meditators. Which four? Here, monks, a certain meditator is skilled in concentration, but not assiduous in concentration. Of these four meditators, monks, the meditator who is both skilled in concentration and assiduous in concentration is the foremost, the best, the chief, the highest, and the most excellent.
