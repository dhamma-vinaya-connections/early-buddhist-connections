602.At Savatthi. A certain bhikkhu, seated to one side, asked the Blessed One: "Bhante, what is the cause, what is the reason, why sometimes it is cold?" "There are, bhikkhu, devas called cold-cloud devas. When it occurs to them: 'Oh, that we might indulge in our own pleasure,' then, in accordance with their mental resolve, it becomes cold. This, bhikkhu, is the cause, this is the reason, why sometimes it is cold." This is the fifty-third.
