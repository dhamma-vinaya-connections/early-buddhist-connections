604.At Savatthi. A certain bhikkhu, seated to one side, asked the Blessed One: "Bhante, what is the cause, what is the condition, why sometimes there are clouds?" "There are, bhikkhu, devas called cloud devas. When it occurs to them: 'Oh, that we might indulge in our own pleasure,' then, in accordance with their mental resolve, there are clouds. This, bhikkhu, is the cause, this is the condition, why sometimes there are clouds." This is the fifty-fifth.
