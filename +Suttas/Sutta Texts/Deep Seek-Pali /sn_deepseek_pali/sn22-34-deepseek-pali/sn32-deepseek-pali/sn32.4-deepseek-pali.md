603.At Savatthi. A certain bhikkhu, seated to one side, asked the Blessed One: "Bhante, what is the cause, what is the reason, why sometimes it gets hot?" "There are, bhikkhu, devas called hot-cloud devas. When it occurs to them: 'Oh, that we might indulge in our own pleasure,' then, in accordance with their mental resolve, it gets hot. This, bhikkhu, is the cause, this is the reason, why sometimes it gets hot." This is the fifty-fourth.
