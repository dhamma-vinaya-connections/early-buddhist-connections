215."Monks, these seven factors of enlightenment, being unhindered, unimpeded, and not corruptions of the mind, when developed and cultivated, conduce to the realization of the fruit of true knowledge and liberation. What seven? The factor of enlightenment of mindfulness, monks, being unhindered, unimpeded, and not a corruption of the mind, when developed and cultivated, conduces to the realization of the fruit of true knowledge and liberation... The factor of enlightenment of equanimity, monks, being unhindered, unimpeded, and not a corruption of the mind, when developed and cultivated, conduces to the realization of the fruit of true knowledge and liberation. These, monks, are the seven factors of enlightenment, being unhindered, unimpeded, and not corruptions of the mind, when developed and cultivated, conduce to the realization of the fruit of true knowledge and liberation."
