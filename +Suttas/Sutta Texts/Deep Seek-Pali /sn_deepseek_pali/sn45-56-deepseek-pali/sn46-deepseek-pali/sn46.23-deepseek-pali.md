# SN 46.23 Ṭhāniyasuttaṃ

## SN 46.23 The Grounds Discourse

204. ‘‘Kāmarāgaṭṭhāniyānaṃ, [kāmarāgaṭṭhānīyānaṃ (sī.)] bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ manasikārabahulīkārā anuppanno ceva kāmacchando uppajjati, uppanno ca kāmacchando bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattati. Byāpādaṭṭhāniyānaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ manasikārabahulīkārā anuppanno ceva byāpādo uppajjati, uppanno ca byāpādo bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattati. Thinamiddhaṭṭhāniyānaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ manasikārabahulīkārā anuppannañceva thinamiddhaṃ uppajjati, uppannañca thinamiddhaṃ bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāyaṃ saṃvattati. Uddhaccakukkuccaṭṭhāniyānaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ manasikārabahulīkārā anuppannañceva uddhaccakukkuccaṃ uppajjati, uppannañca uddhaccakukkuccaṃ bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattati. Vicikicchāṭṭhāniyānaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ manasikārabahulīkārā anuppannā ceva vicikicchā uppajjati, uppannā ca vicikicchā bhiyyobhāvāya vepullāya saṃvattati.

204.“Monks, by frequently attending to things that are grounds for sensual passion, unarisen sensual passion arises, and arisen sensual passion increases and expands. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for ill will, unarisen ill will arises, and arisen ill will increases and expands. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for sloth and torpor, unarisen sloth and torpor arise, and arisen sloth and torpor increases and expands. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for restlessness and remorse, unarisen restlessness and remorse arise, and arisen restlessness and remorse increases and expands. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for doubt, unarisen doubt arises, and arisen doubt increases and expands.”

‘‘Satisambojjhaṅgaṭṭhāniyānaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ manasikārabahulīkārā anuppanno ceva satisambojjhaṅgo uppajjati, uppanno ca satisambojjhaṅgo bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gacchati…pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgaṭṭhāniyānaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ manasikārabahulīkārā anuppanno ceva upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo uppajjati, uppanno ca upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo bhāvanāpāripūriṃ gacchatī’’ti. Tatiyaṃ.

“Monks, by frequently attending to things that are grounds for the enlightenment factor of mindfulness, the unarisen enlightenment factor of mindfulness arises, and the arisen enlightenment factor of mindfulness is brought to fulfillment by development. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for the enlightenment factor of investigation of states, the unarisen enlightenment factor of investigation of states arises, and the arisen enlightenment factor of investigation of states is brought to fulfillment by development. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for the enlightenment factor of energy, the unarisen enlightenment factor of energy arises, and the arisen enlightenment factor of energy is brought to fulfillment by development. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for the enlightenment factor of rapture, the unarisen enlightenment factor of rapture arises, and the arisen enlightenment factor of rapture is brought to fulfillment by development. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for the enlightenment factor of tranquility, the unarisen enlightenment factor of tranquility arises, and the arisen enlightenment factor of tranquility is brought to fulfillment by development. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for the enlightenment factor of samādhi, the unarisen enlightenment factor of samādhi arises, and the arisen enlightenment factor of samādhi is brought to fulfillment by development. By frequently attending to things that are grounds for the enlightenment factor of equanimity, the unarisen enlightenment factor of equanimity arises, and the arisen enlightenment factor of equanimity is brought to fulfillment by development.” The third.
