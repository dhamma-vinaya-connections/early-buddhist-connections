# SN 46.22 Bojjhaṅgadesanāsuttaṃ

## SN 46.22 The Discourse on the Factors of Enlightenment

203. ‘‘Satta vo, bhikkhave, bojjhaṅge desessāmi; taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā? Satisambojjhaṅgo…pe… upekkhāsambojjhaṅgo – ime kho, bhikkhave, satta bojjhaṅgā’’ti. Dutiyaṃ.

203.“Monks, I will teach you the seven factors of enlightenment; listen to that. And what, monks, are the seven factors of enlightenment? The enlightenment factor of mindfulness…the enlightenment factor of investigation of states…the enlightenment factor of energy…the enlightenment factor of rapture…the enlightenment factor of tranquility…the enlightenment factor of samādhi…the enlightenment factor of equanimity. These, monks, are the seven factors of enlightenment.” The second.
