# SN 47.23 Parihānasuttaṃ

## SN 47.23 The Decline Discourse

389. Ekaṃ samayaṃ āyasmā ca ānando āyasmā ca bhaddo pāṭaliputte viharanti kukkuṭārāme. Atha kho āyasmā bhaddo sāyanhasamayaṃ paṭisallānā vuṭṭhito yenāyasmā ānando tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā āyasmatā ānandena saddhiṃ sammodi. Sammodanīyaṃ kathaṃ sāraṇīyaṃ vītisāretvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. Ekamantaṃ nisinno kho āyasmā bhaddo āyasmantaṃ ānandaṃ etadavoca – ‘‘ko nu kho, āvuso ānanda, hetu, ko paccayo yena saddhammaparihānaṃ hoti? Ko nu kho, āvuso ānanda, hetu, ko paccayo yena saddhammaaparihānaṃ hotī’’ti?

389.On one occasion Venerable Ānanda and Venerable Bhadda were dwelling at Pāṭaliputta in the Cock's Monastery. Then, in the evening, Venerable Bhadda arose from seclusion and approached Venerable Ānanda. Having approached, he exchanged greetings with Venerable Ānanda, and when they had concluded their greetings and cordial talk, he sat down to one side. Seated to one side, Venerable Bhadda said to Venerable Ānanda: "What, friend Ānanda, is the cause, what is the reason whereby there is decline of the good Dhamma? And what, friend Ānanda, is the cause, what is the reason whereby there is no decline of the good Dhamma?"

‘‘Sādhu sādhu, āvuso bhadda! Bhaddako kho te, āvuso bhadda, ummaṅgo, bhaddakaṃ paṭibhānaṃ, kalyāṇī paripucchā. Evañhi tvaṃ, āvuso bhadda, pucchasi – ‘ko nu kho, āvuso ānanda, hetu, ko paccayo yena saddhammaparihānaṃ hoti? Ko panāvuso ānanda, hetu, ko paccayo yena saddhammaaparihānaṃ hotī’’’ti? ‘‘Evamāvuso’’ti. ‘‘Catunnaṃ kho, āvuso, satipaṭṭhānānaṃ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā saddhammaparihānaṃ hoti. Catunnañca kho, āvuso, satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā saddhammaaparihānaṃ hoti’’.

"Good, good, friend Bhadda! Excellent is your discernment, friend Bhadda, excellent is your intelligence, excellent is your questioning. For you ask thus: 'What, friend Ānanda, is the cause, what is the condition by which there is decline of the good Dhamma? And what, friend Ānanda, is the cause, what is the condition by which there is no decline of the good Dhamma?'""Yes, friend.""It is, friend, because the four foundations of mindfulness have not been developed and not been cultivated that there is decline of the good Dhamma. And it is, friend, because the four foundations of mindfulness have been developed and cultivated that there is no decline of the good Dhamma."

‘‘Katamesaṃ catunnaṃ? Idhāvuso, bhikkhu kāye kāyānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṃ; vedanāsu…pe… citte…pe… dhammesu dhammānupassī viharati ātāpī sampajāno satimā, vineyya loke abhijjhādomanassaṃ. Imesaṃ kho, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ abhāvitattā abahulīkatattā saddhammaparihānaṃ hoti. Imesañca kho, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā saddhammaaparihānaṃ hotī’’ti. Tatiyaṃ.

"Which four? Here, friends, a bhikkhu ![[Satipatthanas]]. He ![[Satipatthanas]]. It is, friends, because these four foundations of mindfulness have not been developed and not been cultivated that the true Dhamma declines. And it is, friends, because these four foundations of mindfulness have been developed and cultivated that the true Dhamma does not decline." The third.
