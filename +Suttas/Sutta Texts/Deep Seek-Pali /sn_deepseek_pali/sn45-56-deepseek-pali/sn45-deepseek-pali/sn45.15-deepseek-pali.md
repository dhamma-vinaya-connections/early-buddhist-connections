# SN 45.15 Dutiyauppādasuttaṃ

## SN 45.15 The Second Origin Sutta

15. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Aṭṭhime, bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame aṭṭha? Seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Ime kho, bhikkhave, aṭṭha dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā anuppannā uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā’’ti. Pañcamaṃ.

15. At Sāvatthī. "Bhikkhus, these eight qualities, when developed and cultivated, arise, if unarisen, nowhere else but in the Discipline of the Fortunate One. Which eight? Namely, right view...right samādhi. These, bhikkhus, are the eight qualities that, when developed and cultivated, arise, if unarisen, nowhere else but in the Discipline of the Fortunate One." The fifth.
