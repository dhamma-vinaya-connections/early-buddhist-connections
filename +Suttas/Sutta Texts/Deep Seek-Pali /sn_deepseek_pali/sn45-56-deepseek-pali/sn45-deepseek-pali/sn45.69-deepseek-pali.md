152."Monks, suppose there were a tree leaning eastward, sloping eastward, inclining eastward. If it were cut at the root, in which direction would it fall?" "In the direction it leans, slopes, and inclines, Venerable Sir." "Just so, monks, a monk who develops the Noble Eightfold Path, who cultivates the Noble Eightfold Path, inclines toward NibbÄna, slopes toward NibbÄna, tends toward NibbÄna.
