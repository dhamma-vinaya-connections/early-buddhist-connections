83."Monks, this is the accomplishment of wise attention. For a monk accomplished in wise attention, monks, this is to be expected: he will develop the Noble Eightfold Path, he will cultivate the Noble Eightfold Path. And how, monks, does a monk accomplished in wise attention develop the Noble Eightfold Path, cultivate the Noble Eightfold Path? Here, monks, a monk develops right view, which is based on seclusion...he develops right concentration, which is based on seclusion, dispassion, cessation, culminating in relinquishment. It is in this way, monks, that a monk accomplished in wise attention develops the Noble Eightfold Path, cultivates the Noble Eightfold Path." Seventh.
