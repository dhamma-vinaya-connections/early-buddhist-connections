# SN 45.52 Yonisomanasikārasampadāsuttaṃ

## SN 45.52 The Discourse on the Accomplishment of Wise Attention.

83. ‘‘Yathayidaṃ, bhikkhave, yonisomanasikārasampadā. Yonisomanasikārasampannassetaṃ, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno pāṭikaṅkhaṃ – ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvessati, ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkarissati. Kathañca, bhikkhave, bhikkhu yonisomanasikārasampanno ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāveti, ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaroti? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu yonisomanasikārasampanno ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāveti, ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkarotī’’ti. Sattamaṃ.

83."Monks, this is the accomplishment of wise attention. For a monk accomplished in wise attention, monks, this is to be expected: he will develop the Noble Eightfold Path, he will cultivate the Noble Eightfold Path. And how, monks, does a monk accomplished in wise attention develop the Noble Eightfold Path, cultivate the Noble Eightfold Path? Here, monks, a monk develops right view, which is based on seclusion...he develops right samādhi, which is based on seclusion, dispassion, cessation, culminating in relinquishment. It is in this way, monks, that a monk accomplished in wise attention develops the Noble Eightfold Path, cultivates the Noble Eightfold Path." Seventh.
