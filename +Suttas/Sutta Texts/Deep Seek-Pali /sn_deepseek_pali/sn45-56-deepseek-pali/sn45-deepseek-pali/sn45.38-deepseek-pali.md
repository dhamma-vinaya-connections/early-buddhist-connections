# SN 45.38 Dutiyabrahmaññasuttaṃ

## SN 45.38 The Second Discourse on Brahminhood

38. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Brahmaññañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmaññatthañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṃ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṃ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, brahmaññattho? Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo – ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmaññattho’’ti. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

38.At Sāvatthī. “Monks, I will teach you Brahminhood and the goal of Brahminhood. Listen carefully. And what, monks, is Brahminhood? It is just this Noble Eightfold Path, namely: right view … right samādhi. This, monks, is called Brahminhood. And what, monks, is the goal of Brahminhood? It is the destruction of lust, the destruction of hatred, the destruction of delusion. This, monks, is called the goal of Brahminhood.” The Eighth.
