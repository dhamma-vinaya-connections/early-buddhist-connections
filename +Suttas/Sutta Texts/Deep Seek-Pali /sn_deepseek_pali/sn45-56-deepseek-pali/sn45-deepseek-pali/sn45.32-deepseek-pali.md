# SN 45.32 Dutiyapaṭipattisuttaṃ

## SN 45.32 The Discourse on the Second Practice

32. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Micchāpaṭipannañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipannañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipanno? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco micchādiṭṭhiko hoti…pe… micchāsamādhi – ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipanno. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipanno? Idha, bhikkhave, ekacco sammādiṭṭhiko hoti…pe… sammāsamādhi – ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipanno’’ti. Dutiyaṃ.

32.At Sāvatthī. "Monks, I will teach you one who practices wrongly and one who practices rightly. Listen to that. And who, monks, is one who practices wrongly? Here, monks, a certain person has wrong view… wrong samādhi. This is called, monks, one who practices wrongly. And who, monks, is one who practices rightly? Here, monks, a certain person has right view… right samādhi. This is called, monks, one who practices rightly." The second.
