176."Monks, there are these seven latent tendencies. What seven? The latent tendency to sensual lust, the latent tendency to aversion, the latent tendency to views, the latent tendency to doubt, the latent tendency to conceit, the latent tendency to desire for existence, and the latent tendency to ignorance. These, monks, are the seven latent tendencies. For the direct knowledge, complete understanding, utter destruction, and abandoning of these seven latent tendencies... this Noble Eightfold Path is to be cultivated." The fifth.
