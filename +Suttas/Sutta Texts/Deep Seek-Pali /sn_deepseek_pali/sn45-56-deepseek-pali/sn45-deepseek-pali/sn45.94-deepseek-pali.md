177."Monks, there are these five strands of sensuality. What five? Forms cognizable by the eye that are desirable, lovely, agreeable, pleasing, likeable, connected with sensuality, and arousing lust; sounds cognizable by the ear... odors cognizable by the nose... flavors cognizable by the tongue... tangibles cognizable by the body that are desirable, lovely, agreeable, pleasing, likeable, connected with sensuality, and arousing lust. These, monks, are the five strands of sensuality. For the direct knowledge, full understanding, complete destruction, and abandonment of these five strands of sensuality... this Noble Eightfold Path is to be cultivated." The sixth.
