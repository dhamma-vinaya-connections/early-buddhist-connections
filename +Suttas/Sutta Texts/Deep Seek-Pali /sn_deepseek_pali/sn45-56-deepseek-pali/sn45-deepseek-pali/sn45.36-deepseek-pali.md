# SN 45.36 Dutiyasāmaññasuttaṃ

## SN 45.36 The Second Discourse on Recluseship

36. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Sāmaññañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sāmaññatthañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca kho, bhikkhave, sāmaññaṃ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāmaññaṃ. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, sāmaññattho? Yo kho, bhikkhave, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo – ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sāmaññattho’’ti. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

36.At Sāvatthī. “Monks, I will teach you recluseship and the goal of recluseship. Listen carefully. And what, monks, is recluseship? It is just this ![[Noble Path]]. This, monks, is called recluseship. And what, monks, is the goal of recluseship? It is the destruction of lust, the destruction of hatred, the destruction of delusion. This, monks, is called the goal of recluseship.” The Sixth.
