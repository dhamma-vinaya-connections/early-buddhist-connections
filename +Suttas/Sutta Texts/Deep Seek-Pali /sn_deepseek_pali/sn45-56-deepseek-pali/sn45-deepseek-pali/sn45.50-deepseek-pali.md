# SN 45.50 Yonisomanasikārasampadāsuttaṃ

## SN 45.50 The Discourse on the Accomplishment of Wise Attention.

76. ‘‘Yadidaṃ – yonisomanasikārasampadā. Yonisomanasikārasampannassetaṃ, bhikkhave, bhikkhuno pāṭikaṅkhaṃ – ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvessati, ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkarissati. Kathañca, bhikkhave, bhikkhu yonisomanasikārasampanno ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāveti, ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaroti? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti rāgavinayapariyosānaṃ dosavinayapariyosānaṃ mohavinayapariyosānaṃ. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu yonisomanasikārasampanno ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāveti, ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkarotī’’ti. Sattamaṃ.

76.“That is accomplishment of wise attention. For a monk endowed with accomplishment of wise attention, bhikkhus, it is to be expected that he will develop the ![[Noble Path]] culminating in the removal of lust, the removal of hatred, and the removal of delusion. Thus, bhikkhus, a monk endowed with accomplishment of wise attention develops the Noble Eightfold Path, cultivates the Noble Eightfold Path.” The seventh.

Ekadhammapeyyālavaggo sattamo.

The Seventh Chapter of the Single-Quality Repetition Series.

Tassuddānaṃ –

Its summary:

Kalyāṇamittaṃ sīlañca, chando ca attasampadā;Diṭṭhi ca appamādo ca, yoniso bhavati sattamaṃ.

Good friendship, virtue, zeal, and accomplishment of self-possession; view and heedfulness, and wise attention is the seventh.

8. Dutiyaekadhammapeyyālavaggo
