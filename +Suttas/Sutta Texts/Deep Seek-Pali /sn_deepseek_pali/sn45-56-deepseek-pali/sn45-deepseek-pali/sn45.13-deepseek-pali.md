# SN 45.13 Sekkhasuttaṃ

## SN 45.13 The Discourse on the Adept

13. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami…pe… ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca – ‘‘‘sekkho, sekkho’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kittāvatā nu kho, bhante, sekkho hotī’’ti?

13. At Savatthi. Then a certain bhikkhu approached the Blessed One … and sitting to one side, that bhikkhu said to the Blessed One: "‘Trainee, trainee,’ venerable sir, it is said. To what extent, venerable sir, is one a trainee?"

‘‘Idha, bhikkhu, sekkhāya sammādiṭṭhiyā samannāgato hoti…pe… sekkhena sammāsamādhinā samannāgato hoti. Ettāvatā kho, bhikkhu, sekkho hotī’’ti. Tatiyaṃ.

Here, a monk is endowed with a trainee's right view...pe... is endowed with a trainee's right samādhi. To this extent, monk, one is a trainee." Third.
