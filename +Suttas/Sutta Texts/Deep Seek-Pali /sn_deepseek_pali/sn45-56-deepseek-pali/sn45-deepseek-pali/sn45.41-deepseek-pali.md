# SN 45.41 Rāgavirāgasuttaṃ

## SN 45.41 The Discourse on Dispassion.

41. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Sace vo, bhikkhave, aññatitthiyā paribbājakā evaṃ puccheyyuṃ – ‘kimatthiyaṃ, āvuso, samaṇe gotame brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti, evaṃ puṭṭhā tumhe, bhikkhave, tesaṃ aññatitthiyānaṃ paribbājakānaṃ evaṃ byākareyyātha – ‘rāgavirāgatthaṃ kho, āvuso, bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti. Sace pana vo, bhikkhave, aññatitthiyā paribbājakā evaṃ puccheyyuṃ – ‘atthi panāvuso, maggo, atthi paṭipadā rāgavirāgāyā’ti, evaṃ puṭṭhā tumhe, bhikkhave, tesaṃ aññatitthiyānaṃ paribbājakānaṃ evaṃ byākareyyātha – ‘atthi kho, āvuso, maggo, atthi paṭipadā rāgavirāgāyā’ti. Katamo ca, bhikkhave, maggo, katamā ca paṭipadā rāgavirāgāya? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ, bhikkhave, maggo, ayaṃ paṭipadā rāgavirāgāyāti. Evaṃ puṭṭhā tumhe, bhikkhave, tesaṃ aññatitthiyānaṃ paribbājakānaṃ evaṃ byākareyyāthā’’ti. Paṭhamaṃ.

41.At Sāvatthī. “Monks, if wanderers of other sects should ask you: ‘For what purpose, friends, is the holy life lived under the ascetic Gotama?’—being asked thus, monks, you should answer those wanderers of other sects thus: ‘For dispassion, friends, is the holy life lived under the Blessed One.’ If, monks, those wanderers of other sects should ask you: ‘But is there, friends, a path, is there a practice for dispassion?’—being asked thus, monks, you should answer those wanderers of other sects thus: ‘There is, friends, a path, there is a practice for dispassion.’ And what, monks, is the path, and what is the practice for dispassion? It is this Noble Eightfold Path, namely: right view … right samādhi. This, monks, is the path, this is the practice for dispassion. Being asked thus, monks, you should answer those wanderers of other sects in this way.” The First.

## 2-7. Saṃyojanappahānādisuttachakkaṃ

### 2–7. The Six Discourses on the Abandoning of Fetters, and so forth

42-47. ‘‘Sace vo, bhikkhave, aññatitthiyā paribbājakā evaṃ puccheyyuṃ – ‘kimatthiyaṃ, āvuso, samaṇe gotame brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti, evaṃ puṭṭhā tumhe, bhikkhave, tesaṃ aññatitthiyānaṃ paribbājakānaṃ evaṃ byākareyyātha – ‘saṃyojanappahānatthaṃ kho, āvuso, bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti…pe… ‘anusayasamugghātanatthaṃ kho, āvuso, bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti…pe… ‘addhānapariññatthaṃ kho, āvuso, bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti…pe… ‘āsavānaṃ khayatthaṃ kho, āvuso, bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti…pe… ‘vijjāvimuttiphalasacchikiriyatthaṃ kho, āvuso, bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti…pe… ‘ñāṇadassanatthaṃ kho, āvuso, bhagavati brahmacariyaṃ vussatī’ti…pe…. Sattamaṃ.

42–47. “Monks, if wanderers of other sects were to ask you: ‘For what purpose, friends, is the holy life lived under the ascetic Gotama?’—being asked thus, monks, you should answer those wanderers of other sects thus: ‘For the abandonment of fetters, friends, is the holy life lived under the Blessed One.’… ‘For the uprooting of underlying tendencies, friends, is the holy life lived under the Blessed One.’… ‘For the full understanding of the long course, friends, is the holy life lived under the Blessed One.’… ‘For the destruction of the taints, friends, is the holy life lived under the Blessed One.’… ‘For the realization of the fruit of knowledge and liberation, friends, is the holy life lived under the Blessed One.’… ‘For knowledge and vision, friends, is the holy life lived under the Blessed One.’… The seventh.
