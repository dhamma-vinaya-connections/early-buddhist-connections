# SN 45.37 Paṭhamabrahmaññasuttaṃ

## SN 45.37 The First Discourse on Brahminhood

37. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Brahmaññañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, brahmaññaphalāni ca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamañca kho, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṃ? Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Idaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, brahmaññaṃ. Katamāni ca, bhikkhave, brahmaññaphalāni? Sotāpattiphalaṃ, sakadāgāmiphalaṃ, anāgāmiphalaṃ, arahattaphalaṃ – imāni vuccanti, bhikkhave, brahmaññaphalānī’’ti. Sattamaṃ.

37.At Sāvatthī. “Monks, I will teach you Brahminhood and the fruits of Brahminhood. Listen carefully. And what, monks, is Brahminhood? It is just this Noble Eightfold Path, namely: right view … right samādhi. This, monks, is called Brahminhood. And what, monks, are the fruits of Brahminhood? The fruit of stream-entry, the fruit of once-returning, the fruit of non-returning, the fruit of arahantship—these, monks, are called the fruits of Brahminhood.” The Seventh.
