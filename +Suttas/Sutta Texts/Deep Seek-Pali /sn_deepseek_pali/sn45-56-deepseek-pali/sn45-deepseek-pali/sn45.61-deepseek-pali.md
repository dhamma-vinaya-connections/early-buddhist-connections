# SN 45.61 Paṭhamasamuddaninnasuttaṃ

## SN 45.61 The First Discourse on Inclining to the Ocean.

121. ‘‘Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, gaṅgā nadī samuddaninnā samuddapoṇā samuddapabbhārā; evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvento ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaronto nibbānaninno hoti nibbānapoṇo nibbānapabbhāro. Kathañca, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvento ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaronto nibbānaninno hoti nibbānapoṇo nibbānapabbhāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti amatogadhaṃ amataparāyanaṃ amatapariyosānaṃ…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti amatogadhaṃ amataparāyanaṃ amatapariyosānaṃ. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvento ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaronto nibbānaninno hoti nibbānapoṇo nibbānapabbhāro’’ti. Paṭhamaṃ.

121."Just as, bhikkhus, the River Gaṅgā inclines to the ocean, slopes to the ocean, tends to the ocean; so too, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu developing and cultivating the Noble Eightfold Path inclines towards Nibbāna, slopes towards Nibbāna, tends towards Nibbāna. And how, bhikkhus, does a bhikkhu developing and cultivating the Noble Eightfold Path incline towards Nibbāna, slope towards Nibbāna, tend towards Nibbāna? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu develops right view, which is immersed in the Deathless, leading to the Deathless, culminating in the Deathless… he develops right samādhi, which is immersed in the Deathless, leading to the Deathless, culminating in the Deathless. Thus, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu developing and cultivating the Noble Eightfold Path inclines towards Nibbāna, slopes towards Nibbāna, tends towards Nibbāna." (First).

## 2-6. Dutiyādisamuddaninnasuttapañcakaṃ

### 2–6. The Group of Five Discourses Beginning with the Second, on Inclining to the Ocean.

122-126. ‘‘Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, yamunā nadī samuddaninnā samuddapoṇā samuddapabbhārā; evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu…pe… seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, aciravatī nadī samuddaninnā samuddapoṇā samuddapabbhārā; evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu…pe… seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, sarabhū nadī samuddaninnā samuddapoṇā samuddapabbhārā; evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu…pe… seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, mahī nadī samuddaninnā samuddapoṇā samuddapabbhārā; evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu…pe… seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, yā kācimā mahānadiyo, seyyathidaṃ – gaṅgā, yamunā, aciravatī, sarabhū, mahī, sabbā tā samuddaninnā samuddapoṇā samuddapabbhārā; evameva kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvento ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaronto nibbānaninno hoti nibbānapoṇo nibbānapabbhāro. Kathañca, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvento ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaronto nibbānaninno hoti nibbānapoṇo nibbānapabbhāro? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti amatogadhaṃ amataparāyanaṃ amatapariyosānaṃ…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti amatogadhaṃ amataparāyanaṃ amatapariyosānaṃ. Evaṃ kho, bhikkhave, bhikkhu ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bhāvento ariyaṃ aṭṭhaṅgikaṃ maggaṃ bahulīkaronto nibbānaninno hoti nibbānapoṇo nibbānapabbhāro’’ti. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

122–126. "Just as, bhikkhus, the River Yamunā inclines to the ocean, slopes to the ocean, tends to the ocean; so too, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu… Just as, bhikkhus, the River Aciravatī inclines to the ocean, slopes to the ocean, tends to the ocean; so too, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu… Just as, bhikkhus, the River Sarabhū inclines to the ocean, slopes to the ocean, tends to the ocean; so too, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu… Just as, bhikkhus, the River Mahī inclines to the ocean, slopes to the ocean, tends to the ocean; so too, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu… Just as, bhikkhus, all these great rivers—namely, the Gaṅgā, Yamunā, Aciravatī, Sarabhū, and Mahī—all incline to the ocean, slope to the ocean, tend to the ocean; so too, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu developing and cultivating the Noble Eightfold Path inclines towards Nibbāna, slopes towards Nibbāna, tends towards Nibbāna. And how, bhikkhus, does a bhikkhu developing and cultivating the Noble Eightfold Path incline towards Nibbāna, slope towards Nibbāna, tend towards Nibbāna? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu develops right view, which is immersed in the Deathless, leading to the Deathless, culminating in the Deathless… he develops right samādhi, which is immersed in the Deathless, leading to the Deathless, culminating in the Deathless. Thus, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu developing and cultivating the Noble Eightfold Path inclines towards Nibbāna, slopes towards Nibbāna, tends towards Nibbāna." (Sixth).

(Amatogadhadvādasakī tatiyakī).

(The Twelvefold Immersion in the Deathless, the Third).
