151."Monks, just as NÄgas, relying on the king of mountains, the Himalayas, develop their bodies and gain strength; having developed their bodies and gained strength there, they descend into small pools, from small pools they descend into large pools, from large pools they descend into small rivers, from small rivers they descend into great rivers, from great rivers they descend into the great ocean, and there they attain greatness and abundance in body; in the same way, monks, a monk, dependent on virtue, established in virtue, develops the noble eightfold path and cultivates the noble eightfold path, thereby attaining greatness and abundance in the Dhamma.
