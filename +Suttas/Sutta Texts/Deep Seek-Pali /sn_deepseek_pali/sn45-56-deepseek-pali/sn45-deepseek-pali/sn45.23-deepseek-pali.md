# SN 45.23 Paṭhamapaṭipadāsuttaṃ

## SN 45.23 The First Discourse on Practice

23. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Micchāpaṭipadañca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipadañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipadā? Seyyathidaṃ – micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipadā. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā? Seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipadā’’ti. Tatiyaṃ.

23.At Sāvatthī. “Monks, I will teach you wrong practice and right practice. Listen to that. And what, monks, is wrong practice? It is this: wrong view…wrong samādhi. This, monks, is called wrong practice. And what, monks, is right practice? It is this: right view…right samādhi. This, monks, is called right practice.” The third.
