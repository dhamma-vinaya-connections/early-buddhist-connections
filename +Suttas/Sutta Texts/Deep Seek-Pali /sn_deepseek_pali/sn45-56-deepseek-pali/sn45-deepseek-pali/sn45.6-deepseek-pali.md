# SN 45.6 Paṭhamaaññatarabhikkhusuttaṃ

## SN 45.6 First Discourse about a Certain Monk

6. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami…pe… ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca – ‘‘‘brahmacariyaṃ, brahmacariya’nti, bhante, vuccati. Katamaṃ nu kho, bhante, brahmacariyaṃ, katamaṃ brahmacariyapariyosāna’’nti?

6. At Savatthi. Then a certain monk approached the Blessed One… …and sitting to one side, that monk said to the Blessed One: "‘The holy life, the holy life,’ it is said, venerable sir. Pray, venerable sir, what is the holy life? What is the culmination of the holy life?"

‘‘Ayameva kho, bhikkhu, ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo brahmacariyaṃ, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Yo kho, bhikkhu, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo – idaṃ brahmacariyapariyosāna’’nti. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

"This ![[Noble Path]]. The destruction of lust, the destruction of hatred, the destruction of delusion—this is the culmination of the holy life." Sixth.
