# SN 45.78 Esanāsuttaṃ

## SN 45.78 The Discourse on Searches

161. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo’’ti.

161.At Sāvatthī. "Monks, there are these three searches. Which three? The search for sensual pleasures, the search for existence, the search for the holy life. These, monks, are the three searches. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, the ![[Noble Path]], dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, maturing in relinquishment. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, this Noble Eightfold Path is to be developed."

‘‘Tisso imā kho, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti rāgavinayapariyosānaṃ dosavinayapariyosānaṃ mohavinayapariyosānaṃ. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo’’ti.

"Monks, there are these three searches. What three? The search for sensual pleasure, the search for existence, the search for the holy life. These, monks, are the three searches. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, the ![[Noble Path]] culminating in the removal of lust, the removal of hatred, the removal of delusion. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, this Noble Eightfold Path is to be developed."

‘‘Tisso imā kho, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti amatogadhaṃ amataparāyanaṃ amatapariyosānaṃ. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo’’ti.

"Monks, there are these three searches. What three? The search for sensual pleasures, the search for existence, and the search for the holy life. These, monks, are the three searches. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, the ![[Noble Path]], plunging into the Deathless, having the Deathless as its destination, culminating in the Deathless. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, this Noble Eightfold Path is to be developed."

‘‘Tisso imā kho, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti nibbānaninnaṃ nibbānapoṇaṃ nibbānapabbhāraṃ. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ abhiññāya ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo’’ti.

"Monks, there are these three searches. What three? The search for sensual pleasure, the search for existence, the search for the holy life. These, monks, are the three searches. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, the ![[Noble Path]] sloping, tending, and inclining to Nibbāna. For the direct knowledge of these three searches, this Noble Eightfold Path is to be developed."

‘‘Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ pariññāya…pe… ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo’’ti. (Yadapi abhiññā, tadapi pariññāya vitthāretabbaṃ.)

"Monks, there are these three searches. What three? The search for sensual pleasures, the search for existence, the search for the holy life. These, monks, are the three searches. For the full understanding of these three searches… this Noble Eightfold Path is to be developed." (This should be expanded in the same way as the section on direct knowledge, but with 'for full understanding'.)

‘‘Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ parikkhayāya…pe… ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo’’ti. (Yadapi abhiññā, tadapi parikkhayāya vitthāretabbaṃ.)

"Monks, there are these three searches. What three? The search for sensual pleasures, the search for existence, the search for the holy life. These, monks, are the three searches. For the complete destruction of these three searches… this Noble Eightfold Path is to be developed." (This should be expanded in the same way as the section on direct knowledge, but with 'for the exhaustion'.)

‘‘Tisso imā, bhikkhave, esanā. Katamā tisso? Kāmesanā, bhavesanā, brahmacariyesanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso esanā. Imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ pahānāya ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo. Katamo ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu sammādiṭṭhiṃ bhāveti…pe… sammāsamādhiṃ bhāveti vivekanissitaṃ virāganissitaṃ nirodhanissitaṃ vossaggapariṇāmiṃ…pe… imāsaṃ kho, bhikkhave, tissannaṃ esanānaṃ pahānāya ayaṃ ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo bhāvetabbo’’ti. (Yadapi abhiññā, tadapi pahānāya vitthāretabbaṃ.) Paṭhamaṃ.

"Monks, there are these three searches. What three? The search for sensual pleasures, the search for existence, the search for the holy life. These, monks, are the three searches. For the abandoning of these three searches, the ![[Noble Path]] dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, maturing in relinquishment… For the abandoning of these three searches, this Noble Eightfold Path is to be developed." (This should be expanded in the same way as the section on direct knowledge, but with 'for the abandoning'.) The first.
