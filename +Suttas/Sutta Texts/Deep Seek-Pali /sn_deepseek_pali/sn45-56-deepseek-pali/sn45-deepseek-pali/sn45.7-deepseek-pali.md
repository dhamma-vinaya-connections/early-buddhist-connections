# SN 45.7 Dutiyaaññatarabhikkhusuttaṃ

## SN 45.7 The Second Discourse with a Certain Monk

7. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. Atha kho aññataro bhikkhu yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami…pe… ekamantaṃ nisinno kho so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca –

7. The setting was Sāvatthī. Then a certain bhikkhu approached the Blessed One… and, sitting to one side, said to him:

‘‘‘Rāgavinayo dosavinayo mohavinayo’ti, bhante, vuccati. Kissa nu kho etaṃ, bhante, adhivacanaṃ – ‘rāgavinayo dosavinayo mohavinayo’’’ti? ‘‘Nibbānadhātuyā kho etaṃ, bhikkhu, adhivacanaṃ – ‘rāgavinayo dosavinayo mohavinayo’ti. Āsavānaṃ khayo tena vuccatī’’ti.

"‘The subduing of lust, the subduing of hate, the subduing of delusion’—venerable sir, it is said. To what, venerable sir, is this an expression—‘the subduing of lust, the subduing of hate, the subduing of delusion’?"
"It is an expression, bhikkhu, for the element of Nibbāna—‘the subduing of lust, the subduing of hate, the subduing of delusion.’ The destruction of the taints is called by that.”

Evaṃ vutte so bhikkhu bhagavantaṃ etadavoca – ‘‘‘amataṃ, amata’nti, bhante, vuccati. Katamaṃ nu kho, bhante, amataṃ, katamo amatagāmimaggo’’ti? ‘‘Yo kho, bhikkhu, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo – idaṃ vuccati amataṃ. Ayameva ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo amatagāmimaggo, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhī’’ti. Sattamaṃ.

When this was said, that monk addressed the Blessed One: "'The Deathless, the Deathless'—venerable sir, it is said. What now, venerable sir, is the Deathless? What is the path leading to the Deathless?" "Whatever, monk, is the destruction of lust, the destruction of hate, the destruction of delusion—this is called the Deathless. This ![[Noble Path]]." The seventh.
