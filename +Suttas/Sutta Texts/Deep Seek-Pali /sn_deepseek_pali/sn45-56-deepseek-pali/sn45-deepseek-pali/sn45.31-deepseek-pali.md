# SN 45.31 Paṭhamapaṭipattisuttaṃ

## SN 45.31 The Discourse on the First Practice

31. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Micchāpaṭipattiñca vo, bhikkhave, desessāmi, sammāpaṭipattiñca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipatti? Seyyathidaṃ – micchādiṭṭhi…pe… micchāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, micchāpaṭipatti. Katamā ca, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipatti? Seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, sammāpaṭipattī’’ti. Paṭhamaṃ.

31.At Sāvatthī. "Monks, I will teach you wrong practice and right practice. Listen to that. And what, monks, is wrong practice? It is wrong view… wrong samādhi. This is called, monks, wrong practice. And what, monks, is right practice? It is right view… right samādhi. This is called, monks, right practice." The first.
