# SN 45.19 Dutiyakukkuṭārāmasuttaṃ

## SN 45.19 The Second Discourse In the Chicken Monastery

19. Pāṭaliputtanidānaṃ. ‘‘‘Brahmacariyaṃ, brahmacariya’nti, āvuso ānanda, vuccati. Katamaṃ nu kho, āvuso, brahmacariyaṃ, katamaṃ brahmacariyapariyosāna’’nti? ‘‘Sādhu sādhu, āvuso bhadda! Bhaddako kho te, āvuso bhadda, ummaṅgo, bhaddakaṃ paṭibhānaṃ, kalyāṇī paripucchā. Evañhi tvaṃ, āvuso bhadda, pucchasi – ‘brahmacariyaṃ, brahmacariyanti, āvuso ānanda, vuccati. Katamaṃ nu kho, āvuso, brahmacariyaṃ, katamaṃ brahmacariyapariyosāna’’’nti? ‘‘Evamāvuso’’ti. ‘‘Ayameva kho, āvuso, ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo brahmacariyaṃ, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi. Yo kho, āvuso, rāgakkhayo dosakkhayo mohakkhayo – idaṃ brahmacariyapariyosāna’’nti. Navamaṃ.

19. The Occasion at Pāṭaliputta. "'The holy life, the holy life,' friend Ānanda, is spoken of. But what, friend, is the holy life, and what is the culmination of the holy life?" "Good, good, friend Bhadda! Excellent indeed is your insight, friend Bhadda, excellent your intelligence, and excellent your question. For you ask thus: 'The holy life, the holy life,' friend Ānanda, is spoken of. But what, friend, is the holy life, and what is the culmination of the holy life?'" "Yes, friend." "This ![[Noble Path]]. The destruction of lust, the destruction of hatred, the destruction of delusion—this, friend, is the culmination of the holy life." Ninth.
