# SN 51.9 Ñāṇasuttaṃ

## SN 51.9 The Discourse on Knowledge

821. ‘‘‘Ayaṃ chandasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo’ti me bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi. ‘So kho panāyaṃ chandasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo bhāvetabbo’ti me, bhikkhave…pe… ‘bhāvito’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi.

821.“‘This is the basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of desire and volitional formations of striving’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose. ‘This basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of desire and volitional formations of striving is to be developed’—thus, monks… ‘It has been developed’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose.

‘‘‘Ayaṃ vīriyasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi. ‘So kho panāyaṃ vīriyasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo bhāvetabbo’ti me, bhikkhave…pe… ‘bhāvito’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi.

“‘This is the basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of energy and volitional formations of striving’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose. ‘This basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of energy and volitional formations of striving is to be developed’—thus, monks… ‘It has been developed’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose.”

‘‘‘Ayaṃ cittasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi. ‘So kho panāyaṃ cittasamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo bhāvetabbo’ti me, bhikkhave…pe… ‘bhāvito’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi.

“‘This is the basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of mind and volitional formations of striving’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose. ‘This basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of mind and volitional formations of striving is to be developed’—thus, monks… ‘It has been developed’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose.”

‘‘‘Ayaṃ vīmaṃsāsamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi. ‘So kho panāyaṃ vīmaṃsāsamādhippadhānasaṅkhārasamannāgato iddhipādo bhāvetabbo’ti me, bhikkhave…pe… ‘bhāvito’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādī’’ti. Navamaṃ.

“‘This is the basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of investigation and volitional formations of striving’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose. ‘This basis of spiritual power that possesses samādhi of investigation and volitional formations of striving is to be developed’—thus, monks… ‘It has been developed’—thus, monks, in regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, clear knowing arose, light arose.” The ninth.
