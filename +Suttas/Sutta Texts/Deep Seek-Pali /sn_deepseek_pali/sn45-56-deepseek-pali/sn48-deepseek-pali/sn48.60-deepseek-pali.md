# SN 48.60 Dutiyauppādasuttaṃ

## SN 48.60 The Second Arising Discourse

530. ‘‘Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni anuppannāni uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyāni bhāvitāni bahulīkatāni anuppannāni uppajjanti, nāññatra sugatavinayā’’ti. Dasamaṃ.

530."Monks, these five faculties, when developed and cultivated, come into being though they have not yet arisen, nowhere apart from the discipline of the Fortunate One. Which five? The faculty of faith, the faculty of energy, the faculty of mindfulness, the faculty of samādhi, and the faculty of wisdom. These five faculties, when developed and cultivated, come into being though they have not yet arisen, nowhere apart from the discipline of the Fortunate One." The tenth.

Sūkarakhatavaggo chaṭṭho.

The Sūkarakhatavagga, the Sixth.

Tassuddānaṃ –

Its summary:

Sālaṃ mallikaṃ sekho ca, padaṃ sāraṃ patiṭṭhitaṃ;Brahmasūkarakhatāyo, uppādā apare duveti.

Sāla, Mallikā, the Learner, the Foot, the Heartwood, the Established, Brahmā, Sūkarakhatā, and two others on Arising.

7. Bodhipakkhiyavaggo
