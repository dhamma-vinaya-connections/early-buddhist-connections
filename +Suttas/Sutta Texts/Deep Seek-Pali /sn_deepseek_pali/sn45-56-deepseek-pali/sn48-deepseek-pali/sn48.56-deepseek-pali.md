526."Monks, for a monk established in one quality, the five faculties are developed and well-developed. In what one quality? In diligence. And what, monks, is diligence? Here, monks, a monk guards his mind regarding the taints and tainted phenomena. As he guards his mind regarding the taints and tainted phenomena, the faculty of faith comes to fulfillment through development. The faculty of energy comes to fulfillment through development. The faculty of mindfulness comes to fulfillment through development. The faculty of concentration comes to fulfillment through development. The faculty of wisdom comes to fulfillment through development. Thus, monks, for a monk established in one quality, the five faculties are developed and well-developed." The sixth.
