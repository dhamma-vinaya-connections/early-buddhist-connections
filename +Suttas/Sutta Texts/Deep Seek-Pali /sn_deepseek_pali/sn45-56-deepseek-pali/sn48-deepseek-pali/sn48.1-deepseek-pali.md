# SN 48.1 Suddhikasuttaṃ

## SN 48.1 The Pure Discourse

471. Sāvatthinidānaṃ. ‘‘Pañcimāni, bhikkhave, indriyāni. Katamāni pañca? Saddhindriyaṃ, vīriyindriyaṃ, satindriyaṃ, samādhindriyaṃ, paññindriyaṃ – imāni kho, bhikkhave, pañcindriyānī’’ti. Paṭhamaṃ.

471.At Sāvatthī. “Bhikkhus, there are these five faculties. Which five? The faculty of faith, the faculty of energy, the faculty of mindfulness, the faculty of samādhi, and the faculty of wisdom. These, bhikkhus, are the five faculties.” The First.
