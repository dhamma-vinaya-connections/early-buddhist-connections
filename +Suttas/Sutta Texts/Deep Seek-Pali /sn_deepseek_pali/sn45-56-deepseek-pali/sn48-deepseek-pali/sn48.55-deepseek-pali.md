# SN 48.55 Sārasuttaṃ

## SN 48.55 The Essence Discourse

525. ‘‘Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, ye keci sāragandhā, lohitacandanaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati; evameva kho bhikkhave, ye keci bodhipakkhiyā dhammā, paññindriyaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati, yadidaṃ – bodhāya. Katame ca, bhikkhave, bodhipakkhiyā dhammā? Saddhindriyaṃ, bhikkhave, bodhipakkhiyo dhammo, taṃ bodhāya saṃvattati. Vīriyindriyaṃ…pe… satindriyaṃ …pe… samādhindriyaṃ…pe… paññindriyaṃ bodhipakkhiyo dhammo, taṃ bodhāya saṃvattati. Seyyathāpi, bhikkhave, ye keci sāragandhā, lohitacandanaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati; evameva kho, bhikkhave, ye keci bodhipakkhiyā dhammā, paññindriyaṃ tesaṃ aggamakkhāyati, yadidaṃ – bodhāyā’’ti. Pañcamaṃ.

525."Just as, monks, among all fragrant heartwoods, red sandalwood is said to be the foremost; so too, monks, among all the factors of enlightenment, the faculty of wisdom is said to be the foremost, that is, for enlightenment. And what, monks, are the factors of enlightenment? The faculty of faith, monks, is a factor of enlightenment; it conduces to enlightenment. The faculty of energy... the faculty of mindfulness... the faculty of samādhi... the faculty of wisdom is a factor of enlightenment; it conduces to enlightenment. Just as, monks, among all fragrant heartwoods, red sandalwood is said to be the foremost; so too, monks, among all the factors of enlightenment, the faculty of wisdom is said to be the foremost, that is, for enlightenment." The fifth.
