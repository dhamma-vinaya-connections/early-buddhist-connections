524."Just as, monks, whatever footprints there are of animals that roam the forest, all of them are encompassed by the footprint of the elephant, and the elephant's footprint is declared the foremost among them, that is, in terms of size; in the same way, monks, whatever factors there are that conduce to awakening, the faculty of wisdom is declared the foremost among them, that is, for awakening. And what, monks, are the factors that conduce to awakening? The faculty of faith, monks, is a factor that conduces to awakening; the faculty of energy is a factor that conduces to awakening; the faculty of mindfulness is a factor that conduces to awakening; the faculty of concentration is a factor that conduces to awakening; the faculty of wisdom is a factor that conduces to awakening. Just as, monks, whatever footprints there are of animals that roam the forest, all of them are encompassed by the footprint of the elephant, and the elephant's footprint is declared the foremost among them, that is, in terms of size; in the same way, monks, whatever factors there are that conduce to awakening, the faculty of wisdom is declared the foremost among them, that is, for awakening."
