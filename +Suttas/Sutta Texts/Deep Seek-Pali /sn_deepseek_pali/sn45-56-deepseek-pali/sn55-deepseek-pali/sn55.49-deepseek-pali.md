# SN 55.49 Mahānāmasuttaṃ

## SN 55.49 Mahānāma’s Discourse

1045. Kapilavatthunidānaṃ. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho mahānāmaṃ sakkaṃ bhagavā etadavoca – ‘‘catūhi kho, mahānāma, dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti…pe… sambodhiparāyaṇo’’.

1045.At Kapilavatthu. Seated to one side, the Blessed One said to Mahānāma the Sakyan: “Mahānāma, a noble disciple endowed with four qualities is a stream-enterer… destined for full awakening.”

‘‘Katamehi catūhi? Idha, mahānāma, ariyasāvako buddhe…pe… dhamme…pe… saṅghe…pe… ariyakantehi sīlehi samannāgato hoti akhaṇḍehi…pe… samādhisaṃvattanikehi. Imehi kho, mahānāma, catūhi dhammehi samannāgato ariyasāvako sotāpanno hoti avinipātadhammo niyato sambodhiparāyaṇo’’ti. Navamaṃ.

“What four? Here, Mahānāma, a noble disciple is endowed with unwavering confidence in the Buddha… in the Dhamma… in the Saṅgha… He is endowed with virtues dear to the noble ones, unbroken, untorn, unblemished, unmottled, liberating, praised by the wise, ungrasped by craving and views, leading to samādhi. Mahānāma, a noble disciple endowed with these four qualities is a stream-enterer, not subject to falling into the states of woe, assured, destined for full awakening.” Ninth.
