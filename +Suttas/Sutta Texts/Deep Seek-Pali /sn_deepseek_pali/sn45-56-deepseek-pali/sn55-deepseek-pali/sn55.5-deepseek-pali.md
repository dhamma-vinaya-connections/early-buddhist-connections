# SN 55.5 Dutiyasāriputtasuttaṃ

## SN 55.5 The Second Sāriputta Discourse

1001. Atha kho āyasmā sāriputto yena bhagavā tenupasaṅkami; upasaṅkamitvā bhagavantaṃ abhivādetvā ekamantaṃ nisīdi. Ekamantaṃ nisinnaṃ kho āyasmantaṃ sāriputtaṃ bhagavā etadavoca – ‘‘‘sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ, sotāpattiyaṅga’nti hidaṃ, sāriputta, vuccati. Katamaṃ nu kho sāriputta, sotāpattiyaṅga’’nti? ‘‘Sappurisasaṃsevo hi, bhante, sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ, saddhammassavanaṃ sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ, yonisomanasikāro sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ, dhammānudhammappaṭipatti sotāpattiyaṅga’’nti. ‘‘Sādhu sādhu, sāriputta! Sappurisasaṃsevo hi, sāriputta, sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ, saddhammassavanaṃ sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ, yonisomanasikāro sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ, dhammānudhammappaṭipatti sotāpattiyaṅgaṃ’’.

1001.Then Venerable Sāriputta approached the Blessed One, paid homage to him, and sat down to one side. As he was sitting to one side, the Blessed One said to Venerable Sāriputta: “‘Stream-entry factor, stream-entry factor,’ it is said, Sāriputta. What now, Sāriputta, is a stream-entry factor?” “Association with good people, venerable sir, is a stream-entry factor; listening to the true Dhamma is a stream-entry factor; appropriate attention is a stream-entry factor; practice in accordance with the Dhamma is a stream-entry factor.” “Good, good, Sāriputta! Association with good people, Sāriputta, is a stream-entry factor; listening to the true Dhamma is a stream-entry factor; appropriate attention is a stream-entry factor; practice in accordance with the Dhamma is a stream-entry factor.”

‘‘‘Soto, soto’ti hidaṃ, sāriputta, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, sāriputta, soto’’ti? ‘‘Ayameva hi, bhante, ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo soto, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhī’’ti. ‘‘Sādhu sādhu, sāriputta! Ayameva hi, sāriputta, ariyo aṭṭhaṅgiko maggo soto, seyyathidaṃ – sammādiṭṭhi…pe… sammāsamādhi’’.

“‘The stream, the stream,’ it is said here, Sāriputta. What now, Sāriputta, is the stream?” “This Noble Eightfold Path itself, venerable sir, is the stream; that is, right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samādhi.” “Good, good, Sāriputta! This Noble Eightfold Path itself, Sāriputta, is the stream; that is, right view…right samādhi.”

‘‘‘Sotāpanno, sotāpanno’ti hidaṃ, sāriputta, vuccati. Katamo nu kho, sāriputta, sotāpanno’’ti? ‘‘Yo hi, bhante, iminā ariyena aṭṭhaṅgikena maggena samannāgato ayaṃ vuccati sotāpanno, svāyaṃ āyasmā evaṃnāmo evaṃgotto’’ti. ‘‘Sādhu sādhu, sāriputta! Yo hi, sāriputta, iminā ariyena aṭṭhaṅgikena maggena samannāgato ayaṃ vuccati sotāpanno, svāyaṃ āyasmā evaṃnāmo evaṃgotto’’ti. Pañcamaṃ.

“‘A stream-enterer, a stream-enterer,’ it is said here, Sāriputta. What, Sāriputta, is a stream-enterer?” “One who is endowed with this noble eightfold path, venerable sir, is called a stream-enterer. That venerable one of such name and clan is such a one.” “Good, good, Sāriputta! One who is endowed with this noble eightfold path, Sāriputta, is called a stream-enterer. That venerable one of such name and clan is such a one.”
