# SN 52.21 Jhānādisuttaṃ

## SN 52.21 The Discourse on Jhāna, etc.

919. ‘‘Imesañca panāhaṃ, āvuso, catunnaṃ satipaṭṭhānānaṃ bhāvitattā bahulīkatattā jhānavimokkhasamādhisamāpattīnaṃ saṃkilesaṃ vodānaṃ vuṭṭhānaṃ yathābhūtaṃ pajānāmī’’ti. Ekādasamaṃ.

919.Furthermore, friends, because these four foundations of mindfulness have been developed and cultivated, I understand as it actually is the defilement, purification, and emergence regarding the jhānas, liberations, samādhis, and attainments.
