904.The Origin at SÄketa. Seated to one side, Venerable SÄriputta said to Venerable Anuruddha: "Venerable Anuruddha, through the development and cultivation of which qualities have you attained great direct knowledge?" "Friend, it is through the development and cultivation of the four foundations of mindfulness that I have attained great direct knowledge. Which four? Here, friend, I dwell contemplating the body as body, ardent, clearly comprehending, mindful, having removed covetousness and displeasure in regard to the world; feelings as feelings... mind as mind... phenomena as phenomena, ardent, clearly comprehending, mindful, having removed covetousness and displeasure in regard to the world. Friend, it is through the development and cultivation of these four foundations of mindfulness that I have attained great direct knowledge. And further, friend, through the development and cultivation of these four foundations of mindfulness, I directly know the thousandfold world." The sixth.
