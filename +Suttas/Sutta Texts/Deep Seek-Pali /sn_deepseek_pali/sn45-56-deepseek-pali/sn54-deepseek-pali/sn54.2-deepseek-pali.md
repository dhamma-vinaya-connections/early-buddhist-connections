978."Mindfulness of breathing, monks, when developed and cultivated, is of great fruit and great benefit. And how, monks, is mindfulness of breathing developed and cultivated to be of great fruit and great benefit? Here, monks, a monk develops the mindfulness enlightenment factor accompanied by mindfulness of breathing, dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, culminating in relinquishment. He develops the investigation of phenomena enlightenment factor accompanied by mindfulness of breathing, dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, culminating in relinquishment; he develops the energy enlightenment factor accompanied by mindfulness of breathing, dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, culminating in relinquishment; he develops the rapture enlightenment factor accompanied by mindfulness of breathing, dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, culminating in relinquishment; he develops the tranquility enlightenment factor accompanied by mindfulness of breathing, dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, culminating in relinquishment; he develops the concentration enlightenment factor accompanied by mindfulness of breathing, dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, culminating in relinquishment; he develops the equanimity enlightenment factor accompanied by mindfulness of breathing, dependent on seclusion, dependent on dispassion, dependent on cessation, culminating in relinquishment. It is in this way, monks, that mindfulness of breathing is developed and cultivated to be of great fruit and great benefit." This was the second discourse.
