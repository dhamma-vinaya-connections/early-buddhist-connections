# SN 54.17 Saṃyojanappahānasuttaṃ

## SN 54.17 The Discourse on Abandoning Fetters

993. Ānāpānassatisamādhi, bhikkhave, bhāvito bahulīkato saṃyojanappahānāya saṃvattati…pe…. Sattamaṃ.

993.Monks, samādhi through mindfulness of breathing, when developed and cultivated, leads to the abandonment of the fetters… The Seventh.
