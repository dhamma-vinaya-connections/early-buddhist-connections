# SN 54.20 Āsavakkhayasuttaṃ

## SN 54.20 The Discourse on the Destruction of the Taints

996. Āsavānaṃ khayāya saṃvattati. Kathaṃ bhāvito ca, bhikkhave, ānāpānassatisamādhi kathaṃ bahulīkato saṃyojanappahānāya saṃvattati… anusayasamugghātāya saṃvattati… addhānapariññāya saṃvattati… āsavānaṃ khayāya saṃvattati? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu araññagato vā rukkhamūlagato vā…pe. … paṭinissaggānupassī assasissāmīti sikkhati, paṭinissaggānupassī passasissāmīti sikkhati. Evaṃ bhāvito kho, bhikkhave, ānāpānassatisamādhi evaṃ bahulīkato saṃyojanappahānāya saṃvattati…pe… anusayasamugghātāya saṃvattati…pe… addhānapariññāya saṃvattati…pe… āsavānaṃ khayāya saṃvattatīti. Dasamaṃ.

996.…leads to the destruction of the taints. And how, bhikkhus, when developed and cultivated, does samādhi through mindfulness of breathing lead to the abandoning of the fetters… to the eradication of underlying tendencies… to the full comprehension of the course of time… to the destruction of the taints? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu, gone to the wilderness, or to the foot of a tree… trains himself, thinking: ‘I shall breathe in, contemplating abandonment.’ He trains himself, thinking: ‘I shall breathe out, contemplating abandonment.’ Thus developed and cultivated, bhikkhus, samādhi through mindfulness of breathing leads to the abandoning of the fetters… to the eradication of underlying tendencies… to the full comprehension of the course of time… to the destruction of the taints. The Tenth.

Dutiyo vaggo.

The Second Chapter.

Tassuddānaṃ –

Its summary:

Icchānaṅgalaṃ kaṅkheyyaṃ, ānandā apare duve;Bhikkhū saṃyojanānusayā, addhānaṃ āsavakkhayanti.

Icchānaṅgala, Kaṅkheyya, Ānanda, and two others; Bhikkhu, Fetters, Underlying Tendencies, Course of Time, Destruction of the Taints.

Ānāpānasaṃyuttaṃ dasamaṃ.

The Tenth Chapter on Mindfulness of Breathing.
