327.'Suffering, suffering,' friend SÄriputta, it is said. What now, friend, is suffering?" "These are the three kinds of suffering, friend: the suffering of suffering, the suffering of formations, and the suffering of change. These, friend, are the three kinds of suffering." "Is there, friend, a path, is there a practice for the full understanding of these sufferings?" "There is, friend, a path, a practice for the full understanding of these sufferings." "What now, friend, is the path, what is the practice for the full understanding of these sufferings?" "This Noble Eightfold Path, friend, for the full understanding of these sufferings, namely: right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right concentration. This, friend, is the path, this is the practice for the full understanding of these sufferings." "Excellent, friend, is the path, excellent is the practice for the full understanding of these sufferings. And it is quite enough, friend SÄriputta, for heedfulness." The fourteenth.
