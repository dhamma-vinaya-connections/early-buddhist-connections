326.'Becoming, becoming,' friend SÄriputta, it is said. What now, friend, is becoming?" "These three becomings, friend, are: sensual becoming, form becoming, formless becoming. These, friend, are the three becomings." "Is there, friend, a path, a practice, for the full understanding of these becomings?" "There is, friend, a path, a practice, for the full understanding of these becomings." "What now, friend, is the path, what is the practice, for the full understanding of these becomings?" "This Noble Eightfold Path itself, friend, is the path, the practice, for the full understanding of these becomings, that is: right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right concentration. This, friend, is the path, this is the practice, for the full understanding of these becomings." "Excellent, friend, is the path, excellent is the practice, for the full understanding of these becomings. And it is quite enough, friend SÄriputta, for heedfulness." The thirteenth.
