283."Monks, a woman endowed with three qualities, upon the breakup of the body, after death, is for the most part reborn in a state of deprivation, a bad destination, the lower realms, hell. What three? Here, monks, a woman dwells at home in the morning with a mind overcome by the taint of stinginess. At midday, she dwells at home with a mind overcome by envy. In the evening, she dwells at home with a mind overcome by sensual lust. Monks, a woman endowed with these three qualities, upon the breakup of the body, after death, is for the most part reborn in a state of deprivation, a bad destination, the lower realms, hell."
