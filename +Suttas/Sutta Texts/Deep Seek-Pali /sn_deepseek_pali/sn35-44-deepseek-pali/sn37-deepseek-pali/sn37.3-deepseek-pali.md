282."Monks, there are these five peculiar sufferings for women, which women experience, unlike men. What are the five? Here, monks, a woman, while still young, goes to her husband's family and is separated from her relatives. This, monks, is the first peculiar suffering for a woman, which she experiences, unlike men. This, monks, is the fourth peculiar suffering for a woman, which she experiences, unlike men. Furthermore, monks, a woman attends upon a man. This, monks, is the fifth peculiar suffering for a woman, which she experiences, unlike men. These, monks, are the five peculiar sufferings for women, which women experience, unlike men."
