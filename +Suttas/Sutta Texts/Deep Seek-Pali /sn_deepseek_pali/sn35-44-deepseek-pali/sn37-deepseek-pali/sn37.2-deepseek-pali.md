# SN 37.2 Purisasuttaṃ

## SN 37.2 The Man Discourse

281. ‘‘Pañcahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato puriso ekantaamanāpo hoti mātugāmassa. Katamehi pañcahi? Na ca rūpavā hoti, na ca bhogavā hoti, na ca sīlavā hoti, alaso ca hoti, pajañcassa na labhati – imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi aṅgehi samannāgato puriso ekantaamanāpo hoti mātugāmassa. Pañcahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato puriso ekantamanāpo hoti mātugāmassa. Katamehi pañcahi? Rūpavā ca hoti, bhogavā ca hoti, sīlavā ca hoti, dakkho ca hoti analaso, pajañcassa labhati – imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi aṅgehi samannāgato puriso ekantamanāpo hoti mātugāmassā’’ti. Dutiyaṃ.

281."bhikkhus, a man endowed with five qualities is entirely displeasing to women. What five? He is not handsome, not wealthy, not virtuous, he is lazy, and he does not obtain offspring. bhikkhus, a man endowed with these five qualities is entirely displeasing to women. bhikkhus, a man endowed with five qualities is entirely pleasing to women. What five? He is handsome, wealthy, virtuous, skillful and not lazy, and he obtains offspring. bhikkhus, a man endowed with these five qualities is entirely pleasing to women." The second.
