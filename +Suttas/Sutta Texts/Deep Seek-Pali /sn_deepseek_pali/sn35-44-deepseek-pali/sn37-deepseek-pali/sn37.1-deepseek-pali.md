# SN 37.1 Mātugāmasuttaṃ

## SN 37.1 The Discourse on Women

280. ‘‘Pañcahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato mātugāmo ekantaamanāpo hoti purisassa. Katamehi pañcahi? Na ca rūpavā hoti, na ca bhogavā hoti, na ca sīlavā hoti, alaso ca hoti, pajañcassa na labhati – imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi aṅgehi samannāgato mātugāmo ekantaamanāpo hoti purisassa. Pañcahi, bhikkhave, aṅgehi samannāgato mātugāmo ekantamanāpo hoti purisassa. Katamehi pañcahi? Rūpavā ca hoti, bhogavā ca hoti, sīlavā ca hoti, dakkho ca hoti analaso, pajañcassa labhati – imehi kho, bhikkhave, pañcahi aṅgehi samannāgato mātugāmo ekantamanāpo hoti purisassā’’ti. Paṭhamaṃ.

280."bhikkhus, a woman endowed with five qualities is entirely displeasing to a man. What five? She is not beautiful, she is not wealthy, she is not virtuous, she is lazy, and she does not obtain children. bhikkhus, a woman endowed with these five qualities is entirely displeasing to a man. bhikkhus, a woman endowed with five qualities is entirely pleasing to a man. What five? She is beautiful, she is wealthy, she is virtuous, she is skillful and not lazy, and she obtains children. bhikkhus, a woman endowed with these five qualities is entirely pleasing to a man." The first.
