# SN 43.3 Savitakkasavicārasuttaṃ

## SN 43.3 The Discourse on Applied Thought and Sustained Thought

368. ‘‘Katamo ca, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo? Savitakkasavicāro samādhi, avitakkavicāramatto samādhi, avitakkaavicāro samādhi – ayaṃ vuccati, bhikkhave, asaṅkhatagāmimaggo…pe…. Tatiyaṃ.

368.And what, bhikkhus, is the path leading to the unconditioned? It is samādhi with applied thought and sustained thought; samādhi without applied thought, with sustained thought only; samādhi without applied thought and sustained thought. This, bhikkhus, is called the path leading to the unconditioned. [as before] Third.
