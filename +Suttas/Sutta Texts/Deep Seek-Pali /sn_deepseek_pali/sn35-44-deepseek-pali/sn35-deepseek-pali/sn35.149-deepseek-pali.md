# SN 35.149 Ajjhattaaniccachandasuttaṃ

## SN 35.149 The Discourse on Desire for the Internal Impermanent

168. ‘‘Yaṃ, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ, tatra vo chando pahātabbo. Kiñca, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ? Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ; tatra vo chando pahātabbo…pe… jivhā aniccā; tatra vo chando pahātabbo…pe… mano anicco; tatra vo chando pahātabbo. Yaṃ, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ, tatra vo chando pahātabbo’’ti.

168.“bhikkhus, whatever is impermanent, therein you should abandon desire. And what, bhikkhus, is impermanent? The eye, bhikkhus, is impermanent; therein you should abandon desire… The tongue is impermanent; therein you should abandon desire… The mind is impermanent; therein you should abandon desire. Whatever, bhikkhus, is impermanent, therein you should abandon desire.”
