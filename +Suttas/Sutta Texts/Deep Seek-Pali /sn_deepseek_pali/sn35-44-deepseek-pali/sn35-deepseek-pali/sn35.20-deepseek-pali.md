# SN 35.20 Dutiyābhinandasuttaṃ

## SN 35.20 Second Delight Sutta

20. ‘‘Yo, bhikkhave, rūpe abhinandati, dukkhaṃ so abhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ abhinandati, aparimutto so dukkhasmāti vadāmi. Yo sadde…pe… gandhe… rase… phoṭṭhabbe… dhamme abhinandati, dukkhaṃ so abhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ abhinandati, aparimutto so dukkhasmā’’ti vadāmi.

20. "bhikkhus, whoever delights in form, delights in suffering. Whoever delights in suffering, I say, is not freed from suffering. Whoever delights in sounds... in smells... in tastes... in tactile objects... in mental phenomena, delights in suffering. Whoever delights in suffering, I say, is not freed from suffering."

‘‘Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, rūpe nābhinandati, dukkhaṃ so nābhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ nābhinandati, parimutto so dukkhasmāti vadāmi. Yo sadde…pe… gandhe… rase… phoṭṭhabbe… dhamme nābhinandati, dukkhaṃ so nābhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ nābhinandati, parimutto so dukkhasmā’’ti vadāmi. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

But, bhikkhus, whoever does not delight in form does not delight in suffering. Whoever does not delight in suffering, I say, is freed from suffering. Whoever does not delight in sounds... in smells... in tastes... in tactile objects... in mental phenomena does not delight in suffering. Whoever does not delight in suffering, I say, is freed from suffering. The eighth.
