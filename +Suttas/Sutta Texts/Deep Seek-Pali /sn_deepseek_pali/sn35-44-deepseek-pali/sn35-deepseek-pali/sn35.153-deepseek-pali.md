# SN 35.153 Ajjhattānāgatāniccasuttaṃ

## SN 35.153 The Discourse on Internal Future Impermanence

187. ‘‘Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ anāgataṃ…pe… jivhā aniccā anāgatā…pe… mano anicco anāgato. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe… nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī’’ti.

187."The eye, bhikkhus, is impermanent, not yet come... The tongue is impermanent, not yet come... The mind is impermanent, not yet come. Seeing thus... he understands: ‘there is no more for this state of being.’"
