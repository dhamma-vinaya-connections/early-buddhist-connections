# SN 35.158 Ajjhattāyatanaaniccasuttaṃ

## SN 35.158 The Discourse on the Impermanence of the Internal Sense Bases

222. ‘‘Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ…pe… jivhā aniccā…pe… mano anicco. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe… nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī’’ti.

222.“bhikkhus, the eye is impermanent…the tongue is impermanent…the mind is impermanent. Seeing thus…he understands: ‘There is no more for this state of being.’”
