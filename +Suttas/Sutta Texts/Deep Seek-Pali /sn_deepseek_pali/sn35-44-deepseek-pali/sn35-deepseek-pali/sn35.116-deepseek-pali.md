# SN 35.116 Khaṇasuttaṃ

## SN 35.116 The Opportunity Sutta

135. ‘‘Lābhā vo, bhikkhave, suladdhaṃ vo, bhikkhave, khaṇo vo paṭiladdho brahmacariyavāsāya. Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, chaphassāyatanikā nāma nirayā. Tattha yaṃ kiñci cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati aniṭṭharūpaṃyeva passati, no iṭṭharūpaṃ; akantarūpaṃyeva passati, no kantarūpaṃ; amanāparūpaṃyeva passati, no manāparūpaṃ. Yaṃ kiñci sotena saddaṃ suṇāti…pe… yaṃ kiñci ghānena gandhaṃ ghāyati…pe… yaṃ kiñci jivhāya rasaṃ sāyati…pe… yaṃ kiñci kāyena phoṭṭhabbaṃ phusati…pe… yaṃ kiñci manasā dhammaṃ vijānāti aniṭṭharūpaṃyeva vijānāti, no iṭṭharūpaṃ; akantarūpaṃyeva vijānāti, no kantarūpaṃ; amanāparūpaṃyeva vijānāti, no manāparūpaṃ. Lābhā vo, bhikkhave, suladdhaṃ vo, bhikkhave, khaṇo vo paṭiladdho brahmacariyavāsāya. Diṭṭhā mayā, bhikkhave, chaphassāyatanikā nāma saggā. Tattha yaṃ kiñci cakkhunā rūpaṃ passati iṭṭharūpaṃyeva passati, no aniṭṭharūpaṃ; kantarūpaṃyeva passati, no akantarūpaṃ; manāparūpaṃyeva passati, no amanāparūpaṃ…pe… yaṃ kiñci jivhāya rasaṃ sāyati…pe… yaṃ kiñci manasā dhammaṃ vijānāti iṭṭharūpaṃyeva vijānāti, no aniṭṭharūpaṃ; kantarūpaṃyeva vijānāti, no akantarūpaṃ; manāparūpaṃyeva vijānāti, no amanāparūpaṃ. Lābhā vo, bhikkhave, suladdhaṃ vo, bhikkhave, khaṇo vo paṭiladdho brahmacariyavāsāyā’’ti. Dutiyaṃ.

135.“You are fortunate, bhikkhus, truly fortunate, bhikkhus, for having obtained the opportunity to live the holy life. bhikkhus, I have seen a hell called the Sixfold Sense Sphere. There, whatever form one sees with the eye is only unpleasing, not pleasing; only disagreeable, not agreeable; only unattractive, not attractive. Whatever sound one hears with the ear… whatever odor one smells with the nose… whatever taste one savors with the tongue… whatever touch one feels with the body… whatever mental phenomenon one cognizes with the mind is only unpleasing, not pleasing; only disagreeable, not agreeable; only unattractive, not attractive. You are fortunate, bhikkhus, truly fortunate, bhikkhus, for having obtained the opportunity to live the holy life. bhikkhus, I have seen a heaven called the Sixfold Sense Sphere. There, whatever form one sees with the eye is only pleasing, not unpleasing; only agreeable, not disagreeable; only attractive, not unattractive… whatever taste one savors with the tongue… whatever mental phenomenon one cognizes with the mind is only pleasing, not unpleasing; only agreeable, not disagreeable; only attractive, not unattractive. You are fortunate, bhikkhus, truly fortunate, bhikkhus, for having obtained the opportunity to live the holy life.” The Second.
