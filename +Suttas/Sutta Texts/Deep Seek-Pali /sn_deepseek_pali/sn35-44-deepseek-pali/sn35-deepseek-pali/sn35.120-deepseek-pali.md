139."Monks, whatever is not yours, abandon it. That, being abandoned, will lead to your welfare and happiness. And what, monks, is not yours? Forms are not yours; abandon them. Those, being abandoned, will lead to your welfare and happiness. Sounds... smells... tastes... tactile sensations... mental phenomena are not yours; abandon them. Those, being abandoned, will lead to your welfare and happiness. Just as, monks, whatever is in this Jeta Grove... so too, monks, forms are not yours; abandon them. Those, being abandoned, will lead to your welfare and happiness." The sixth.
