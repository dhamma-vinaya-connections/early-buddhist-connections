# SN 35.21 Paṭhamadukkhuppādasuttaṃ

## SN 35.21 First Origin of Suffering Sutta

21. ‘‘Yo, bhikkhave, cakkhussa uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo. Yo sotassa…pe… yo ghānassa… yo jivhāya… yo kāyassa… yo manassa uppādo ṭhiti abhinibbatti pātubhāvo, dukkhasseso uppādo, rogānaṃ ṭhiti, jarāmaraṇassa pātubhāvo.

21. "bhikkhus, the arising, persistence, production, and appearance of the eye is the arising of suffering, the persistence of diseases, and the appearance of aging and death. The arising, persistence, production, and appearance of the ear... the nose... the tongue... the body... the mind is the arising of suffering, the persistence of diseases, and the appearance of aging and death."

‘‘Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, cakkhussa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo. Yo sotassa… yo ghānassa… yo jivhāya… yo kāyassa… yo manassa nirodho vūpasamo atthaṅgamo, dukkhasseso nirodho, rogānaṃ vūpasamo, jarāmaraṇassa atthaṅgamo’’ti. Navamaṃ.

"Its cessation, pacification, and disappearance is the cessation of suffering, the pacification of diseases, and the disappearance of aging and death. The ear… the nose… the tongue… the body… the mind is the cessation of suffering, the pacification of diseases, and the disappearance of aging and death." The ninth.
