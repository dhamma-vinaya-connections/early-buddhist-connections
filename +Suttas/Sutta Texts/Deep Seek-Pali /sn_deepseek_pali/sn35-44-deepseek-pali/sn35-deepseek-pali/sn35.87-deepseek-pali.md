"And what, monks, is the cessation of suffering? Dependent on the eye and forms, eye-consciousness arises. The meeting of the three is contact. With the remainderless fading away and cessation of that very craving comes cessation of clinging; with cessation of clinging, cessation of existence; with cessation of existence, cessation of birth; with cessation of birth, aging and death, sorrow, lamentation, pain, grief, and despair cease.
