# SN 35.103 Saṃyojaniyadhammasuttaṃ

## SN 35.103 The Discourse on Fettering Things

122. ‘‘Saṃyojaniye ca, bhikkhave, dhamme desessāmi saṃyojanañca. Taṃ suṇātha. Katame ca, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyā dhammā, katamañca saṃyojanaṃ? Santi, bhikkhave, cakkhuviññeyyā rūpā iṭṭhā kantā manāpā piyarūpā kāmūpasaṃhitā rajanīyā. Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyā dhammā. Yo tattha chandarāgo, taṃ tattha saṃyojanaṃ…pe… santi, bhikkhave, jivhāviññeyyā rasā…pe… santi, bhikkhave, manoviññeyyā dhammā iṭṭhā kantā manāpā piyarūpā kāmūpasaṃhitā rajanīyā. Ime vuccanti, bhikkhave, saṃyojaniyā dhammā. Yo tattha chandarāgo taṃ tattha saṃyojana’’nti. Navamaṃ.

122."bhikkhus, I will teach you fettering things and the fetter. Listen to that. And what, bhikkhus, are fettering things, and what is the fetter? There are, bhikkhus, forms cognizable by the eye—desirable, agreeable, pleasing, lovely, connected with sensual desire, and enticing. These are called, bhikkhus, fettering things. Whatever desire and lust there is for them, that is the fetter there. [...] There are, bhikkhus, flavors cognizable by the tongue. [...] There are, bhikkhus, mental phenomena cognizable by the mind—desirable, agreeable, pleasing, lovely, connected with sensual desire, and enticing. These are called, bhikkhus, fettering things. Whatever desire and lust there is for them, that is the fetter there." The ninth.
