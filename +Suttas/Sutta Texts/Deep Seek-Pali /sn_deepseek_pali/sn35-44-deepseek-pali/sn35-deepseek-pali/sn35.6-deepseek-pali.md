6. Monks, forms are not-self. What is not-self should be seen as it really is with right wisdom: 'This is not mine, this I am not, this is not my self.' Sounds... smells... tastes... tactile sensations... mental phenomena are not-self. What is not-self should be seen as it really is with right wisdom: 'This is not mine, this I am not, this is not my self.' Seeing thus... he understands: 'There is no more for this state of being.'"
