# SN 35.159 Ajjhattāyatanadukkhasuttaṃ

## SN 35.159 The Discourse on Suffering and the Internal Sense Bases

223. ‘‘Cakkhu, bhikkhave, dukkhaṃ…pe… jivhā dukkhā…pe… mano dukkho. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe… nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī’’ti.

223.“bhikkhus, the eye is suffering…the tongue is suffering…the mind is suffering. Seeing thus…he understands: ‘There is no more for this state of being.’”
