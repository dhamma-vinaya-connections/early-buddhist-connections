# SN 35.162 Bāhirāyatanadukkhasuttaṃ

## SN 35.162 The Discourse on Suffering and the External Sense Bases

226. ‘‘Rūpā, bhikkhave, dukkhā. Saddā… gandhā… rasā… phoṭṭhabbā… dhammā dukkhā. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe… nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī’’ti.

226.“bhikkhus, forms are suffering. Sounds…smells…tastes…tactile sensations…mental phenomena are suffering. Seeing thus…he understands: ‘There is no more for this state of being.’”
