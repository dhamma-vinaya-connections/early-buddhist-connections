10. Forms, Bhikkhus, are impermanent, whether past or future. What need to speak of those in the present! Seeing thus, Bhikkhus, a learned noble disciple becomes unconcerned with past forms, does not delight in future forms, and is committed to the disenchantment, dispassion, and cessation of present forms. Sounds... smells... tastes... touches... mental phenomena are impermanent, whether past or future. What need to speak of those in the present! Seeing thus, Bhikkhus, a learned noble disciple becomes unconcerned with past mental phenomena, does not delight in future mental phenomena, and is committed to the disenchantment, dispassion, and cessation of present mental phenomena.
