# SN 35.160 Ajjhattāyatanaanattasuttaṃ

## SN 35.160 The Discourse on the Non-self of the Internal Sense Bases

224. ‘‘Cakkhu, bhikkhave, anattā…pe… jivhā anattā…pe… mano anattā. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe… nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī’’ti.

224.“bhikkhus, the eye is non-self…the tongue is non-self…the mind is non-self. Seeing thus…he understands: ‘There is no more for this state of being.’”
