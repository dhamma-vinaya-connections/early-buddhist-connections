# SN 35.150 Ajjhattaaniccarāgasuttaṃ

## SN 35.150 The Discourse on Lust for the Internal Impermanent

169. ‘‘Yaṃ, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ, tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo. Kiñca, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ? Cakkhu, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ; tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo…pe… jivhā aniccā; tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo…pe… mano anicco; tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo. Yaṃ, bhikkhave, aniccaṃ, tatra vo rāgo pahātabbo’’ti.

169.“bhikkhus, whatever is impermanent, therein you should abandon lust. And what, bhikkhus, is impermanent? The eye, bhikkhus, is impermanent; therein you should abandon lust… The tongue is impermanent; therein you should abandon lust… The mind is impermanent; therein you should abandon lust. Whatever, bhikkhus, is impermanent, therein you should abandon lust.”
