# SN 35.19 Paṭhamābhinandasuttaṃ

## SN 35.19 The First Discourse on Rejoicing

19. ‘‘Yo, bhikkhave, cakkhuṃ abhinandati, dukkhaṃ so abhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ abhinandati, aparimutto so dukkhasmāti vadāmi. Yo sotaṃ…pe… yo ghānaṃ…pe… yo jivhaṃ abhinandati, dukkhaṃ so abhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ abhinandati, aparimutto so dukkhasmāti vadāmi. Yo kāyaṃ…pe… yo manaṃ abhinandati, dukkhaṃ so abhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ abhinandati, aparimutto so dukkhasmā’’ti vadāmi.

19. "bhikkhus, whoever delights in the eye delights in suffering. Whoever delights in suffering, I say, is not released from suffering. Whoever delights in the ear... the nose... the tongue delights in suffering. Whoever delights in suffering, I say, is not released from suffering. Whoever delights in the body... the mind delights in suffering. Whoever delights in suffering, I say, is not released from suffering."

‘‘Yo ca kho, bhikkhave, cakkhuṃ nābhinandati, dukkhaṃ so nābhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ nābhinandati, parimutto so dukkhasmāti vadāmi. Yo sotaṃ…pe… yo ghānaṃ…pe… yo jivhaṃ nābhinandati, dukkhaṃ so nābhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ nābhinandati, parimutto so dukkhasmāti vadāmi. Yo kāyaṃ…pe… yo manaṃ nābhinandati, dukkhaṃ so nābhinandati. Yo dukkhaṃ nābhinandati, parimutto so dukkhasmā’’ti vadāmi. Sattamaṃ.

"But now, bhikkhus, whoever does not delight in the eye does not delight in suffering. Whoever does not delight in suffering, I say, is released from suffering. Whoever does not delight in the ear... the nose... the tongue does not delight in suffering. Whoever does not delight in suffering, I say, is released from suffering. Whoever does not delight in the body... the mind does not delight in suffering. Whoever does not delight in suffering, I say, is released from suffering." The seventh.
