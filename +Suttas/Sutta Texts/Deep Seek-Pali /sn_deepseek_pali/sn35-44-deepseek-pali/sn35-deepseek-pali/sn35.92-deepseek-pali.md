# SN 35.92 Ajjhattikāyatanaparijānanasuttaṃ

## SN 35.92 Discourse on Comprehending the Internal Sense Bases

111. ‘‘Cakkhuṃ, bhikkhave, anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Sotaṃ… ghānaṃ… jivhaṃ… kāyaṃ… manaṃ anabhijānaṃ aparijānaṃ avirājayaṃ appajahaṃ abhabbo dukkhakkhayāya. Cakkhuñca kho, bhikkhave, abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāya…pe… jivhaṃ… kāyaṃ… manaṃ abhijānaṃ parijānaṃ virājayaṃ pajahaṃ bhabbo dukkhakkhayāyā’’ti. Aṭṭhamaṃ.

111.“bhikkhus, not fully knowing the eye, not fully understanding it, not developing dispassion for it, not abandoning it, one is incapable of the destruction of suffering. The ear…the nose…the tongue…the body…the mind—not fully knowing it, not fully understanding it, not developing dispassion for it, not abandoning it, one is incapable of the destruction of suffering. But, bhikkhus, by fully knowing the eye, fully understanding it, developing dispassion for it, abandoning it, one is capable of the destruction of suffering…the ear…the nose…the tongue…the body…the mind—by fully knowing it, fully understanding it, developing dispassion for it, abandoning it, one is capable of the destruction of suffering.”
