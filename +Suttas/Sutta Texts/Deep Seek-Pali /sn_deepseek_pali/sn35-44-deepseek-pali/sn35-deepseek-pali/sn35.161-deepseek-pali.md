# SN 35.161 Bāhirāyatanaaniccasuttaṃ

## SN 35.161 The Discourse on the Impermanence of the External Sense Bases

225. ‘‘Rūpā, bhikkhave, aniccā. Saddā… gandhā… rasā… phoṭṭhabbā… dhammā aniccā. Evaṃ passaṃ…pe… nāparaṃ itthattāyāti pajānātī’’ti.

225.“bhikkhus, forms are impermanent. Sounds…smells…tastes…tactile sensations…mental phenomena are impermanent. Seeing thus…he understands: ‘There is no more for this state of being.’”
