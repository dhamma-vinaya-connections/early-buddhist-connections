# SN 36.29 Tatiyasamaṇabrāhmaṇasuttaṃ

## SN 36.29 The Third Discourse on Ascetics and Brahmins

277. ‘‘Ye hi keci, bhikkhave, samaṇā vā brāhmaṇā vā vedanaṃ nappajānanti, vedanāsamudayaṃ nappajānanti, vedanānirodhaṃ nappajānanti, vedanānirodhagāminiṃ paṭipadaṃ nappajānanti…pe… pajānanti…pe… sayaṃ abhiññā sacchikatvā upasampajja viharantī’’ti. Navamaṃ.

277."bhikkhus, whatever ascetics or brahmins do not understand feeling, do not understand the origin of feeling, do not understand the cessation of feeling, do not understand the path leading to the cessation of feeling... (and so on, as in the previous discourse, down to)... having realized it for themselves by direct knowledge, they abide having attained it." The ninth.
