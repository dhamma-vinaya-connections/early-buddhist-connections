# SN 36.30 Suddhikasuttaṃ

## SN 36.30 The Discourse on Purity

278. ‘‘Tisso imā, bhikkhave, vedanā. Katamā tisso? Sukhā vedanā, dukkhā vedanā, adukkhamasukhā vedanā – imā kho, bhikkhave, tisso vedanā’’ti. Dasamaṃ.

278."bhikkhus, there are these three feelings. Which three? Pleasant feeling, painful feeling, and neither-painful-nor-pleasant feeling. These, bhikkhus, are the three feelings." The tenth.
