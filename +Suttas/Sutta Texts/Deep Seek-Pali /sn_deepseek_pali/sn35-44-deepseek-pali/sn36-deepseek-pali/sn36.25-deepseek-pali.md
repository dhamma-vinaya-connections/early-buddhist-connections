# SN 36.25 Ñāṇasuttaṃ

## SN 36.25 The Knowledge Discourse

273. ‘‘‘Imā vedanā’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādi. ‘Ayaṃ vedanāsamudayo’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi…pe… āloko udapādi. ‘Ayaṃ vedanāsamudayagāminī paṭipadā’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi…pe… ‘ayaṃ vedanānirodho’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi …pe… ‘ayaṃ vedanānirodhagāminī paṭipadā’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi…pe… ‘ayaṃ vedanāya assādo’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu…pe… ‘ayaṃ vedanāya ādīnavo’ti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu…pe… ‘idaṃ kho nissaraṇa’nti me, bhikkhave, pubbe ananussutesu dhammesu cakkhuṃ udapādi, ñāṇaṃ udapādi, paññā udapādi, vijjā udapādi, āloko udapādī’’ti. Pañcamaṃ.

273.‘These are feelings’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, true knowledge arose, light arose. ‘This is the origin of feeling’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me... light arose. ‘This is the path leading to the origin of feeling’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me... light arose. ‘This is the cessation of feeling’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me... light arose. ‘This is the path leading to the cessation of feeling’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me... light arose. ‘This is the gratification in feeling’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before... ‘This is the danger in feeling’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before... ‘This is the escape from feeling’—thus, bhikkhus, with regard to things unheard before, vision arose in me, knowledge arose, wisdom arose, true knowledge arose, light arose." The Fifth.
