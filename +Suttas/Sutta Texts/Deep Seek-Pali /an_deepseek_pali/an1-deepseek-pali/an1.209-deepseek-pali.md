308.Monks, one individual, arising in the world, arises for the harm and unhappiness of many people, for the detriment, harm, and suffering of many people, both gods and humans. Which one individual? He is one with wrong view, with distorted vision. He draws many people away from the true Dhamma and establishes them in false Dhamma. This, monks, is the one individual who, arising in the world, arises for the harm and unhappiness of many people, for the detriment, harm, and suffering of many people, both gods and humans.
