278.It is impossible, monks, it cannot happen that in one world-system two wheel-turning monarchs would arise, one before the appearance of the wheel-gem and one after its disappearance. That is not possible. But it is possible, monks, that in one world-system one wheel-turning monarch would arise. That is possible.
