170.Monks, a single person, when arising in the world, arises for the welfare of the many, for the happiness of the many, out of compassion for the world, for the good, welfare, and happiness of devas and humans. Who is that single person? The Tathagata, the Arahant, the Perfectly Self-Enlightened One. This, monks, is that single person who, when arising in the world, arises for the welfare of the many, for the happiness of the many, out of compassion for the world, for the good, welfare, and happiness of devas and humans.
