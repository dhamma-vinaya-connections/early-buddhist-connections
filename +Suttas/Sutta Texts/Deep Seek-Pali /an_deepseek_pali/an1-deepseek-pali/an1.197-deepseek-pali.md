290.It is impossible, monks, it cannot happen that one endowed with bodily misconduct would, on that account and as its result, on the dissolution of the body, after death, be reborn in a good destination, a heavenly world. That is not possible. But it is possible, monks, that one endowed with bodily misconduct would, on that account and as its result, on the dissolution of the body, after death, be reborn in a state of deprivation, an unhappy destination, the lower realms, hell. That is possible.
