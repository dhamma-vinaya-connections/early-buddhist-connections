"And what, monks, is the power of cultivation? Here, monks, a monk develops the enlightenment factor of mindfulness, which is based on seclusion, dispassion, and cessation, culminating in relinquishment. He develops the enlightenment factor of investigation of phenomena... the enlightenment factor of energy... the enlightenment factor of rapture... the enlightenment factor of tranquility... the enlightenment factor of concentration... the enlightenment factor of equanimity, which is based on seclusion, dispassion, and cessation, culminating in relinquishment. This, monks, is called the power of cultivation. These, monks, are the two powers."
