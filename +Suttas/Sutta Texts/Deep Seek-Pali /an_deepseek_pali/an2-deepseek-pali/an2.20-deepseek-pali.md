# AN 2.20

20. Bhikkhus, these two factors lead to the confusion and disappearance of the true Dhamma. Which two? Poorly arranged phrasing, and poorly interpreted meaning. When the phrasing is poorly arranged, Bhikkhus, the meaning is also difficult to understand. These two factors, Bhikkhus, lead to the confusion and disappearance of the true Dhamma.
