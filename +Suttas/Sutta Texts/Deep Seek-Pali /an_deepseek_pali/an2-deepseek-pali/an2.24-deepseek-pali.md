# AN 2.24

24. Monks, these two slander the TathÄgata. Which two? One who presents what was not spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata as spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata, and one who presents what was spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata as not spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata. These two, monks, slander the TathÄgata. Monks, these two do not slander the TathÄgata. Which two? One who presents what was not spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata as not spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata, and one who presents what was spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata as spoken or uttered by the TathÄgata. These two, monks, do not slander the TathÄgata.
