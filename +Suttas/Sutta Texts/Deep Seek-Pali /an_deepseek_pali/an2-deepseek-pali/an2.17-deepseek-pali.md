"Here, brahmin, someone has done bodily misconduct and not done bodily good conduct; has done verbal misconduct and not done verbal good conduct; has done mental misconduct and not done mental good conduct. Thus, brahmin, because of deeds done and deeds not done, some beings here, on the dissolution of the body, after death, are reborn in the plane of misery, in a bad destination, in the lower realms, in hell.
