# AN 2.31

31. Monks, seeing two benefits, I frequent remote lodgings in forests and woodlands. What are the two? Seeing my own abiding in ease in the present, and having compassion for the later generation. Monks, it is seeing these two benefits that I frequent remote lodgings in forests and woodlands.
