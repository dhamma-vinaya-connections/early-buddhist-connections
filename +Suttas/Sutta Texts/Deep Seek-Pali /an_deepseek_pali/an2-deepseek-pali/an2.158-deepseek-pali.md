# AN 2.158

158."Monks, there are these two kinds of hospitality. Which two? Hospitality with material things and hospitality with the Dhamma. These, monks, are the two kinds of hospitality. Of these two kinds of hospitality, hospitality with the Dhamma is the foremost."
