# AN 2.76

76."Monks, there are these two kinds of happiness. What two? Happiness with delight as its object and happiness with equanimity as its object. These, monks, are the two kinds of happiness. The foremost of these two kinds of happiness, monks, is happiness with equanimity as its object."
