# AN 2.21

21. Bhikkhus, these two factors lead to the stability, non-confusion, and non-disappearance of the true Dhamma. Which two? Well-arranged phrasing, and well-interpreted meaning. When the phrasing is well-arranged, Bhikkhus, the meaning is also well-understood. These two factors, Bhikkhus, lead to the stability, non-confusion, and non-disappearance of the true Dhamma.
