# AN 2.66

66."Monks, there are these two kinds of happiness. What are the two? The happiness of sensual pleasure and the happiness of renunciation. These, monks, are the two kinds of happiness. The foremost of these two kinds of happiness, monks, is the happiness of renunciation."
