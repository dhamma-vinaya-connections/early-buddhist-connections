# AN 2.22

22. Monks, there are these two fools. Which two? One who does not see a transgression as a transgression, and one who does not accept a transgression as it should be when it is explained to him. These, monks, are the two fools. There are these two wise ones. Which two? One who sees a transgression as a transgression, and one who accepts a transgression as it should be when it is explained to him. These, monks, are the two wise ones.
