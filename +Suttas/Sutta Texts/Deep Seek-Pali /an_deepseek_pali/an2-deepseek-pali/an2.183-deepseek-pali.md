# AN 2.183

183."Monks, one endowed with two qualities lives in suffering. Which two? Anger and resentment... denigration and domineering... envy and stinginess... deceit and trickery... shamelessness and lack of moral dread. Endowed with these two qualities, monks, one lives in suffering."
