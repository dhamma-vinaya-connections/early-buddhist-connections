# AN 2.40

40."Monks, at the time when robbers are strong, kings are weak. At that time, the king finds no ease in going out or returning, or in inspecting the border regions. Even brahmins and householders find no ease in going out or returning, or in overseeing their external affairs. Just so, monks, at the time when bad monks are strong, virtuous monks are weak. At that time, the virtuous monks remain silent in the midst of the Saá¹…gha, sitting as if ruminating, or they dwell in border regions. This, monks, is for the harm of many people, for the suffering of many people, for the disadvantage, harm, and suffering of gods and humans."

"At the time, monks, when kings are powerful, thieves are weak. At that time, monks, the king finds it easy to travel or go out, or to investigate the border regions. Even brahmins and householders find it easy to travel or go out, or to inspect their external affairs. Just so, monks, at the time when virtuous monks are powerful, wicked monks are weak. At that time, monks, the wicked monks remain silent, shrinking in the midst of the Saá¹…gha, or else they depart by whatever way (they can). This, monks, is for the welfare and happiness of many people, for the benefit, welfare, and happiness of gods and humans."
