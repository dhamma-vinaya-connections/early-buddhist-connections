# AN 2.151

151."Monks, there are these two kinds of compassion. Which two? Compassion through material things and compassion through the Dhamma. These, monks, are the two kinds of compassion. Of these two kinds of compassion, compassion through the Dhamma is the foremost."

DÄnavaggo tatiyo.
