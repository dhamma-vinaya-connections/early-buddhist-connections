# AN 2.87

87."Monks, unwholesome states arise having the conditioned as object, not the unconditioned. By abandoning that very conditioned, those unwholesome states do not come to be."

Sanimittavaggo tatiyo.

The Chapter on Signs, the Third.

(9) 4. Dhammavaggo

(9) 4. The Chapter on the Dhamma
