# AN 2.163

163."Monks, there are these two kinds of expansion. Which two? Expansion in material things and expansion in the Dhamma. These, monks, are the two kinds of expansion. Of these two kinds of expansion, expansion in the Dhamma is the foremost."

SanthÄravaggo catuttho.

The fourth chapter is "The Chapter on Sharing."
