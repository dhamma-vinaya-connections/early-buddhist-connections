# AN 2.77

77."Monks, there are these two kinds of happiness. Which two? Happiness based on form and happiness based on the formless. These, monks, are the two kinds of happiness. Of these two kinds of happiness, that happiness based on the formless is the foremost."

Sukhavaggo dutiyo.

The Chapter on Happiness, the Second.

(8) 3. Sanimittavaggo

(8) 3. The Chapter on Signs
