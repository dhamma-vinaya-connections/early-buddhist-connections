102.Monks, seeing six advantages, it is enough for a bhikkhu to establish the perception of impermanence regarding all formations, having made no reservation. What six? 'All formations will appear to me as unestablished. My mind will find no delight in the entire world. My mind will turn away from the entire world. My mind will incline toward NibbÄna. My fetters will go to abandonment. I will be endowed with supreme recluseship.' Seeing these six advantages, it is enough for a bhikkhu to establish the perception of impermanence regarding all formations, having made no reservation. The Seventh Discourse.
