97.Monks, there are these six advantages in realizing the fruit of stream-entry. What six? One is assured of the True Dhamma; one is not subject to decline; one's suffering is brought to an end; one is endowed with knowledge not shared by others; one has clearly seen the cause; and the phenomena arisen from the cause are well seen. These, monks, are the six advantages in realizing the fruit of stream-entry. The Second Discourse.
