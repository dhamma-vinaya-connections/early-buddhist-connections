114."Monks, there are these three qualities. What three? Discontent, lack of thorough understanding, and great ambition. These, monks, are the three qualities. For the abandoning of these three qualities, three qualities should be cultivated. What three? For the abandoning of discontent, contentment should be cultivated. For the abandoning of lack of thorough understanding, thorough understanding should be cultivated. For the abandoning of great ambition, having few desires should be cultivated. These, monks, are the three qualities that should be cultivated for the abandoning of those three qualities." The eighth.
