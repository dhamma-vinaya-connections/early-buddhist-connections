112."Monks, there are these three qualities. What three? The view of gratification, the view of self, and wrong view. These, monks, are the three qualities. For the abandonment of these three qualities, three qualities should be cultivated. What three? For the abandonment of the view of gratification, the perception of impermanence should be cultivated. For the abandonment of the view of self, the perception of non-self should be cultivated. For the abandonment of wrong view, right view should be cultivated. For the abandonment of those three qualities, these three qualities should be cultivated." The sixth.
