'The perception of disenchantment towards all conditioned phenomena will be present in me, just as if facing an executioner with a raised sword. My mind will withdraw from the entire world, and I will be a constant seer of peace in NibbÄna. My underlying tendencies will go to utter destruction. I will be diligent in my duties. And I will have properly attended to the Teacher with loving-kindness.' Monks, considering these six benefits, it is quite enough for a monk to establish the perception of suffering in all conditioned phenomena, without any limitations." The eighth.
