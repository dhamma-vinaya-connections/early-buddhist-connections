# AN 6.68 Saṅgaṇikārāmasuttaṃ

## AN 6.68 The Discourse on Delight in Company

68. ‘‘‘So vata, bhikkhave, bhikkhu saṅgaṇikārāmo saṅgaṇikarato saṅgaṇikārāmataṃ anuyutto, gaṇārāmo gaṇarato gaṇārāmataṃ anuyutto, eko paviveke abhiramissatī’ti netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. ‘Eko paviveke anabhiramanto cittassa nimittaṃ gahessatī’ti netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. ‘Cittassa nimittaṃ agaṇhanto sammādiṭṭhiṃ paripūressatī’ti netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. ‘Sammādiṭṭhiṃ aparipūretvā sammāsamādhiṃ paripūressatī’ti netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. ‘Sammāsamādhiṃ aparipūretvā saṃyojanāni pajahissatī’ti netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati. ‘Saṃyojanāni appahāya nibbānaṃ sacchikarissatī’ti netaṃ ṭhānaṃ vijjati.

68.“Monks, it is impossible that a monk who delights in company, is devoted to company, delights in groups, and is devoted to groups, will find joy in solitude. Not finding joy in solitude, it is impossible that he will grasp the mental sign. Not grasping the mental sign, it is impossible that he will fulfill right view. Not fulfilling right view, it is impossible that he will fulfill right samādhi. Not fulfilling right samādhi, it is impossible that he will abandon the fetters. Not abandoning the fetters, it is impossible that he will realize Nibbāna.”

‘‘‘So vata, bhikkhave, bhikkhu na saṅgaṇikārāmo na saṅgaṇikarato na saṅgaṇikārāmataṃ anuyutto, na gaṇārāmo na gaṇarato na gaṇārāmataṃ anuyutto, eko paviveke abhiramissatī’ti ṭhānametaṃ vijjati. ‘Eko paviveke abhiramanto cittassa nimittaṃ gahessatī’ti ṭhānametaṃ vijjati. ‘Cittassa nimittaṃ gaṇhanto sammādiṭṭhiṃ paripūressatī’ti ṭhānametaṃ vijjati. ‘Sammādiṭṭhiṃ paripūretvā sammāsamādhiṃ paripūressatī’ti ṭhānametaṃ vijjati. ‘Sammāsamādhiṃ paripūretvā saṃyojanāni pajahissatī’ti ṭhānametaṃ vijjati. ‘Saṃyojanāni pahāya nibbānaṃ sacchikarissatī’ti ṭhānametaṃ vijjatī’’ti. Catutthaṃ.

“Monks, it is possible that a monk who does not delight in company, is not devoted to company, does not delight in groups, and is not devoted to groups, will find joy in solitude. Finding joy in solitude, it is possible that he will grasp the mental sign. Grasping the mental sign, it is possible that he will fulfill right view. Fulfilling right view, it is possible that he will fulfill right samādhi. Fulfilling right samādhi, it is possible that he will abandon the fetters. Abandoning the fetters, it is possible that he will realize Nibbāna.” This is the fourth.
