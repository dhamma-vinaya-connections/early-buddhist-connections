18. "If, monks, wanderers of other sects were to ask you thus: 'Friends, do you live the holy life under the ascetic Gotama for the sake of attaining rebirth in the heavens?' Wouldn't you, monks, being asked thus, feel troubled, ashamed, and disgusted?" "Yes, venerable sir." "So it seems, monks, that you are troubled, ashamed, and disgusted by divine longevity, divine beauty, divine happiness, divine fame, and divine sovereignty. How much more, then, monks, should you be troubled, ashamed, and disgusted by bodily misconduct, by verbal misconduct... by mental misconduct." The eighth.
