33."Bhikkhus, there are these eight grounds for giving. Which eight? One gives a gift out of desire. One gives a gift out of hatred. One gives a gift out of delusion. One gives a gift out of fear. One gives a gift thinking, 'Giving was done before; it was done by my father and grandfather; I am not fit to diminish the ancient family lineage.' One gives a gift thinking, 'Having given this gift, at the breaking up of the body, after death, I will be reborn in a good destination, in a heavenly world.' One gives a gift thinking, 'When I give this gift, my mind is pleased, gladness and happiness arise.' One gives a gift for the sake of the adornment and equipment of the mind. These, bhikkhus, are the eight grounds for giving." The third.
