31."Bhikkhus, there are these eight kinds of giving. Which eight? One gives a gift upon approach; one gives a gift out of fear; one gives a gift thinking, 'He gave to me'; one gives a gift thinking, 'He will give to me'; one gives a gift thinking, 'Giving is good'; one gives a gift thinking, 'I cook, these do not cook; it is not right for me, who cooks, not to give to those who do not cook'; one gives a gift thinking, 'By giving this gift, a good reputation will arise for me'; one gives a gift for the sake of the mind's adornment and equipment. These, bhikkhus, are the eight kinds of giving." First.
