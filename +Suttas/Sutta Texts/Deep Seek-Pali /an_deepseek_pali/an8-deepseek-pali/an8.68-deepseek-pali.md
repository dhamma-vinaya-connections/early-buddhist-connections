68."Monks, there are these eight noble ways of speaking. Which eight? Declaring as unseen what was not seen; declaring as unheard what was not heard; declaring as unsensed what was not sensed; declaring as unknown what was not known; declaring as seen what was seen; declaring as heard what was heard; declaring as sensed what was sensed; declaring as known what was known. These, monks, are the eight noble ways of speaking." The eighth.
