65."Monks, a monk endowed with five qualities is fit for discussion among his fellow practitioners. What five? Here, monks, a monk is himself accomplished in virtue and is able to answer a question that has arisen concerning accomplishment in virtue; he is himself accomplished in concentration and is able to answer a question that has arisen concerning accomplishment in concentration; he is himself accomplished in wisdom and is able to answer a question that has arisen concerning accomplishment in wisdom; he is himself accomplished in liberation and is able to answer a question that has arisen concerning accomplishment in liberation; he is himself accomplished in the knowledge and vision of liberation and is able to answer a question that has arisen concerning accomplishment in the knowledge and vision of liberation. Monks, a monk endowed with these five qualities is fit for discussion among his fellow practitioners."
