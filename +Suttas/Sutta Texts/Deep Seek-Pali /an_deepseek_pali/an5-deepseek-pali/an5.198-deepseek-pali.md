198.Bhikkhus, speech endowed with five factors is well spoken, not poorly spoken; it is blameless and irreproachable by the wise. What are the five? It is spoken at the right time; it is spoken truthfully; it is spoken gently; it is spoken connected with the goal; and it is spoken with a mind of loving-kindness. Bhikkhus, speech endowed with these five factors is well spoken, not poorly spoken; it is blameless and irreproachable by the wise.
