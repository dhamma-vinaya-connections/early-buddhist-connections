# AN 7.90 Vedagūsuttaṃ

## AN 7.90 The Discourse on the Knowledge-Master

90. Viditattā vedagū hoti…pe…. Chaṭṭhaṃ.

90."Monks, it is through having known (the Vedas) that one is a knowledge-master..." The sixth.
