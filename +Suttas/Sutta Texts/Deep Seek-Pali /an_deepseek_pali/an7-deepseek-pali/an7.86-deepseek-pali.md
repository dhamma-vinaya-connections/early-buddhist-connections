# AN 7.86 Samaṇasuttaṃ

## AN 7.86 The Discourse on Ascetics

86. Sattannaṃ, bhikkhave, dhammānaṃ samitattā samaṇo hoti…pe…. Dutiyaṃ.

86."Monks, it is through the pacification of seven things that one is an ascetic..." The second.
