# AN 7.89 Nhātakasuttaṃ

## AN 7.89 The Discourse on the Purified One

89. Nhātattā nhātako [nahātatto nahātako (sī. syā.)] hoti…pe…. Pañcamaṃ.

89."Monks, it is through having bathed (internally) that one is a purified one..." The fifth.
