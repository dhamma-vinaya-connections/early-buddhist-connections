# AN 7.87 Brāhmaṇasuttaṃ

## AN 7.87 The Discourse on the Brahmin

87. Bāhitattā brāhmaṇo hoti…pe…. Tatiyaṃ.

87."Monks, it is through having expelled (evil) that one is a brahmin..." The third.
