# AN 7.88 Sottiyasuttaṃ

## AN 7.88 The Discourse on the Sottiya

88. Nissutattā [nissuttattā (syā.) ma. ni. 1.434 passitabbaṃ] sottiyo [sotthiko (sī.), sottiko (syā.)] hoti…pe…. Catutthaṃ.

88."Monks, it is through having heard (much) that one is a sottiya..." The fourth.
