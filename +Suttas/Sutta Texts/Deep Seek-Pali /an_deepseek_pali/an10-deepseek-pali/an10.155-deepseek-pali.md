# AN 10.155 Sevitabbasuttaṃ

## AN 10.155 The Discourse on One to Be Associated With

155. ‘‘Dasahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo na sevitabbo. Katamehi dasahi? Micchādiṭṭhiko hoti, micchāsaṅkappo hoti, micchāvāco hoti, micchākammanto hoti, micchāājīvo hoti, micchāvāyāmo hoti, micchāsati hoti, micchāsamādhi hoti, micchāñāṇī hoti, micchāvimutti hoti – imehi kho, bhikkhave, dasahi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo na sevitabbo.

155.“Monks, a person endowed with ten qualities should not be associated with. What ten? He is of wrong view, of wrong intention, of wrong speech, of wrong action, of wrong livelihood, of wrong effort, of wrong mindfulness, of wrong samādhi, of wrong knowledge, and of wrong liberation. Monks, a person endowed with these ten qualities should not be associated with.”

‘‘Dasahi, bhikkhave, dhammehi samannāgato puggalo sevitabbo. Katamehi dasahi? Sammādiṭṭhiko hoti, sammāsaṅkappo hoti, sammāvāco hoti, sammākammanto hoti, sammāājīvo hoti, sammāvāyāmo hoti, sammāsati hoti, sammāsamādhi hoti, sammāñāṇī hoti, sammāvimutti hoti – imehi kho, bhikkhave, dasahi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo sevitabbo’’ti.

“Monks, a person endowed with ten qualities should be associated with. What ten? He is of right view, of right intention, of right speech, of right action, of right livelihood, of right effort, of right mindfulness, of right samādhi, of right knowledge, and of right liberation. Monks, a person endowed with these ten qualities should be associated with.”

"Monks, a person endowed with ten qualities should not be associated with... should be associated with... should not be attended upon... should be attended upon... is not worthy of offerings... is worthy of offerings... is not praiseworthy... is praiseworthy... is disrespectful... is respectful... is without deference... is with deference... does not achieve their goal... achieves their goal... does not purify themselves... purifies themselves... does not overcome conceit... overcomes conceit... does not grow in wisdom... grows in wisdom..."

‘‘Bahuṃ apuññaṃ pasavati… bahuṃ puññaṃ pasavati. Katamehi dasahi? Sammādiṭṭhiko hoti, sammāsaṅkappo hoti, sammāvāco hoti, sammākammanto hoti, sammāājīvo hoti, sammāvāyāmo hoti, sammāsati hoti, sammāsamādhi hoti, sammāñāṇī hoti, sammāvimutti hoti – imehi kho, bhikkhave, dasahi dhammehi samannāgato puggalo bahuṃ puññaṃ pasavatī’’ti.

"Monks, a person endowed with ten qualities generates much demerit... generates much merit. What are the ten? They have right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samādhi, right knowledge, and right liberation. Monks, a person endowed with these ten qualities generates much merit."
