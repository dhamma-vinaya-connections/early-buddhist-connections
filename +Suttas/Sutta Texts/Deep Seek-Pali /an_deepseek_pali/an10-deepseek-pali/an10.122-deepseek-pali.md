# AN 10.122 Āsavakkhayasuttaṃ

## AN 10.122 The Discourse on the Destruction of the Taints

122. ‘‘Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā āsavānaṃ khayāya saṃvattanti. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi, sammāñāṇaṃ, sammāvimutti – ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā bhāvitā bahulīkatā āsavānaṃ khayāya saṃvattantī’’ti. Dasamaṃ.

122."Monks, these ten states, when developed and cultivated, lead to the destruction of the taints. Which ten? Right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samādhi, right knowledge, and right liberation. These, monks, are the ten states that, when developed and cultivated, lead to the destruction of the taints." Tenth.

Paccorohaṇivaggo dutiyo.

The Second Chapter on Declining

Tassuddānaṃ –

The summary of it is:

Tayo adhammā ajito, saṅgāravo ca orimaṃ;Dve ceva paccorohaṇī, pubbaṅgamaṃ āsavakkhayoti.

Three unrighteous acts, Ajita, Saṅgārava, and Orima; two on declining, the forerunner, and the destruction of the taints.
