# AN 10.133 Ekādasamasuttaṃ

## AN 10.133 The Eleventh Discourse

133. ‘‘Dasayime, bhikkhave, sammattā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi, sammāñāṇaṃ, sammāvimutti – ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa sammattā’’ti. Ekādasamaṃ.

133.“Bhikkhus, these ten are right courses. What ten? Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samādhi, right knowledge, right liberation. These, bhikkhus, are the ten right courses.”

Parisuddhavaggo tatiyo.

The Chapter on Purity, the Third.

(14) 4. Sādhuvaggo

(14) 4. The Chapter on the Wholesome
