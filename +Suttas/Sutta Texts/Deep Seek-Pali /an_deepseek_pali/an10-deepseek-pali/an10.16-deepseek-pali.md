16.Bhikkhus, these ten individuals are worthy of offerings, worthy of hospitality, worthy of gifts, worthy of reverential salutation, an unsurpassed field of merit for the world. Which ten? The Tathagata, the Arahant, the Perfectly Self-Enlightened One; a Paccekabuddha; one liberated both ways; one liberated by wisdom; one who is a body-witness; one attained to view; one liberated by faith; one who follows faith; one who follows the Dhamma; a lineage-member. These, bhikkhus, are the ten individuals worthy of offerings, worthy of hospitality, worthy of gifts, worthy of reverential salutation, an unsurpassed field of merit for the world. The sixth.
