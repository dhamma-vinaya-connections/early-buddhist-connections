# AN 10.70 Dutiyakathāvatthusuttaṃ

## AN 10.70 The Second Discourse on Subjects for Discussion

70. Ekaṃ samayaṃ bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena sambahulā bhikkhū pacchābhattaṃ piṇḍapātapaṭikkantā upaṭṭhānasālāyaṃ sannisinnā sannipatitā anekavihitaṃ tiracchānakathaṃ anuyuttā viharanti, seyyathidaṃ – rājakathaṃ corakathaṃ mahāmattakathaṃ…pe… itibhavābhavakathaṃ iti vāti.

70.At one time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Park. Now on that occasion, a number of bhikkhus, after their meal, on returning from their almsround, had gathered and were sitting together in the assembly hall, engaged in various kinds of animal talk, that is: talk about kings, robbers, great ministers …pe… and talk about becoming this or that.

‘‘Dasayimāni, bhikkhave, pāsaṃsāni ṭhānāni. Katamāni dasa? Idha, bhikkhave, bhikkhu attanā ca appiccho hoti, appicchakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Appiccho bhikkhu appicchakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“Bhikkhus, there are these ten grounds for praise. What ten? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu is himself of few wishes and speaks to the other bhikkhus on fewness of wishes. ‘A bhikkhu is of few wishes and speaks to the other bhikkhus on fewness of wishes’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca santuṭṭho hoti, santuṭṭhikathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Santuṭṭho bhikkhu santuṭṭhikathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He is himself content and speaks to the other bhikkhus on contentment. ‘A bhikkhu is content and speaks to the other bhikkhus on contentment’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca pavivitto hoti, pavivekakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Pavivitto bhikkhu pavivekakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He is himself secluded and speaks to the other bhikkhus on seclusion. ‘A bhikkhu is secluded and speaks to the other bhikkhus on seclusion’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca asaṃsaṭṭho hoti, asaṃsaṭṭhakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Asaṃsaṭṭho bhikkhu asaṃsaṭṭhakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He is himself unentangled and speaks to the other bhikkhus on non-entanglement. ‘A bhikkhu is unentangled and speaks to the other bhikkhus on non-entanglement’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca āraddhavīriyo hoti, vīriyārambhakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Āraddhavīriyo bhikkhu vīriyārambhakathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He has himself aroused his energy and speaks to the other bhikkhus on arousing energy. ‘A bhikkhu has aroused his energy and speaks to the other bhikkhus on arousing energy’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca sīlasampanno hoti, sīlasampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Sīlasampanno bhikkhu sīlasampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He is himself accomplished in virtue and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of virtue. ‘A bhikkhu is accomplished in virtue and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of virtue’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca samādhisampanno hoti, samādhisampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Samādhisampanno bhikkhu samādhisampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He is himself accomplished in samādhi and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of samādhi. ‘A bhikkhu is accomplished in samādhi and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of samādhi’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca paññāsampanno hoti, paññāsampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Paññāsampanno bhikkhu paññāsampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He is himself accomplished in wisdom and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of wisdom. ‘A bhikkhu is accomplished in wisdom and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of wisdom’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca vimuttisampanno hoti, vimuttisampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Vimuttisampanno bhikkhu vimuttisampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ.

“He is himself accomplished in liberation and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of liberation. ‘A bhikkhu is accomplished in liberation and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of liberation’: this is a ground for praise.”

‘‘Attanā ca vimuttiñāṇadassanasampanno hoti, vimuttiñāṇadassanasampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā hoti. ‘Vimuttiñāṇadassanasampanno bhikkhu vimuttiñāṇadassanasampadākathañca bhikkhūnaṃ kattā’ti pāsaṃsametaṃ ṭhānaṃ. Imāni kho, bhikkhave, dasa pāsaṃsāni ṭhānānī’’ti. Dasamaṃ.

“He is himself accomplished in the knowledge and vision of liberation and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of the knowledge and vision of liberation. ‘A bhikkhu is accomplished in the knowledge and vision of liberation and speaks to the other bhikkhus on the accomplishment of the knowledge and vision of liberation’: this is a ground for praise. These, bhikkhus, are the ten grounds for praise.” Tenth.

Yamakavaggo dutiyo.

The Chapter on Pairs, the Second.

Tassuddānaṃ –

The summary:

Avijjā taṇhā niṭṭhā ca, avecca dve sukhāni ca;Naḷakapāne dve vuttā, kathāvatthūpare duveti.

Ignorance, craving, and conclusion, unwavering confidence and two on happiness; two taught at Naḷakapāna, and two more on subjects for discussion.

(8) 3. Ākaṅkhavaggo
