33."Venerable sir, with how many qualities must a bhikkhu be endowed to be appointed to a commission of inquiry?" "UpÄli, a bhikkhu must be endowed with ten qualities to be appointed to a commission of inquiry. What ten? Here, UpÄli, a bhikkhu is virtuous; he dwells restrained by the restraint of the PÄtimokkha, accomplished in good conduct and resort, seeing danger in the slightest faults, and trains himself by undertaking the training rules.
