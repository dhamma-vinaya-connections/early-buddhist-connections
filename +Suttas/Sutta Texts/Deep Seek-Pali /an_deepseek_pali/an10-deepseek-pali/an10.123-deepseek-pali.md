# AN 10.123 Paṭhamasuttaṃ

## AN 10.123 The First Discourse

123. ‘‘Dasayime, bhikkhave, dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā, nāññatra sugatavinayā. Katame dasa? Sammādiṭṭhi, sammāsaṅkappo, sammāvācā, sammākammanto, sammāājīvo, sammāvāyāmo, sammāsati, sammāsamādhi, sammāñāṇaṃ, sammāvimutti – ime kho, bhikkhave, dasa dhammā parisuddhā pariyodātā, nāññatra sugatavinayā’’ti. Paṭhamaṃ.

123."Monks, these ten states are perfectly pure and bright, not found elsewhere than in the discipline of the Well Gone One. Which ten? Right view, right intention, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right samādhi, right knowledge, and right liberation. These, monks, are the ten states perfectly pure and bright, not found elsewhere than in the discipline of the Well Gone One." First.
