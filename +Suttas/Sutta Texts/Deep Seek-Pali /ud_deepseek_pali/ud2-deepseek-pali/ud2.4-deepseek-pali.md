Then many monks approached the Blessed One. Having approached, they paid homage to the Blessed One and sat down to one side. Seated to one side, those monks said to the Blessed One: "Now, venerable sir, the Blessed One is honored, respected, revered, venerated, esteemed, and obtains robes, almsfood, lodgings, and medicinal requisites.
