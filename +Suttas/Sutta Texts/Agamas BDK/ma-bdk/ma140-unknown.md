It is the same, I say, with ignorant fools [very loosely] keeping the precepts of a renunciant; for those [people] are serving their desires. Negligent, lacking right mindfulness, without right concentration, with their minds deranged by stupidity and their faculties distracted, they keep the precepts very loosely.
