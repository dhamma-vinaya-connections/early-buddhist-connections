This is how the path is attained through a concentrated mind, not through a distracted mind. How is the path attained through wisdom, not through delusion? This means that a monk dwells in developing wisdom, contemplating the rise and fall of dharmas, attaining knowledge like this, noble wisdom that is brightly penetrating, discriminative understanding, for the true cessation of dukkha. This is how the path is attained through wisdom, not through delusion.
