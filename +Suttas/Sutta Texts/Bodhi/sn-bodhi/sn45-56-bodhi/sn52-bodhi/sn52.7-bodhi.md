# SN52.7 The Destruction of Craving

At Sāvatthī. There the Venerable Anuruddha addressed the bhikkhus thus: “Friends, bhikkhus!”

“Friend!” those bhikkhus replied. The Venerable Anuruddha said this:

“Friends, these four establishments of mindfulness, when developed and cultivated, lead to the destruction of craving. What four? Here, friends, a bhikkhu ![[Satipatthanas]]. These four establishments of mindfulness, when developed and cultivated, lead to the destruction of craving.”
