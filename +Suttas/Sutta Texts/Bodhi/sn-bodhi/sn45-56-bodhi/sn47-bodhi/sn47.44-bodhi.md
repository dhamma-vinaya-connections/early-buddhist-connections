# SN47.44 Mindful

“Bhikkhus, a bhikkhu should dwell mindful. This is our instruction to you.

“And how, bhikkhus, is a bhikkhu mindful? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu ![[Satipatthanas]]. He ![[Satipatthanas]]. It is in this way, bhikkhus, that a bhikkhu is mindful.

  
  

“Bhikkhus, a bhikkhu should dwell mindful. This is our instruction to you.”
