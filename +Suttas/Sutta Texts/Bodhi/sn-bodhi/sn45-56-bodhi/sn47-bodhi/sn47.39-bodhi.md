# SN47.39 Development

“Bhikkhus, I will teach you the development of the four establishments of mindfulness. Listen to that….

“What, bhikkhus, is the development of the four establishments of mindfulness? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu ![[Satipatthanas]]. He ![[Satipatthanas]]. This, bhikkhus, is the development of the four establishments of mindfulness.”
