# SN47.24 Simple Version

At Sāvatthī. “Bhikkhus, there are these four establishments of mindfulness. What four? Here, bhikkhus, a bhikkhu ![[Satipatthanas]]. \[174\] He ![[Satipatthanas]]. These are the four establishments of mindfulness.”
