# AN6.20 Mindfulness of Death (2)

> **Division:** II. CORDIALITY

â€œBhikkhus, mindfulness of death, when developed and cultivated, is of great fruit and benefit, culminating in the deathless, having the deathless as its consummation. And how is this so?

â€œHere, bhikkhus, when day has receded and night has approached, a bhikkhu reflects thus: â€˜I could die on account of many causes.
