# AN6.72 Strength

> **Division:** II. NON-RETURNER

â€œBhikkhus, possessing six qualities a bhikkhu is incapable of attaining strength in concentration. What six? (1) Here, a bhikkhu is not skilled in the attainment of concentration; (2) he is not skilled in the duration of concentration; (3) he is not skilled in emergence from concentration; (4) he does not practice carefully; (5) he does not practice persistently; and (6) he does not do what is suitable. Possessing these six qualities, a bhikkhu is incapable of attaining strength in concentration.
