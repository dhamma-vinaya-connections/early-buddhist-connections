# AN5.224 Miserly

> **Division:** III. LENGTHY WANDERING

One becomes miserly with dwelling places, miserly with families, miserly with gains, miserly with praise, and miserly with the Dhamma. These are the five dangers in residing too long \[in the same place\].

â€œBhikkhus, there are these five benefits in residing for a balanced period \[in the same place\]. What five?
