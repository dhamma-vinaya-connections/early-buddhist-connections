# AN5.239 Miserliness (1)

> **Division:** IV. RESIDENT

(3) He is miserly with regard to gains. (4) He is miserly with regard to praise. (5) He squanders what has been given out of faith. Possessing these five qualities, a resident bhikkhu is deposited in hell as if brought there.
