# AN5.237 One Who Deserves Dispraise (2)

> **Division:** IV. RESIDENT

â€œBhikkhus, possessing five qualities, a resident bhikkhu is deposited in hell as if brought there. What five? (1) Without investigating and scrutinizing, he speaks praise of one who deserves dispraise. (2) Without investigating and scrutinizing, he speaks dispraise of one who deserves praise. (3) He is miserly and greedy with regard to dwellings. (4) He is miserly and greedy with regard to families. (5) He squanders what has been given out of faith. Possessing these five qualities, a resident bhikkhu is deposited in hell as if brought there.
