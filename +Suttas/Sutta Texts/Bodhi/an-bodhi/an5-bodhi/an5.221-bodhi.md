# AN5.221 Lengthy Wandering (1)

> **Division:** III. LENGTHY WANDERING

â€œBhikkhus, there are these five dangers for one who engages in lengthy and unsettled wandering. What five? One does not hear what one has not heard; one does not clarify what one has heard; one is not confident about the portion that one has heard; one contracts a severe illness; and one has no friends. These are the five dangers for one who engages in lengthy and unsettled wandering.
