# AN5.286 A Bhikkhu

> **Division:** II. TRAINING RULES REPETITION SERIES

â€œBhikkhus, possessing five qualities, a bhikkhu is deposited in hell as if brought there. What five? He is one who destroys life, takes what is not given, does not observe celibacy, speaks falsely, and indulges in liquor, wine, and intoxicants, the basis for heedlessness. \[276\] Possessing these five qualities, a bhikkhu is deposited in hell as if brought there.
