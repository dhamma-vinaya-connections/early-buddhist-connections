# AN5.222 Lengthy Wandering (2)

> **Division:** III. LENGTHY WANDERING

One does not achieve what one has not yet achieved; one falls away from what one has already achieved; one is timid about some things one has achieved; one contracts a severe illness; and one has no friends. These are the five dangers for one who engages in lengthy and unsettled wandering.
