# AN8.4 Pleasing (2)

> **Division:** I. LOVING-KINDNESS

“Bhikkhus, possessing eight qualities, a bhikkhu is displeasing and disagreeable to his fellow bhikkhus and is neither respected nor esteemed by them. What eight? Here, a bhikkhu is (1) desirous of gains, (2) honor, and (3) reputation; (4) he does not know the proper time and (5) does not know moderation; (6) he is impure; (7) he speaks much; and (8) he insults and reviles his fellow bhikkhus. Possessing these eight qualities, a bhikkhu is displeasing and disagreeable to his fellow bhikkhus and is neither respected nor esteemed by them.

“Bhikkhus, possessing eight qualities, a bhikkhu is pleasing and agreeable to his fellow bhikkhus and is respected and esteemed by them. What eight? Here, a bhikkhu is (1) not desirous of gains, (2) honor, and (3) reputation; (4) he is one who knows the proper time and (5) who knows moderation; (6) he is pure; (7) he does not speak much; and (8) he does not insult and revile his fellow bhikkhus. Possessing these eight qualities, a bhikkhu is pleasing and agreeable to his fellow bhikkhus and is respected and esteemed by them.”
