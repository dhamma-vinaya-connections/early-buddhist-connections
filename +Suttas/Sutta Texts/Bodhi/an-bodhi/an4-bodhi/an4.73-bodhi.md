(3) â€œAgain, a bad person does not disclose his own faults even when asked about them, how much less then when not asked. â€œOne who possesses these four qualities can be understood to be a bad person.

â€œBhikkhus, one who possesses \[another\] four qualities can be understood to be a good person. What four?

â€œOne who possesses these four qualities can be understood as a good person.
