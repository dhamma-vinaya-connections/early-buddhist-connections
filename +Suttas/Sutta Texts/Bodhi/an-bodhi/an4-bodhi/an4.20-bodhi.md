# AN4.20 An Assigner of Meals

> **Division:** II. WALKING

He takes a wrong course because of desire, because of hatred, because of delusion, or because of fear. If an assigner of meals possesses these four qualities, he is deposited in hell as if brought there.

â€œBhikkhus, if an assigner of meals possesses four qualities, he is deposited in heaven as if brought there. What four? He does not take a wrong course because of desire, because of hatred, because of delusion, or because of fear. If an assigner of meals possesses these four qualities, he is deposited in heaven as if brought there.â€

Those people uncontrolled in sensual pleasures,

who are unrighteous, not revering the Dhamma,

gone \[astray\] through desire, hate, and fear

are called a stained assembly.

Such is said by the Ascetic who knows.

Therefore those good persons who are praiseworthy,

firm in the Dhamma, who do nothing bad,

unswayed by desire, hate, and fear,

are called an elite assembly.

Such is said by the Ascetic who knows. \[20\]
