# AN7.49 Perceptions (2)

> **Division:** V. THE GREAT SACRIFICE

â€œBhikkhus, these seven perceptions, when developed and cultivated, are of great fruit and benefit, culminating in the deathless, having the deathless as their consummation. What seven?
