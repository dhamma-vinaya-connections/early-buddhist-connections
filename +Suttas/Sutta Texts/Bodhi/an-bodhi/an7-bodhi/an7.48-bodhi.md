# AN7.48 Perceptions (1)

> **Division:** V. THE GREAT SACRIFICE

The perception of unattractiveness, the perception of death, the perception of the repulsiveness of food, the perception of nondelight in the entire world, the perception of impermanence, the perception of suffering in the impermanent, and the perception of non-self in what is suffering.
