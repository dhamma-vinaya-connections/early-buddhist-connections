# SN45.171 - Oghasutta - Floods
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Noble eightfold path is to be developed for direct knowledge of these 4 things , for the full understanding of them, for their utter destruction, for their abandoning

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Flood and bond of sensuality,  existence, views, and ignorance, clinging to sensuality,  existence, rules and vows, clinging to a doctrine of self
> 
> Bodily knot of covetousness,  ill will, the of distorted grasp of rules and vows, of adherence to dogmas
