# SN52.7 - Taṇhākkhayasutta - The Destruction of Craving
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> When some bhikkhus ask anuruddha how he attained such higher knowledge, he replies it is from developing the four kinds of mindfulness meditation

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The four kinds of mindfulness meditation lead to the ending of craving
> 
> And it’s because of developing and cultivating these four kinds of mindfulness meditation that I directly know the entire galaxy
>
