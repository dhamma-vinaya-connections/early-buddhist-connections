# SN51.18 - Bhikkhusutta - A Bhikkhu
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Whatever ascetics are of great spiritual power have developed the four bases of psychic power.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> All the ascetics and brahmins in the past, future, or present who are mighty and powerful have become so by developing and cultivating the four bases of psychic power.
> 
> By developing and cultivating the four bases of psychic power, a bhikkhu realizes the undefiled freedom of heart and freedom by wisdom in this very life.
>
