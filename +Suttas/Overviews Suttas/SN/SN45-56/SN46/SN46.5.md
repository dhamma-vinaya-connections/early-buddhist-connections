# SN46.5 - Bhikkhusutta - Bhikkhu
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Here the awakening factors are described in the context of hearing the teachings and reflecting on them. This leads to full enlightenment, or at least to some lesser attainment

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> After hearing the teaching of such noble ones, a bhikkhu will live withdrawn in both body and mind, as they recollect and think about that teaching and from there the awakening factors
> 
> They’re called awakening factors because they lead to awakening
>
