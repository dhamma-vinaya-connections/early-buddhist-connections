# SN46.56 - Abhayasutta - Abhaya
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  replies that the cause of ignorance is the hindrances, and the cause of knowledge is the awakening factors

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When one dwells with a mind  overwhelmed by 5 hindrances , and one does not understand as it really is the escape from them this is a cause and condition for lack of knowledge and vision
> 
> With a mind that has developed the enlightenment factors he knows and sees things as they really are. This  is a cause for knowledge and vision
>
