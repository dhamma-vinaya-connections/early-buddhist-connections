# SN46.57-184 - Aá¹­á¹­hikamahapphalasutta - The Skeleton
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A series of passages on the benefits of meditating on a skeleton. Some editions treat the sections as separate suttas

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Developing the 7 awakening factors with these perceptions is of great benefit, can expect arahanship or non returner,  leads to great security from bondage, great sense of urgency and dwelling in comfort
> 
> Perceptions of skeleton, livid, bloated or worms infested corpse, love, compassion, rejoice, equanimity, breathing, foulness, death, repulsive of food, dissatisfaction, impermanence, suffering, non self, giving up, dispassion, cessation
