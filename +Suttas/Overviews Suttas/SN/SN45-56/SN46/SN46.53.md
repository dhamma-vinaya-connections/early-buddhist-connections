# SN46.53 - Aggisutta - Fire
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The right and wrong ways to respond to sluggishness or restlessness.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Mind becomes sluggish, it is untimely to develop serenity, samadhi nor equanimity but time for discrimination of states, energy or rapture. mindfulness, I say is always useful
> 
> When the mind becomes excited right time for serenity, samadhi or equanimity but wrong time for discrimination of states, energy nor rapture
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Fire regulation by wet or dry wood, like use of awakening factors depending on mind energy
