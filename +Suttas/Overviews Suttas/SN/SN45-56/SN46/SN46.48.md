# SN46.48 - Ä€diccasutta - The Sun
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> As the dawn precedes the sunrise, so too good friendship and proper attention  precedes the awakening factors

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When a bhikkhu is accomplished in careful attention, it is to be expected that he will develop and cultivate the seven factors of enlightenment.
> 
> When a bhikkhu has a good friend, it is to be expected that he will develop and cultivate the seven factors of enlightenment
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Dawn preceded the sun, like virtue, good friends and wise attention precedes the sun
