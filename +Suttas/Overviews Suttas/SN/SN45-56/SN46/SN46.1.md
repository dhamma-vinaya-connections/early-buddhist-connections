# SN46.1 - Himavantasutta - Himalaya
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Dragons nurture their strength in the himalayas, then enter the rivers and reach the ocean. So too, a bhikkhu nurtures ethics and then develops the seven awakening factors

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Awakening factor of mindfulness, investigation of states, energy, rapture, tranquility, samadhi, and equanimity relies on seclusion, fading away, and cessation, and ripens as letting go.
> 
> Develops and cultivates the seven awakening factors depending on and grounded on ethics
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Dragon development like bhikkhus development
