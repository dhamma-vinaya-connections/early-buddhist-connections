# SN46.52 - Pariyāyasutta - A Method of Exposition
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Some wanderers tell some buddhist bhikkhus that they, too, teach the five hindrances and the seven awakening factors, so what is the difference? The Buddha  explains by giving a detailed analytical treatment that he says is beyond the scope of the wanderers.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Method of exposition by means of which the five hindrances become ten
> 
> Method of exposition by means of which the seven factors of enlightenment become fourteen
>
