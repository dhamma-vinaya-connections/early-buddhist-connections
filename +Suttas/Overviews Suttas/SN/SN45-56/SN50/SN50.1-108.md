# SN50.1-108 - PeyyÄla - Repetition Series
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Mere instantiation of the standard repetition series as applied to the five powers. These powers are effectively identical with the five faculties, as stated at sn 48.43, being merely distinguished as a different teaching approach

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Five powers  should be developed for the direct knowledge, complete understanding, finishing, and giving up of these five higher fetters
> 
> Chapter are: ganges, diligence, hard work, searches, floods
