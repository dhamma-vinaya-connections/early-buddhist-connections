# SN48.48 - Catutthapubbārāmasutta - The Eastern Park (4)
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Having developed wisdom a bhikkhu may declare their awakening, as wisdom fulfills the other faculties

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> For a noble disciple who possesses wisdom, the faith, energy, mindfulness,  samadhi that follows from it becomes stabilized
> 
> Because these five faculties have been developed and cultivated that a bhikkhu who has destroyed the taints
>
