> [!info] Summary
> The five faculties of pleasure, pain, happiness, sadness, and equanimity are taught in a detailed exposition that treats them in an unusual order. The abandoning of each is related to the attainment of a particular meditative absorption

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> He understands the pain, sadness, pleasure, happiness and equanimity  faculty; he understands the origin, the cessation y; and where the arisen  faculty ceases without remainder
> 
> Pain ceases in 1st jhana, sadness in 2nd, pleasures ceases in 3rd jhana happiness ceases in 4th jhana and equanimity in cessation of perceptions and feelings
