# SN48.17 - TatiyavitthÄrasutta - In Detail (3)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> One who has developed the five faculties fully is a perfected one. Developing them to a lesser degree, one reaches lesser attainments

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> A difference in faculties leads to a difference in fruits
> 
> One who has none of them at all is an outsider.
