# SN47.1 - Ambapālisutta - Ambapali
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  teaches the bhikkhus about the four kinds of mindfulness meditation and the practice of situational awareness.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> ![[Satipatthanas]]
> 
> You should live, keen, aware, and mindful, rid of desire and aversion for the world.
>
