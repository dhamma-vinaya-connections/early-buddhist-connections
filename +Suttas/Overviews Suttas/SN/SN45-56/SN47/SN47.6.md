# SN47.6 - Sakuṇagghisutta - The Hawk
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The parable of the quail and the hawk. When the quail ventured outside her ancestral territory, she became vulnerable. And what is a bhikkhu’s ancestral territory? The four kinds of mindfulness meditation

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Others territories are the five kinds of sensual stimulation
> 
> You should roam inside your own territory the 4 mindfulness meditations
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Quail and hawk parable, like own and others territories
