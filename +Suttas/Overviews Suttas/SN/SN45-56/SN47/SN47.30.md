# SN47.30 - MÄnadinnasutta - Manadinna
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> SÄriputta asks anuruddha what makes someone a trainee and an adept. It is someone who has  developed the four kinds of mindfulness meditation.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Because one has partly developed the four establishments of mindfulness that one is a trainee
> 
> It is because one has completely developed these four establishments of mindfulness that one is beyond training
