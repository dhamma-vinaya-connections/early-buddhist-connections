# SN56.2 - Paṭisallānasutta - Seclusion
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> bhikkhus should develop immersion and seclusion  so as to understand the four noble truths: suffering, its origin, its cessation, and the path.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Truly understand: ‘this is suffering’ … ‘this is the origin of suffering’ … ‘this is the cessation of suffering’ … ‘this is the practice that leads to the cessation of suffering
> 
> Develop samadhi and seclusion . A bhikkhu who has them truly understands
>
