# SN56.42 - Papātasutta - The Precipice
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha takes the bhikkhus to a steep precipice, and points out that those who do not understand the four noble truths fall into a still deeper precipice

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Do not delight nor generate in volitional formations that lead to birth, aging, death and pain
> 
> Not having generated such volitional formations, they are not burnt, do not tumble down the precipice the great darkness
> 

> [!info] Similes
> cliff, inferno and darkness similes of not knowing the Noble Truths
