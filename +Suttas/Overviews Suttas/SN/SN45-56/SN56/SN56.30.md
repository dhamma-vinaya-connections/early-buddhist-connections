# SN56.30 - Gavampatisutta - Gavampati
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Some mendicants are discussing whether the four truths are necessarily seen together. Venerable Gavampati asserts that he has learned it from the Buddha that they are: if you see one, you see them all

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> does one who sees suffering also see the origin of suffering, also see the cessation of suffering, also see the way leading to the cessation of suffering?
> 
> in the presence of the Blessed One I have heard and learnt that if you see one you see them all
