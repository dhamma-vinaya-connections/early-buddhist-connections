# SN56.26 - Mittasutta - Friends
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> You should encourage your friends in the four noble truths

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> To your family and friends you should exhort, settle, and establish for making the breakthrough to the Four Noble Truths as they really are
> 
> The noble truth of suffering, the noble truth of the origin of suffering, the noble truth of the cessation of suffering, the noble truth of the way leading to the cessation of suffering
