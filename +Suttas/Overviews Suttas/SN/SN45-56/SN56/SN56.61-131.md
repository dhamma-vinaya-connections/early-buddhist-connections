> [!info] Summary
> A fortunate rebirth is rare. Therefore: practice!

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> those beings are few who are reborn among human beings, with wisdom, keep the 10 precepts, honour mother, father, wise persons,  respect elders, abstain from Dancing and Singing, gold and silver, accepting raw grains, meat, slaves, cattle, fields, buying, selling, cheat, binding, robbery, plunder, and violence
> 
> those beings are more numerous who, when they pass away as devas or human beings, are reborn in the animal or ghost realm or hell
