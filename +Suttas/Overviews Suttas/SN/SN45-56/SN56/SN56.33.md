> [!info] Summary
> The small creatures in the ocean are greater in number than all the foliage in the jungles of India. Still, your rebirths are more than this.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Beings who have not seen the four noble truths roam on from one birth to another, like a stick thrown end to end
