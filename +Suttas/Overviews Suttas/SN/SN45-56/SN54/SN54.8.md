# SN54.8 - PadÄ«popamasutta - The Simile of the Lamp
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Before his awakening the Buddha  generally practiced breath meditation, which kept him alert and peaceful and let to the ending of defilements. One who wishes for any of the higher fruits of the renunciate life should practice the same way

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Closely focus on this immersion due to mindfulness of breathing is possible to experience 4 jhanas, 4 immaterial jhanas, cessation of perceptions and feelings
> 
> If they feel a pleasant, unpleasant or neutral  feeling, they feel it detached. And chose between repulsive and the unrepulsive, and equanimus
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Lamp depended on oil and a wick to burn, like feelings and body of an arahant become extinguish at death
