# SN11.24 - Transgression - SN11:24
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> When two mendicants have a falling out, the Buddha  recalls the wise words of sakka on not letting anger ruin friendship.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Two kinds of wise people: one who sees a transgression as a transgression; and one who, when another is confessing a transgression, pardons him in accordance with the dhamma
> 
> Bring anger under your control;
Do not let your friendships decay.
Do not blame one who is blameless;
Do not utter divisive speech
>
