# SN10.6 - Piyaá¹…karasutta - Piyaá¹…kara
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A female spirit hushes her children as she listens to the teaching. But her son punabbasu says that he had not been talking, as he too wants to learn

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> For neither son nor husband,
Dear as they are, can free you from suffering
> 
> As listening to the true teaching
Frees living creatures from suffering
