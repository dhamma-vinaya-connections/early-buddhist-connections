# SN2.2 - Dutiyakassapasutta - Kassapa (2)
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The deity Kassapa speaks of how a bhikkhu should be a meditator, peaceful and free

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> In sitting alone in hidden places,
And in calming the mind.
> 
> Known the arising and passing of the world,
Healthy-minded, independent, that is their reward
>
