# SN4.1 - Tapokammasutta - Mortification
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> MÄra accuses the Buddha  of having abandoned the path of true austerity.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> All that mortification in search of immortality
Is as futile
As oars and rudder on dry land
> 
> Ethics, immersion, and wisdom:
By developing this path to awakening
I attained ultimate purity
