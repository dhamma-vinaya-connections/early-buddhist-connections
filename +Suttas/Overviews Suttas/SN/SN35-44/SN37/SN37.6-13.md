# SN37.6-13 -  - Malicious, Etc
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Things that make a woman either agreeable or disagreeable to men. And vice versa

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Attractive women  are beautiful, wealthy, and virtuous; she is clever and industrious; and she begets children, without envy ... not stingy ... not of loose conduct ... virtuous ... learned ... energetic ... mindful
> 
> Disagreeable women are selfish, envious and lustful, without faith, shameless, unafraid of wrongdoing, angry, unwise, keeps five precepts,
