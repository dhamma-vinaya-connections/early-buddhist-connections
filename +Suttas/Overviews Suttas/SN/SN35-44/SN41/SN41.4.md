# SN41.4 - Dutiyaisidattasutta - Isidatta (2)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Citta the householder invites some mendicants to his home for a meal. He asks them about the diversity of the elements, but only the junior monk, isidatta, is able to answer.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Diversity of elements are the 6 internal and external senses and their respective consciousness
> 
> Citta approached mahaka in his hut and asked him to demonstrate his skills, and mahaka obliged. Citta offers to support him with requisites, but mahaka left, never to return
