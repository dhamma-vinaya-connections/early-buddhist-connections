# SN44.11 - SabhiyakaccÄnasutta - Sabhiya KaccÄna
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The fate of the tathagata after death cannot be described because the causes for any worldly description of his fate would have totally ceased

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Impossible to describe arahants as 'possessed of form' or 'formless' or 'percipient' or 'non-percipient' or 'neither percipient nor non-percipient'
> 
> Because if that cause, that reason, were to cease totally everywhere
