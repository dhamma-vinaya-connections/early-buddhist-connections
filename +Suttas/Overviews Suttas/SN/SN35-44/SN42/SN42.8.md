# SN42.8 - Saá¹…khadhamasutta - The Conch Blower
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  clarifies a crucial point about kamma: although you can never undo a past misdeed, there are ways you can mitigate its inevitable harmful results

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> I have killed, stealed, sexual misconduct, lied. That was not right. But if I become remorseful for that reason, that evil deed of mine will not be undone  so, reflecting thus, he abandons them
> 
> Having abandoned covetousness, ill will and wrong views, he practice with a mind full of love, compassion, rejoice and equanimity
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Conch trumpet blower sounds in all directions like a mind full of love,compassion, rejoice and equanimity
