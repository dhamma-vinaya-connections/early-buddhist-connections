# SN40.4 - Catutthajhanapañhāsutta - The Fourth jhāna
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Moggallāna speaks to the bhikkhus of a time when his practice of meditation attainments was faltering. He expresses his gratitude for the Buddha , who urged him to stabilize that attainment

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> 4 jhanas, sphere of infinity of space, consciousness, nothingness, neither perception nor non-perception and signless
> 
> Don’t neglect the attainments settle your mind; unify your mind and immerse it in each of the attainments
>
