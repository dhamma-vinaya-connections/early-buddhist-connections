# SN35.135 - Khaṇasutta - The Opportunity
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> This human realm — neither too pleasurable nor too painful — is the best place to practice dhamma.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> You’re fortunate, bhikkhus, so very fortunate, to have the opportunity to live the spiritual life.”
> 
> In hell everything is unpleasant and disagreeable and in heaven everything is pleasant and agreeable
>
