> [!info] Summary
> Venerable migajÄla asks how one lives alone, and how with a partner. The Buddha  says that so long as one is bound by desire to the senses, one lives with a partner. A mendicant free of such desire dwells alone, even if they live in close association with worldly people

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Bound by  delight in the 6 senses, craving is his partner, and he has not abandoned it; therefore he is called one dwelling with a partner
