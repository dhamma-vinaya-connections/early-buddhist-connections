# SN35.231 - KhÄ«rarukkhopama - Simile Of The Latex Tree
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Like a tree that yields sap when cut, so long as desire is present it will be activated by the senses.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> If lust, hate and delusion still exists and has not been abandoned even trifling forms that enter into range of the eye obsess the mind,
> 
> If lust, hate and delusion  have been abandoned then even prominent forms that enter into range of the eye do not obsess the mind
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Tree with sap, like lust, hate and delusion in unobstructed persons
