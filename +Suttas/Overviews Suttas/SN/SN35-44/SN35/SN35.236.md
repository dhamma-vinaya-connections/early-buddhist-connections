# SN35.236 - HatthapÄdopama - Simile Of Hands And Feet
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> So long as this body exists there is suffering due to the senses

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When there are hands, picking up and putting down are discerned. When there are feet, coming and going are discerned
> 
> When there is the eye, pleasure and pain arise internally with eye-contact as condition, the same for other sense faculties
