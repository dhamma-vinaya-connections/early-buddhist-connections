# SN35.112 - BÄhirÄyatanaparijÄnanasutta - Fully Understanding (2)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Without understanding the six interior and external  sense fields and becoming dispassionate towards them, it is impossible to be free of suffering

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Directly knowing and fully understanding the internal senses, by developing dispassion towards it and abandoning it, one is capable of destroying suffering
> 
> Directly knowing and fully understanding the externall senses, by developing dispassion towards it and abandoning it, one is capable of destroying suffering
