# SN35.247 - ChappÄá¹‡akopama - Simile Of 6 Animals
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The senses are like a snake, a crocodile, a bird, a dog, a jackal, and a monkey all tied up together, pulling this way and that. Mindfulness is like a post that keeps them grounded

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> He dwells having set up mindfulness of the body, with a measureless mind, and he understands as it really is that liberation of mind, liberation by wisdom, wherein those evil unwholesome states cease without remainder
> 
> When a monk whose mindfulness immersed in the body is developed & pursued, the 6 senses does not pull toward pleasing , nor repelled the  unpleasing
> 

> [!info] Similes
> 6 animals all tied up together, pulling this way and that, like the 6 senses and mindfulness is like a post that keeps them grounded.
