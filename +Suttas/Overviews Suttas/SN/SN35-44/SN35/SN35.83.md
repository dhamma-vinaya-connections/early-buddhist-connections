# SN35.83 - Phaggunapañhāsutta - Phagguna
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Some bhikkhus check with the Buddha  that it’s correct to say that the spiritual life is lived to end suffering. The Buddha  answers that it is, and explains what that suffering is: the process of sense experience

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> ‘the purpose of living the spiritual life under the Buddha  is to completely understand suffering
> 
> The 6 sense fields, their consciousness and feelings are impermanent, suffering, wearing away that why they are called the world
>
