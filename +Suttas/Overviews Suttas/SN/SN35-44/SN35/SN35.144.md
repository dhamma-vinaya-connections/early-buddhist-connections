# SN35.144 - BÄhiradukkhahetusutta - Exterior and Cause Are Suffereing

> [!info] Summary
> Since the causes of the six interior and exterior sense fields are impermanent, suffering andnotself, how can the senses be otherwise?

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The 6 sense fields, their consciousness and feelings are impermanent, suffering and non self
>
