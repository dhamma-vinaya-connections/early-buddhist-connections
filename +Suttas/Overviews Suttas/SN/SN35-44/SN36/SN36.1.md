# SN36.1 - SamÄdhisutta - Concentration
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> There are three feelings, and all of them are suffering.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Pleasant feeling should be seen as suffering. Painful feeling should be seen as a dart. Neutral feeling should be seen as impermanent
> 
> The underlying tendency to greed should be given up when it comes to pleasant feeling. to repulsion when it comes to painful feeling. To ignorance  when it comes to neutral feeling
>
