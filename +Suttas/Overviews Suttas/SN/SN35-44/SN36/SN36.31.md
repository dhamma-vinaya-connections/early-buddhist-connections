# SN36.31 - NirÄmisasutta - Spiritual
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  describes the various grades of potential happiness and freedom, ranging from the worldly to the transcendent

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> There is worldly, unworldly joy, and there is a still greater unworldly joy, happiness, equanimity and liberation
> 
> Worldly is from senses, unworldly from jhanas and the greatest is being free from greed, hate and delusion
>
