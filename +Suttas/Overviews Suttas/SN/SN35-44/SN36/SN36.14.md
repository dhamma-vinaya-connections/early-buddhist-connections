# SN36.14 - AgÄrasutta - The Guest House
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Feelings arise in the body like winds blowing in the sky and guests in a hotel.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> So in this very body here
Various kinds of feelings arise,
Pleasant ones and painful ones,
And those neither painful nor pleasant.
> 
> If a monk is ardent and does not neglect
To practice mindfulness and comprehension clear,
The nature of all feelings will he understand
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Guests in house come and go like feelings
