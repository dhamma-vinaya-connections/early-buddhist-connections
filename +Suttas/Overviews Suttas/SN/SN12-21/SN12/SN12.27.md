# SN12.27 - Paccayasutta - Conditions
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Dependent origination is framed within the four noble truths. bhikkhu who understands dependent origination is a trainee who is accomplished in view.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Bhikkhu understands aging-and-death, its origin, its cessation, and the way leading to its cessation. He understands birth ... existence ... clinging [44] … craving ... feeling ... contact ... the six sense bases ... name-and-form … consciousness ... volitional formations
> 
> Noble disciple thus understands the condition; thus understands the origin of the condition; thus understands the cessation of the condition; thus understands the way leading to the cessation of the condition,84 he is then called a noble disciple who is accomplished in view
>
