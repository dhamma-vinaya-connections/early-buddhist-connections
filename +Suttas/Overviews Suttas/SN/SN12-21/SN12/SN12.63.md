# SN12.63 - Puttamaá¹sasutta - Son's Flesh
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A meditation on inter-relatedness, showing with four striking similes the suffering inherent in everything the body and mind depend upon for nourishment

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Nutriment of physical food to be regarded with dispassion to be free of the five strings of sensuality;  nutriment of contact is comprehended, there is dispassion for three feelings
> 
> When nutriment of intellectual intention is comprehended, there is dispassion for the three forms of craving and nutriment of consciousness is comprehended dispassion for name & form
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Nutrient similes of son's flesh eared by parents, flayed cow being chewed by insects, avoiding pit of glowing embers, criminal strike by arrows; like the 4 nutriments
