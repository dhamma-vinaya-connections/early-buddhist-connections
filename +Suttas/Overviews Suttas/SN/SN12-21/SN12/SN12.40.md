# SN12.40 - TatiyacetanÄsutta - Volition (3)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains the causal link between mental fabrications and consciousness

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> What you intend or plan, and what you have underlying tendencies for become a support for the continuation of consciousness. When this support exists, consciousness becomes established
> 
> When consciousness is established, name and form are conceived. Name and form are conditions for the six sense fields and there is also an inclination
