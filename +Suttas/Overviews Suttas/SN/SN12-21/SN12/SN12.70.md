# SN12.70 - Susimaparibbājakasutta - Susīma
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains to susima that development of psychic powers is not a prerequisite for enlightenment “first, comes knowledge of the stability of the dhamma, afterwards knowledge of nibbāna.”211

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Hearing that some bhikkhus claimed to be perfected, susīma approaches and asks them if they have developed psychic powers or the formless states, to which they say no
> 
> The Buddha , who explains that awakening follows insight into the principles of impermanence and causality.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Thieves punish by king, like who tries to steal the dhamma
