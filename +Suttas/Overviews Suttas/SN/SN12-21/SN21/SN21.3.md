> SÄriputta and MoggallÄna come together for a discussion, which touches on the use of psychic powers and energy. The two great monks end up warmly praising each other

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Willingly, let only my skin, sinews, and bones remain, and let the flesh and blood dry up in my body, but I will not relax my energy so long as I have not attained what can be attained by manly strength
