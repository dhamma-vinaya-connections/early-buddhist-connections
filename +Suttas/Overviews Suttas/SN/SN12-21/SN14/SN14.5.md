# SN14.5 - DutiyavedanÄnÄnattasutta - Diversity of Feelings (2)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The senses function due to the various different elements of sense experience, namely, the internal sense organ, the external sense stimulus, and consciousness

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The diverse internal elements of sense experience give rise to diverse contacts. But it is not the case that diverse contacts give rise to the diverse elements of sense experience
> 
> Diverse contacts give rise to diverse feelings but it is not the case that diverse feelings give rise to diverse contacts
>
