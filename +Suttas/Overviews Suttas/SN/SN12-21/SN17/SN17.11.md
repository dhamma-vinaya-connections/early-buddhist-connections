# SN17.11 - Suvaṇṇapātisutta - A Golden Bowl
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Material possessions, honor, and praise are terrible things, which can corrupt the mind even of a pure bhikkhu.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> ‘this venerable would not tell a deliberate lie even for the sake of a gold, silver, father, mother, brothera, sisters nor sons
> 
> But some time later I see them tell a deliberate lie because their mind is overcome and overwhelmed by possessions, honor and popularity
>
