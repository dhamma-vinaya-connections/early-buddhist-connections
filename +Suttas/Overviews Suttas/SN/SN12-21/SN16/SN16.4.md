> [!info] Summary
> Kassapa is content with robes, alms-food, lodging, and medicines.  While some mendicants approach families in the hope of receiving fine gifts or respect

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Approach families like the moon: withdrawn in body and mind, always the newcomer, and never impudent.
> 
> Teach others the Dhamma because of the natural excellence of the teaching, out of compassion, kindness, and sympathy,  not for selfishreasons
