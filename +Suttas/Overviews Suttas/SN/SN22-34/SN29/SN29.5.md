## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Dragons may be born from eggs, the womb, moisture, or spontaneously. Those born spontaneously are the best.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> One becomes a dragon due to ambivalent deeds in a past life and the appropriate aspiration.
> 
> Dragons sometimes transform their bodies and observe the sabbath in order to get a good rebirth.
