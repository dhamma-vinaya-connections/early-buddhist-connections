> [!info] Summary
> A mendicant should develop immersion (samÄdhi) and solitude in order to truly understand the origin and ending of the five aggregates.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Understand the origin and ending of form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness
>
