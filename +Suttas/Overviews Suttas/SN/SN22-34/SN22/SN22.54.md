# SN22.54 - BÄ«jasutta - Seeds
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Consciousness is like a seed that is planted in the soil of the other four aggregates and watered with craving and attachment is what fuels the cycle of rebirth.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> â€œthe four grounds of consciousness should be seen as like the earth element. Relishing and greed should be seen as like the water element. Consciousness with its fuel should be seen as like the five kinds of plants propagated from seeds
> 
> Giving up passion consciousness, thus unestablished, not proliferating, not performing any function, is released so, it is steady. So  it is contented. so it is not agitated. so, he is totally unbound
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Seed and plant life attain to growth, increase, and expansion, all do so based upon the earth, like consciousness based upon the other aggregates and greed as water
