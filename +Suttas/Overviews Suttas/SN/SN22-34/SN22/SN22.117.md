# SN22.117 - Bandhanasutta - Bondage
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> An unawakened person is bound both internally and externally to the aggregates and grows old in bondage

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Who does not regard aggregates as self, or self as possessing aggregates  or aggregates as in self, or self as in aggregates
> 
> Is not bound by bondage to aggregates who is not bound by inner and outer bondage, who sees the near shore and the far shore. He is freed from suffering,
>
