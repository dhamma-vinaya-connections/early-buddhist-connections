> I am now being itched, in past I was itched and in the future I will be itchy if I were to seek delight in the 5 aggregates whether  internal or external, gross or subtle, inferior or superior, far or near thus they should be seen as it really is with correct wisdom thus: â€˜this is not mine, this I am not, this is not my self.
