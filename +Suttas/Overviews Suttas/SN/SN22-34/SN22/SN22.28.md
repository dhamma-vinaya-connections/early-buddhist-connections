> [!info] Summary
> Beings are attached to the aggregates due to gratification, repelled due to drawbacks, and find escape because there is an escape.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> If you take pleasure in the aggregates  you take pleasure in suffering. If you take pleasure in suffering, I say youâ€™re not exempt from suffering
