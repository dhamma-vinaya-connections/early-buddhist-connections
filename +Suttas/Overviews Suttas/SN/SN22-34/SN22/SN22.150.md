# SN22.150 - Ajjhattasutta - Internally
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Pleasure and pain arise inside oneself due to grasping the aggregates

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When there is and, by clinging to the aggregates  pleasure and pain arise internally
> 
> But without clinging to what is impermanent, suffering, and subject to change, pleasure and pain don't arise
>
