# SN22.51 - Nandikkhayasutta - Destruction of Delight (1)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Right view is seeing the aggregates as they are, impermanent, and this leads to freedom

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Bhikkhu sees as impermanent aggregates which is actually impermanent: that is his right view
> 
> With the destruction of lust comes the destruction of delight. With the destruction of delight and lust the mind is liberated and is said to be well liberated.
>
