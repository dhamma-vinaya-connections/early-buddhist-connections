# SN22.110 - Arahantasutta - Arahant
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The aggregates are to be fully understood, ending the defilements is the full understanding of them, and an Arahant is the one who has achieved this

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness. should be completely understood. Their origin, ending, gratification, drawback, and escape
> 
> The ending of greed, hate, and delusion. This is called complete understanding.
