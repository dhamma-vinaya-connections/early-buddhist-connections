> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Don't take 5 aggregates to be the self or have a self or be in the self or self in them, nor this self is the same as the cosmos nor : 'I would not be, neither would there be what is mine. I will not be, neither will there be what is mine, all these views are fabrications
> 
> To an uninstructed,  touched by that which is felt born of contact with ignorance, craving arises. That fabrication is born of that.  That craving... That feeling... That contact...
