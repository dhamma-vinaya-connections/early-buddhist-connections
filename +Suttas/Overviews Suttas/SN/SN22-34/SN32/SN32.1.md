# SN32.1 - Suddhikasutta - Plain Version
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Cloud gods may live in cool clouds, warm clouds, storm clouds, wind clouds, or rain clouds.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Someone of good conduct and appropriate aspiration may be reborn as a cloud god living in various kinds of cloud if they give various gifts.
> 
> When the cool, warm, storm, wind or rain cloud gods decide to revel in their own element, the weather becomes like that.
