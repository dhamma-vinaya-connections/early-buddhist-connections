# SN27.9 - DhÄtusutta - Elements
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Desire for four physical elements is a corruption of the mind, and letting go such desire one is able to realize the various kinds of direct knowledge.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Desire and greed for the earth element, the water element, the fire element, the air element, the space element, or the consciousness element is a corruption of the mind.
> 
> Letting go such desire one is able to realize the various kinds of direct knowledge
