# SN23.3 - Bhavanettisutta - The Conduit to Existence
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  invokes a dramatic simile to explain how to dismantle attachment to the five aggregates.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> One tightly stuck, in desire, lust, delight, and craving for any of the aggregates ; therefore one is called a being
> 
> Desire, lust, delight, craving, engagement and clinging, mental standpoints, adherences, and underlying tendencies regarding 5 aggregates  conduit to existence
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Sand castles like the 5 aggregates
