# DN32 - Āṭānāṭiya Sutta - Āṭānāṭiya Protection
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Mighty spirits hold a congregation, and warn the Buddha  that, since not all spirits are friendly, the bhikkhus should learn verses of protection

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The āṭānāṭiya protection for the guarding, protection, safety, and comfort of the monks, nuns, laymen, and laywomen
> 
> To recite when any non-human being with malicious intent, possess, harass, harm or hurt us
>
