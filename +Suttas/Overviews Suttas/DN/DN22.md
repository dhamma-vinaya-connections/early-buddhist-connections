> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  details the seventh factor of the noble eightfold path, mindfulness meditation. This discourse is essentially identical to mn 10, with the addition of an extended section on the four noble truths derived from mn 141
This sutta offers comprehensive practical instructions on the development of mindfulness in meditation. The Buddha  describes how the development of continuous mindfulness of the four satipatthana ("foundations of mindfulness" or "frames of reference") â€” mindfulness of the body, of feelings, of the mind, and of mind-objects â€” can lead ultimately to full awakening
