> [!info] Summary
> Even more than if your clothes are on fire, you should make an effort to understand the four noble truths

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Life may be fraught with pain, but the breakthrough to Awakening is utterly painless.
> 
> I do not say that the breakthrough to the Four Noble Truths is accompanied by suffering or displeasure. Rather, the breakthrough to the Four Noble Truths is accompanied only by happiness and joy
