# SA416 - SA 4.8 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The famous first discourse, taught at varanasi to the group of five ascetics. It begins by rejecting the extremes of asceticism and indulgence and recommends the middle way of the eightfold path. Then it defines the four noble truths and analyzes them in twelve aspects.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Noble truth of suffering is to be, and know when is being  fully understood that is 5 aggregates and 6 senses; noble truth of the origin of suffering is to be and has been  abandoned that is craving for existence and sensuality
> 
> Noble truth of the cessation of suffering is to be and has been realized, the completecessationof that craving; noble truth of the way leading to the cessation of suffering is to be, and has been developed the noble 8 path
>
