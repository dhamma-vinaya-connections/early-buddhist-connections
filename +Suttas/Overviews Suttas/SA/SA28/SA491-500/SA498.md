## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> SÄriputta boldly claims that no-one has ever, or will ever, be more enlightened than the Buddha . He understands a principle of the dhamma.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> All those who become awakened do so by giving up the five hindrances, practicing the four kinds of mindfulness meditation, and developing the seven awakening factors.
> 
> Some foolish people may have perplexity or uncertainty regarding the tathÄgata, when they hear this Dhamma exposition their perplexity or uncertainty regarding the tathÄgata will be abandoned
>

> Fortress with one gate, like only one way to liberation
