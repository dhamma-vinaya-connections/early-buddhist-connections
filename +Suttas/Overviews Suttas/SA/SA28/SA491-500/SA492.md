# SA492 - SA 28.73 - ?
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Meditative attainments don’t always lead to letting go of the attachment to self.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Who focus on the cessation of identification or smashing ignorance but is not eager, wouldn’t expect that bhikkhu to stop identifying.
> 
> Who focus on the cessation of identification or smashing ignorance and is eager, would expect that bhikkhu to stop identifying.
>
