# SA545 - SA 30.11 - ?
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Even if kings beg them to disrobe, a bhikkhu who has developed the four kinds of mindfulness meditation is unmoved. Their mind flows to nibbāna like the ganges to the sea

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> It’s simply impossible for a bhikkhu who is developing and cultivating the four kinds of mindfulness meditation to reject the training
> 
> Your faculties are so very clear, and your complexion is pure and bright.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Ganges slopes to the east, like a monks mind slopes toward liberation
