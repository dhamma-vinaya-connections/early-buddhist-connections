# SA540 - SA 30.6 - ?
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Some bhikkhus ask anuruddha how he remains equanimous even though ill. He replies that it is due to the four kinds of mindfulness meditation

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> “what meditation does venerable anuruddha practice so that physical pain doesn’t occupy his mind?
> 
> I meditate with my mind firmly established in the four kinds of mindfulness meditation so that physical pain doesn’t occupy my mind
>
