# SA1247 - SA 49.2 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Meditation is like purifying gold. A meditator should focus on different aspects at different times in order to proceed smoothly

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Should attend periodically to the theme of concentration; he should attend periodically to the theme of uplifted energy; he should attend periodically to the theme of equanimity
> 
> Their mind becomes pliable, workable, and radiant, not brittle, and has properly entered immersion for the ending of defilements.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Goal cleaning process, like mind purification steps
