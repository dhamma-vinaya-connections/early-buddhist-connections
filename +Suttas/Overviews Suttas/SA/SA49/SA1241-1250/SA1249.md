# SA1249 - SA 49.4 - ?
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A cowherd with eleven qualities is competent to look after a herd of cattle. Likelwise, a bhikkhu with eleven qualities can grow in the dhamma

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> bhikkhu know form, is skilled in characteristics, don’t tolerate any bad, unskillful qualities, practice restraint of senses, teaches others the dhamma, ask questions about dhamma, finds joy in the meaning of dhamma, truly understands the noble eightfold path and mindfulness meditation, knows moderation, show extra respect to senior bhikkhus of long standing, long gone forth
> 
> A bhikkhu with these eleven qualities can achieve growth, improvement, or maturity in this teaching and training
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Cowherd skillful qualities like a skillful qualities
