# SA1255 - SA 49.10 - ?
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Love is more beneficial than generosity.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Hundred pots of food as charity are not as beneficial as   someone o develop a mind of lovingkindness even for the time it takes to pull a cow’s udder
> 
> We will develop and cultivate the liberation of mind by lovingkindness, make it our vehicle, make it our basis, stabilize it, exercise ourselves in it, and fully perfect it.’
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Spear not possible to bend like it is not possible to overthrow a bhikkhu who has developed love.
