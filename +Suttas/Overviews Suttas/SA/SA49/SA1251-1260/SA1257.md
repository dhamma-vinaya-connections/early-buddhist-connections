# SA1257 - SA 49.12 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> As the dirt in a fingernail is smaller than the great earth, the beings reborn as humans are few.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Those beings who are reborn among human beings are few. But those beings are more numerous who are reborn elsewhere than among human beings.
> 
> Vital formations perish very quickly
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Fingernail with soil compared to the earth, as humans compare to other beings
