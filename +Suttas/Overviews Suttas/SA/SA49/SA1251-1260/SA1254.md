# SA1254 - SA 49.9 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> As a family with few men and many women is easily attacked, non-human beings may attack a mendicant without love.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> It is difficult for nonhuman beings to assail a bhikkhu who has developed and cultivated the liberation of mind by lovingkindness
> 
> Just as it is difficult for burglars to assail those families that have few women and many men
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Protection from burglars in house, like a the protection of unconditional love
