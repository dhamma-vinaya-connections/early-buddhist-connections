# SA654 - SA 10.13 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Only when noble wisdom arises do the other four faculties become stable

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Just as all footprints fit into that of an elephant, wisdom is the chief of qualities that lead to awakening.
> 
> Just as lion is the best of beast or red sandalwood is the best of fragrances, wisdom is the chief of qualities that lead to awakening.
>
