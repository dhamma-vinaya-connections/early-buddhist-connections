# SA286 - SA 3.4 - Gautama (2)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Craving increases when you linger on pleasing things that stimulate grasping

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When one dwells contemplating gratification in things that can be clung to, the fetters and craving increases
> 
> When one dwells contemplating danger in things that can be clung to, craving ceases
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Fire, lamp, tree and sapling similes like grasping as fuel for samsara
