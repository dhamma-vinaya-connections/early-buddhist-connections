# SA188 - SA 2.1-4 - Impermanent, Painful, Empty, and Not Self
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Because the aggregates are impermanent, suffering, and not self a noble disciple becomes disillusioned with them.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Form, feelings, perceptions, fabrications and consciousness are impermanent, sufferingand notself, and their causes
> 
> The instructed noble disciple experiences revulsion towards, he becomes dispassionate. Through dispassion [his mind] is liberated.
