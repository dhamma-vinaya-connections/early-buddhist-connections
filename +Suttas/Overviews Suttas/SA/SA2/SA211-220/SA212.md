# SA212 - SA 2.96 - The Careful Practice
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Not all bhikkhus have completed their work with regards to the six senses, but some of them have

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> 6 sense fields are agreeable and those that are disagreeable. [one should train so that] these do not persist obsessing one’s mind even when they are repeatedly experienced.
> 
> With diligence their energy is roused up and unflagging, their mindfulness is established and lucid, their body is tranquil and undisturbed, and their mind is immersed in samādhi.
>
