# SA249 - SA 2.197 - Neither Existence (Remainder) Nor Non-Existence (Non-Remainder)
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The wanderer vacchagotta asks the Buddha  why the various speculative views come to be. The Buddha  replies that it is because of not knowing the noble truths respect the  aggregates

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Because of not knowing the aggregates, its origin, its cessation, and the way leading to its cessation that those various speculative views arise
> 
> Because of not seeing,  not breaking through, not comprehending, not penetrating, not discerning, not discriminating, not examining, not differentiating, not directly cognizing
