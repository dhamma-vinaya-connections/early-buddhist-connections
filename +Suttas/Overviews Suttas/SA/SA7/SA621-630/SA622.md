## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  teaches the mendicants about the four kinds of mindfulness meditation and the practice of situational awareness.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Meditates by observing an aspect of the body, feelings, mind, phenomena ardent, clearly comprehending, mindful, having removed covetousness and displeasure in regard to the world
> 
> You should live, keen, aware, and mindful, rid of desire and aversion for the world.
