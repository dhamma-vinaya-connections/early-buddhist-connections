# SA615 - SA 7.18 - Bhikṣuṇī Residence
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> How to respond skillfully to distracted states of mind that interfere with concentration.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When in meditation arises physical tension, or mental sluggishness, or the mind is externally scattered. That bhikkhu should direct their mind towards an inspiring foundation
> 
> Undirected meditation is bhikkhu understands: ‘my mind is not directed externally.’ And they understand: ‘over a period of time it’s unconstricted, freed, and undirected while 4 mindfulness meditation
