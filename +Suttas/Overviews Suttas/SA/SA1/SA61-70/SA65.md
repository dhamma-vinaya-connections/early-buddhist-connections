# SA65 - SA 1.62-73 - Feeling
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A bhikkhu should develop immersion (samādhi) and solitude in order to truly understand the origin and ending of the five aggregates.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Understand the origin and ending of form, feeling, perception, choices, and consciousness
> 
> Origin is to approve, welcome, and keep clinging to the aggregates . This gives rise to relishing, grasping, continued existence, birth and dukkha
>
