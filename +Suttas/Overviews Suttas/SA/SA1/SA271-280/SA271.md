## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ven. Tissa, mired in laziness, receives a wake-up call from the Buddha

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> In one who is not without passion, desire, love, thirst, fever, & craving for the aggregates does there arise sorrow, lamentation, pain, distress, & despair from change & alteration in his aggregates
> 
> Take the right fork stands for the noble eightfold path, go further ignorance, sensuality, anger and despair and you will find nibbana
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Path with fork, dense thicket, swap and precipice, like the gradual training
