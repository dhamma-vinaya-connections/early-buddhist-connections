# SA26 - SA 1.32 - The Well Taught Dharma
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A true Dhamma speaker teaches for letting go of the aggregates

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> One teaches and practice the Dhamma for the purpose of disenchantment toward the aggregates  for its fading away and cessation, one can be called a speaker on the dhamma.
> 
> Through revulsion towards aggregates through its fading away and cessation, one is liberated by nonclinging, one can be called a bhikkhu who has attained nibbÄna in this very life
