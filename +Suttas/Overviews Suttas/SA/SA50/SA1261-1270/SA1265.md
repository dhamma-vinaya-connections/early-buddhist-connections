# SA1265 - SA 50.1 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  gives an ailing ven. Vakkali a timeless teaching: "he who sees dhamma, sees me."

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> He who sees me sees dhamma. Truly seeing dhamma, one sees me; seeing me one sees dhamma
> 
> I do not doubt that whatever is impermanent is suffering. I do not doubt that in regard to what is impermanent, suffering, and subject to change, I have no more desire, lust, or affection
