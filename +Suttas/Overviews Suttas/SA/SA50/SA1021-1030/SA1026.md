> [!info] Summary
> An ailing monk attains arahatship when the Buddha  engages him in a dialogue about not-self. The Buddha  told him to abandon desire for sense experience, which is impermanent.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The 6 sense fields, their consciousness and feelings are impermanent, suffering and non self
>
