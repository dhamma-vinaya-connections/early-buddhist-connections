# SA1029 - SA 50.10 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  visits a sick ward, and offers advice to the monks on how to approach death with mindfulness

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Pleasant, painful and neutral feelings dependent on my own body and contact which are impermanent
> 
> Meditate observing impermanence, vanishing, dispassion, cessation, and letting go in the body and  feelings to let go of the underlying tendencies
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Lamp depended on oil and a wick to burn, like feelings and body of an arahant become extinguish at death
