# SA1049 - SA 51.11 - ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The various kinds of bad deeds are caused by greed, hate, and delusion

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Killing living, stealing, lying, sexual misconduct, harsh, divisive and non sense speech, covetousness, ill will and wrong views
> 
> And so greed, hate, and delusion are sources and origins for deeds. With the ending of greed, hate, and delusion, the sources of deeds are ended
