# AN5.57 - Abhiá¹‡hapaccavekkhitabbaá¹­hÄna - Subjects For Regular Reviewing
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  describes the "five facts that one should reflect on often, whether one is a woman or a man, lay or ordained."

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> I am liable to grow old, to get sick, to die, I must be parted and separated from all I hold dear and beloved, I am the owner of my deeds and heir to my deeds I shall be the heir of whatever deeds I do, whether good or bad
> 
> Reviewing this subject often, they entirely give up bad conduct, or at least reduce it. and further when they review this subject often, the path is born in them by developing it so, they give up the fetters
>
