# AN5.159 - UdÄyÄ« - With UdÄyÄ«
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains to ven. Ananda the five prerequisites for teaching Dhamma to others.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Teach Dhamma  step by step,  explaining my methods, out of kindness, not for the purpose of material reward, without hurting myself or others
> 
> According to the commentary, "hurting oneself" means exalting oneself. "hurting others" means putting other people down
