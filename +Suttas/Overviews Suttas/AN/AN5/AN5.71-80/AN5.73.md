# AN5.73 - Dhamma Viharin - One Who Dwells In The Dhamma
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The difference between someone who merely studies the letter of the dharma, and someone who truly lives by it.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> One who studies a lot, the one who advocates a lot, the one who recites a lot, the one who thinks a lot, and the one who lives by the teaching
> 
> Practice jhana, monk. Don't be heedless. Don't later fall into regret. This is our message to you."
