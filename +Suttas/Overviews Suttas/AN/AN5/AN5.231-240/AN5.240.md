# AN5.240 - Anukampa - Compassionate bhikkhu
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> bhikkhu with five qualities shows compassion to the lay people

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Encourage them in higher ethics. visit them when they are sick, inspire with a Dhamma talk, and to make merits with incoming bhikkhus,
> 
> Criticize those deserving of criticism, and they praise those deserving of praise, they’re not stingy regarding monasteries, families, and material possessions.
>
