# AN5.179 - Gihi - Layperson
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> How to recognize a lay stream-winner.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Laypeople whose actions are restrained in the five precepts, and who get four blissful meditations in the present life
> 
> Four blissful meditations are experiential confidence in the Buddha , Dhamma and Sangha with ethical conduct is loved by the noble ones, unbroken,
