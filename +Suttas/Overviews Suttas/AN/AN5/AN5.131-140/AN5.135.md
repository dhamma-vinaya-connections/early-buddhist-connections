# AN5.135 - Patthanā - Aspiration
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> bhikkhu with five qualities aspires to end the defilements

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Has faith, is healthy, not devious or deceitful, energetic and wise
> 
> Is ethical, learned, mind is firmly establishedin 4 mindfulness,  energetic and wise
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Prince qualities, like bhikkhu aspires for liberation qualities
