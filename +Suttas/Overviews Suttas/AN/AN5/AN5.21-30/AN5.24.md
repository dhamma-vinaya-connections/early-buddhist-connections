# AN5.24 - Dutiyaupanisa - Vital Conditions
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  teaches how, like a tree that grows due to its leaves, liberation follows when ethics is present

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Ethics, no regrets, joy, rapture, tranquility, bliss, samadhi, truly know and see, disillusion and dispassion, knowledge and vision of freedom
> 
> Good qualities flow on and fill up from one to the other, for going from the near shore to the far shor
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Tree that was complete with branches and foliage, like fulness of the path
