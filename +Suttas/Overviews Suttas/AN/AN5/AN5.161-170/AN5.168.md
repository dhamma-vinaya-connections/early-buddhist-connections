# AN5.168 - Sila - Ethics
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Vital condition for knowledge and vision of freedom

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Ethics, samadhi, true knowledge, dispassion and disenchantment are vital condition for knowledge and vision of freedom
> 
> An ethical person, who has fulfilled ethics, has fulfilled a vital condition for right immersion
