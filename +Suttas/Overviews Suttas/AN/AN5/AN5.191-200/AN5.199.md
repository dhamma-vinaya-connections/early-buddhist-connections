# AN5.199 - Kula - Families
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> On the rewards of being attentive and hospitable to a visiting monk/nun

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When ethical renunciates come to a family they became confident they are in path to heaven, when bow down, and offer them a seat they are in path leading to a birth in an eminent family.
> 
> They get rid of the stain of stinginess is path leading to being illustrious, share what they have, are in path to whealth, and when listen to the teachings are in path to wisdom
