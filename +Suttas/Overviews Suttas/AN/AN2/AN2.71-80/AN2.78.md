> [!info] Summary
> Prevent unskillful qualities to arise by giving up their causes and conditions

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Bad, unskillful qualities have different  causes, foundations, sources, conditions, reasons, feeling, form, perceptions consciousness and conditioned basis
