# AN6.58 - Āsava - Defilements
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains diverse methods for overcoming diverse kinds of defilements

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Defilements that should be given up by restrain, by using, by enduring, by avoiding, by getting rid and by developing
> 
> A bhikkhu with these six factors is worthy of offerings dedicated to the gods, worthy of hospitality, worthy of a religious donation, worthy of veneration
>
