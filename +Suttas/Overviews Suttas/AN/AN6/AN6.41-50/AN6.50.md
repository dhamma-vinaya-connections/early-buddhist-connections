# AN6.50 - Indriyasaá¹ƒvara - Sense Restraint
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Just as a tree without leaves cannot grow, sense restraint is the foundation for spiritual growth

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Sense restraint is a vital condition for ethical conduct, right samadhi, true knowledge and vision, disillusionment and dispassion and knowledge and vision of freedom.
> 
> One who has fulfilled all these conditions  has fulfilled a vital condition for knowledge and vision of freedom.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Tree complete in all its parts, like someone fulfilled all training
