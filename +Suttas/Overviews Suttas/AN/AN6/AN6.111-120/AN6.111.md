# AN6.111 - Vitakka - Thoughts
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Antidotes for bad thoughts, and perceptions and properties

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Develop thoughts and perceptions of renunciation to give up sensual thoughts, thoughts of good will to give up malicious thoughts, and thoughts of harmlessness to give up cruel thoughts
> 
> Develop the element of renunciation to give up the element of sensuality, the element of good will to give up the element of malice, and the element of harmlessness to give up the element of cruelty.
