# AN6.113 - AssÄda - Gratification
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Antidotes for views of gratification, self, and wrong views, discontent, cruelty, and misconduct.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Develop the perception of impermanence to give up the view that things are gratifying; the perception of not-self to give up the view of self; and right view to give up wrong view.
> 
> Develop rejoicing to give up negativity, harmlessness to give up cruelty, and principled conduct to give up unprincipled conduct.
>
