# AN6.1 - Paṭhamaāhuneyya - Worthy Of Offerings
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A bhikkhu is worthy of gifts because of equanimity for the six senses

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Equanimity for the six senses.
> 
> Worthy of a religious donation, worthy of veneration with joined palms, and is the supreme field of merit for the world
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Thoroughbred horse, like accomplished disciples
