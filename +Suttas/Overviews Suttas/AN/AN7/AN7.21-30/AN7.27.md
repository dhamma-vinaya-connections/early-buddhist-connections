# AN7.27 - Saññā - Perceptions
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  teaches seven principles of non-decline to the bhikkhus

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Perception of impermanence …
Not-self … ugliness … drawbacks … giving up …fading away … cessation
> 
> These nine perceptions, when developed and cultivated, are very fruitful and beneficial. They culminate in the deathless and end with the deathless
>
