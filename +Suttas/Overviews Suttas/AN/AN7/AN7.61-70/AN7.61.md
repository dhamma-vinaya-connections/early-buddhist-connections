# AN7.61 - PacalÄyamÄna - Nodding Off
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Before his awakening, MoggallÄna is struggling with sleepiness in meditation. The Buddha  visits him and gives seven ways to dispel drowsiness, and other important teachings

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Shake off your drowsiness by don't attend to that perception when drowsy, think about and consider the teaching, recite in detail the teaching, pinch your ears and rub your limbs, flush your eyes with water, look around, focus on the perception of light or walk meditation; finally lie down
> 
> Where a monk has heard, 'all phenomena are unworthy of attachment.' Having heard that he fully knows all things. then when they experience any kind of feeling they focus observing impermanence, dispassion, cessation, and letting go in those feelings
