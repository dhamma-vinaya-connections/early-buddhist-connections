> [!info] Summary
> A mendicant who has seven qualities is worthy, with a special emphasis on listening to and learning the teaching

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Knows the teachings, knows the meaning, has self-knowledge, knows moderation, knows the right time, knows assemblies, and knows people high and low
