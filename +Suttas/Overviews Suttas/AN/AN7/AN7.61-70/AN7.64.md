# AN7.64 - Kodhana - Angry Person
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Seven dangers of giving in to anger

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> An angry person is ugly, sleeps poorly, destroys his wealth, and status, relatives, friends, & colleagues avoid him. doesn't know his own benefit, and he kill beings and do violence to himself,
> 
> A man conquered by anger is in a mass of darkness.
He takes pleasure in bad deeds as if they were good,
But later, when his anger is gone,
He suffers as if burned with fire
>
