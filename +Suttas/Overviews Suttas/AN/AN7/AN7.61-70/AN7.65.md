> [!info] Summary
> A sense of conscience is the foundation that leads step by step to liberation

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Conscience and prudence, vital condition for sense restraint, that for ethics, that for right samadhi, that for true knowledge and vision, that for disillusionment and dispassion and that for knowledge and vision of freedom
> 
> Person who has fulfilled conscience and prudence has fulfilled a vital condition for sense restraint and so on
