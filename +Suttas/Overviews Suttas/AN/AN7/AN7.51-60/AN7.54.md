# AN7.54 - AbyÄkata - Undeclared Points
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains why a noble disciple has no doubt when it comes to the unexplained points

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Undeclared points are view-standpoint, , this perception-standpoint, this product of conceiving, this product of elaboration, this clinging-standpoint: that's anguish
> 
> The instructed disciple of the noble ones discerns anguish, discerns the origination of anguish, discerns the cessation of anguish, discerns the path of practice leading to the cessation of anguish, and so for him that anguish ceases
>
