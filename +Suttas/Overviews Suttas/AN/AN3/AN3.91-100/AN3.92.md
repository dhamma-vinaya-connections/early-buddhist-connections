# AN3.92 - AccÄyika - Urgent
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Just as a farmer can't predict when the fruit will ripen, so we can't predict when awakening will occur. So just keep your practice strong; the rest will take care of itself.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Undertaking the training in the higher ethics, the higher mind, and the higher wisdom are the duties of a bhikkhu
> 
> As they train it comes the time when their mind is freed from defilements by not grasping
>
