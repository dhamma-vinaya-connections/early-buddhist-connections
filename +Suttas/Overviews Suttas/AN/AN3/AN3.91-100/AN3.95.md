# AN3.95 - ParisÄ - Assemblies
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Assemblies that are the best, or divided, or harmonious. A harmonious assembly lead the mind to happiness and stillness

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Assembly of the best, a divided assembly, and a harmonious assembly.
> 
> Take the lead in seclusion, rousing energy for attaining the unattained
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Rains heavily on a mountain top, and the water flows downhill filling pools and rivers till the ocean, like factors for awakening
