# AN3.96 - Ājānīya - Thoroughbred
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A bhikkhu is beautiful, strong, and fast like a thoroughbred horse

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Bhikkhu is beautiful because is ethical, strong for energetic and fast for his wisdom
> 
> With these three factors is worthy of offerings dedicated to the gods, worthy of hospitality, worthy of a religious donation
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Thoroughbred horse worthy for the king, like worthy  disciples
