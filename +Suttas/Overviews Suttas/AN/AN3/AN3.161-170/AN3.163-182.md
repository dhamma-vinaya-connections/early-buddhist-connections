# AN3.163-182 - KammapathapeyyÄ - Courses Of Deeds
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Someone with three qualities is cast down to hell and the opposite is raised up to heaven

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> They themselves kill, encourage others to kill, and they approve of killing, steal, sexual misconduct, are covetous, with ill will and wrong views
> 
> They themselves lie. They encourage others to lie. And they approve of lying, harsh speech, divisive speech and talk nonsense
>
