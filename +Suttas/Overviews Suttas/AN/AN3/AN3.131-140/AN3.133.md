# AN3.133 - Yodhājīva - Warrior
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Three ways a bhikkhu is like a king’s warrior

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Worthy bhikkhus are long-distance shooter, a marksman, and one who shatters large objects.
> 
> They have right understanding: ‘this is not mine, I am not this, this is not my self.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Warriors like worthy bhikkhus
