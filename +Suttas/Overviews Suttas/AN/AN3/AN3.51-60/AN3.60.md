# AN3.60 - Saṅgārava - With Saṅgārava
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A brahmin argues that a sacrifice is less selfish than the bhikkhu’s life. And the king’s men are deploring the decline in psychic abilities among the saṅgha.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Demonstration of psychic power, a demonstration of revealing, and a demonstration of instruction.
> 
> Demonstration where someone instructs others: ‘think like this, not like that. Focus your mind like this, not like that. Give up this, and live having achieved that. It’s the finest of the three kinds of demonstration.
>
