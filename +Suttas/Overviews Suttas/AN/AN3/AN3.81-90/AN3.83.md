# AN3.83 - Samaṇa - Ascetics
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Practicing the Dhamma means more than simply acting the part.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Have keen enthusiasm for undertaking the training in the higher ethics, the higher mind, and the higher wisdom
> 
> Duties of a farmer are like those of a bhikkhu.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Donkey tries to moo like a cow, like bhikkhus that don't train in 3 trainings
