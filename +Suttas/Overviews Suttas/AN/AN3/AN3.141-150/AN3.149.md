# AN3.149 - Akusala - Unskillful
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Deeds by body, speech and mind, determine rebirth either in good or in bad places

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Good person keep themselves healthy and whole by skillful, blameless and pure deeds
> 
> Bad person keep themselves broken and damaged by unskillful, blameworthy and impure deeds
