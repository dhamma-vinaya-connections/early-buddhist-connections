# AN3.34 - NidÄna - Sources
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Greed, hate, and delusion,  are like seeds. but action (kamma) performed by an Arahant bears no kammic fruit

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Greed, hate, and delusion are sources that give rise to deeds
> 
> Wherever one's selfhood turns up, there that action will ripen. Where that action ripens, there one will experience its fruit,
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Seeds in good soil would grow, increase, and mature, like deeds sourced in greed, aversion and delusion
