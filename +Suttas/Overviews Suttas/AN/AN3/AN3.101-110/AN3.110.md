# AN3.110 - Arakkhita - Unprotected
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Protecting your mind is like protecting a house.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Mind is unprotected, deeds of body, speech, and mind are unprotected
> 
> When deeds are unprotected, they become corrupted and rotten
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Roof broken rest of house get soaked, and become rottenhouse, like unprotected mind
