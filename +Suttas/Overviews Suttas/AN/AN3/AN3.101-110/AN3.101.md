# AN3.101 - Paá¹ƒsudhovaka - Dirt Washer
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Meditation is like purifying gold. A meditator should progressively eliminate more refined corruptions

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Mind has coarse corruptions: bad bodily, verbal, and mental conduct, middling corruptions: sensual, malicious, or cruel thoughts. fine corruptions: thoughts of family, country, and being looked up to
> 
> When he is rid of them, there remain only thoughts of the dhamma. His concentration is kept in place by the fabrication of forceful restraint. But there comes a time when his mind grows steady inwardly, settles down, grows unified & concentrated
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Goal cleaning process, like mind purification steps
