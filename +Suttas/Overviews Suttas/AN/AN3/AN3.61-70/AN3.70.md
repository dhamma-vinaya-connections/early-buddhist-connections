# AN3.70 - Uposatha - Sabbath
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Buddha describes right and wrong ways of observing the uposatha days. Those who observe the uposatha correctly with 6 reflections and 8 precepts and description of lifespans of deities

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Uposatha of the cowherds, the sabbath of the jains, and the sabbath of the noble ones
> 
> Uposatha of the noble ones it is the cleansing of the defiled mind through the proper technique. As he is recollecting the tathagata, his mind is calmed, and joy arises; the defilements of his mind are abandoned,
