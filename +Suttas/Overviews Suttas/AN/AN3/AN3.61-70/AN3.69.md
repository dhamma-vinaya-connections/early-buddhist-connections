# AN3.69 - AkusalamÅ«la - Unskillful Roots
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Unwholesome roots create unwholesome actions, and lead to suffering

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When a greedy, hateful, or deluded person chooses to act by way of body, speech, or mind, that too is unskillful.
> 
> That person suffers in the present life, with anguish, distress, and fever.And When the body breaks up, after death, they can expect to be reborn in a place of loss,
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Creepers that choke a big tree like greed, hate, and delusion
