# AN3.62 - Bhaya - Dangers
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Although fire, flood, and war may threaten to divide families, the world is fraught with even greater dangers. Here is a surefire way to overcome them all.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The perils of old age, sickness, and death that tear mothers and children apart
> 
> There is a path and a practice that leads to giving up and going beyond the three perils that  tear mothers and children apart
