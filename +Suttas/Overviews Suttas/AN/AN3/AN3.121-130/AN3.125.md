# AN3.125 - Gotamakacetiya - Gotamaka Shrine
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  teaches a simple but powerful message: his teaching is from personal experience, and it works.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> I teach based on direct knowledge, with a demonstrable basis, and with reasons
> 
> Since this is so, you should follow my advice and instruction. This is enough for you to feel joyful, delighted, and happy
