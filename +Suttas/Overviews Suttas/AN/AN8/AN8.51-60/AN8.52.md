# AN8.52 - Ovada - Nuns Advisers
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> With eight qualities a monk may be appointed to teach the nuns

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Qualities of advicer for nuns: ethical, learned, know both monastic codes, good speakers, able to educate, likeable for most nuns, never harrassed nuns and ordained for 20 years
> 
> A monk with these eight qualities may be agreed on as an adviser for nuns
