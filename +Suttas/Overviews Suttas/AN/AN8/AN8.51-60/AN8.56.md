# AN8.56 - Bhaya - Danger
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains how addiction to sensual pleasures is perilous

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Sensual pleasures are called: dangers, suffering, boil, dart, tie, disease, bog and womb
> 
> Someone who is caught up in sensual greed and shackled by lustful desire is not freed from dangers in the present life or in lives to come
