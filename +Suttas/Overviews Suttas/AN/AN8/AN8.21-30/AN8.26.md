# AN8.26 - MahÄnÄma - With MahÄnÄma
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Questioned by his relative MahÄnÄma, the Buddha  explains what makes someone a buddhist lay follower, a virtuous lay follower, and a lay follower practicing for the welfare of all

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Accomplished in faith, ethics, generosity, see bhikkhus, listen, remember, examine, understand the meaning of dhamma, and practice accordingly
> 
> Lay follower who is practicing to benefit both themselves and others have these qualities and encourage others to do the same
