# AN8.7 - Devadattavipatti - Devadatta’s Failure
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Devadatta’s downfall was the eight worldly conditions.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Gain …loss … fame … disgrace … honor … dishonor … wicked desires … bad friendship
> 
> It’s good for a bhikkhu to check their own failings and success from time to time. and  to check the failings and success of others
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Grains get from large heap of grain, like wisdom coming from the Buddha
