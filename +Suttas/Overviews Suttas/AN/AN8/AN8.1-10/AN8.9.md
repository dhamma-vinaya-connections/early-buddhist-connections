# AN8.9 - Nanda - Nanda
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  praises the grace and restraint of venerable Nanda

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Guards the sense doors, eats in moderation, is dedicated to wakefulness, and has mindfulness and situational awareness
> 
> He live the full and pure spiritual life
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Field of barley some bad barley, grain is being winnowed, rotten inside trees, like bad behavior bhikkhu
