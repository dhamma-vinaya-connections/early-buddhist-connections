# AN8.66 - Vimokkha - Liberations
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Eight meditative liberations, based on the vision of forms and the attainment of the formless

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Having physical form, they see visions, not perceiving form internally, they see visions externally, focused only on beauty, infinity of space, infinity of consciousness, nothingness and neither perceptions nor not perceptions, cessation of perceptions and feelings
> 
> These are the eight liberations
