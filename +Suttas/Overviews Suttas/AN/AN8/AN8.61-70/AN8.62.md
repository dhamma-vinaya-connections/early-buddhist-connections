# AN8.62 - Alaá¹ƒ - Good Enough
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Eight sets of qualities pertain to benefiting oneself and other.  having all is good for both

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Good qualities for themselves but not for others: quick-witted in skillful, memorize the teachings, examine the meaning, understands and practice accordingly
> 
> Good qualities for others  not for themselves : quick-witted in skillful, memorize the teachings, don't examine the meaning, understands and don't practice accordingly but good speaker and they educate others
