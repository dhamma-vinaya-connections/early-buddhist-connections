# AN8.81 - SatisampajaÃ±Ã±a - Mindfulness And Situational Awareness
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Mindfulness and situational awareness are a foundation for developing higher spiritual qualities leading to liberation

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Mindfulness and situational awareness, vital condition for conscience and prudence, for sense restraint, ethics, for samadhi, for knowledge and vision of truths, for disillusionment and dispassion, for knowledge and vision of freedom
> 
> One who lacks any of these has destroyed a vital condition for knowledge and vision of freedom
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Tree that lacked branches and foliage. Its shoots, bark, softwood, and heartwood, like one who lacks these qualities
