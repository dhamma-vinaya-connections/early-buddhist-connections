# AN4.96 - Attahita - Benefit Oneself
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> What does it mean to practice Dhamma for one's own benefit â€” and for another's?

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> One who practices to benefit neither themselves nor others;
One who practices to benefit others, but not themselves;
One who practices to benefit themselves, but not others; and
One who practices to benefit both themselves and others.
> 
> Beneficial practices are: remove their own greed, hate, and delusion, quick-witted when it comes to skillful qualities, understanding the meaning and the teaching, they practice accordingly. and educate, encourage, fire up, and inspire their spiritual companions and keep 5 precepts
>

> Cream of ghee is best milk product, like one who practice for his and others benefits
