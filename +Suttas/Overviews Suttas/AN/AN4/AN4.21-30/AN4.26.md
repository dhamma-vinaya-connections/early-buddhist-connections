# AN4.26 - Kuha - Deceivers
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> bhikkhus who are deceivers, stubborn, flatterers, frauds, insolent, and scattered: they are no bhikkhus of the Buddha

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Those who are genuine, not flatterers, wise, 
Amenable, and serene: 
These do grow in the teaching
> 
> Those who are deceivers, stubborn, flatterers, and frauds, 
Insolent and scattered: 
These don’t grow in the teaching
>
