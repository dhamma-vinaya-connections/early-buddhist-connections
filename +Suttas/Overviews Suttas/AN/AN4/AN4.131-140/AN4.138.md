# AN4.138 - Nikaá¹­á¹­ha - Retreat
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Retreat of body and mind

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> One is on retreat in body, but not mind;
One is on retreat in mind, but not body;
One is on retreat in neither body nor mind; and
One is on retreat in both body and mind
> 
> Retreat of body is to frequents remote lodgings in the wilderness and the mind is to only think thoughts of renunciation, good will, and harmlessness
