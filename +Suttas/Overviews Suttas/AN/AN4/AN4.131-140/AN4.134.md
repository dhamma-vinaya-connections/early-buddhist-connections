> [!info] Summary
> Living on the fruit of effort or of past karma and blame levels

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> One who lives off the fruit of initiative, but not deeds;
One who lives off the fruit of deeds, but not initiative;
One who lives off the fruit of both initiative and deeds; and
One who lives off the fruit of neither initiative nor deeds
> 
> Blameworthy, the mostly blameworthy, the slightly blameworthy, and the blameless
