# AN4.112 - Kesi - With Kesi
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains to kesi, a horsetrainer, how he teaches dhamma. This brilliant exposition warrants close study by every teacher, as it reveals the multiple levels in which effective teaching operates

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> I guide a person in training sometimes gently, sometimes harshly, and sometimes both gently and harshly and if they don't follow any of these forms of training, I ignored them
> 
> Good disciples are integrity, speed, patience, and sweetness.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Training of horses, like training of disciples
