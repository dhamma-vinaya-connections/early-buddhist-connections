# AN4.200 - Pema - Affection
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The opinions of our friends and enemies often influence our own thoughts and feelings about others. This kind of thinking is rooted in craving, and the Buddha  offers a cure

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Affection is born of affection. Aversion is born of affection. Affection is born of aversion. Aversion is born of aversion.
> 
> In 4 jhanas a monk who doesn't pull in, doesn't push away, doesn't smolder, doesn't flare up, and doesn't burn.
>
