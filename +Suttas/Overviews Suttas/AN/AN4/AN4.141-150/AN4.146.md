# AN4.146 - KÄla - Times
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> There are these four times to balance learning and practice

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> A time for listening to the teaching, a time for discussing the teaching, a time for serenity, and a time for discernment
> 
> When these four times are rightly developed and progressed, they gradually lead to the ending of defilements
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Rain on mountains flowing down hill like right practice
