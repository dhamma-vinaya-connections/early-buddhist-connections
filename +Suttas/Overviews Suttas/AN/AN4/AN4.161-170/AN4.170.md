# AN4.170 - Yuganaddha - Conjunction
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ven. Ananda describes the paths to arahantship by which insight (vipassana) and tranquillity (samatha) work hand-in-hand

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Who develops serenity before discernment, after, in conjunction
> 
> And others mind is seized by restlessness. But there comes a time when their mind is stilled internally
