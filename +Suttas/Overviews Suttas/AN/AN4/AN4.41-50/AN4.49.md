# AN4.49 - Vipallasa - Perversions
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Distortions of perception, mind, and view

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Taking impermanence as permanence.Taking Suffering as happiness.Taking Not-self as self.Taking Ugliness as beauty.
> 
> Buddhas arise to make things bright,
They present this profound teaching
Which brings suffering to an end.
When those with wisdom have heard this,
They recuperate their right mind:
>
