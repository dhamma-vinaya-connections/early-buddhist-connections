# AN4.244 - Āpattibhaya - Perils Of Offenses
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A bhikkhu fears offenses just as someone would fear punishment

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Set perception of peril regarding expulsion, suspension, confession or acknowledgable offenses
> 
> It can be expected that if they haven’t committed a confessable offense they won’t, and if they committed one they will deal with it properly
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Man fears crimes consequences like bhikkhus fear  offenses
