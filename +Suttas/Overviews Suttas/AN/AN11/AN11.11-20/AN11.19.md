# AN11.19 - Samādhi - Immersion
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ānanda asks the Buddha  about a mysterious state of immersion in meditation where there is no normal perception, yet one is still conscious

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Meditation not perceiving earth, water, fire, air, infinity of space, of consciousness, nothingness, neither nor perceptions, this world & next world, what  is seen, heard, thought, known, attained, sought, or explored by the mind
> 
> When a bhikkhu perceives:
‘this is peaceful; this is sublime—that is, the stilling of all activities, the letting go of all attachments, the ending of craving, fading away, cessation, extinguishment
>
