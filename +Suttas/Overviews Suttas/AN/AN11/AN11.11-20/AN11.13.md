# AN11.13 - Nandiya - With Nandiya
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Nandiya the sakyan sees that the monks are preparing robes after the rains season. Knowing that the Buddha  will soon depart, he asks how they are to live with their busy lives

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Establish in faith, ethics, mindfulness, samadhi and wisdom and develop recollections of the Buddha , dhamma, Sangha, own ethics, own generosity and divinities
> 
> This way you should establish mindfulness internally, develop this while you are walking, while you are standing, sitting, lying down, while you are busy at work, resting in your home crowded with children
