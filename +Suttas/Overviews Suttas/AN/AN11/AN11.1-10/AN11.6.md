# AN11.6 - Byasana - Disasters
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> When a bhikkhu abuses their fellow monastics, eleven disasters follow

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Don’t achieve the unachieved. loose  achieved, don’t refine their good qualities. They Overestimate their good qualities. Or They live the spiritual life dissatisfied. Or They commit a corrupt offense. Or They reject the training and return to a lesser life. Or They contract a severe illness. Or They go mad and lose their mind. They Feel lost when they die. And They go to hell
> 
> abuses and insults their spiritual companions, speaking ill of the noble ones, will, without a doubt
>
