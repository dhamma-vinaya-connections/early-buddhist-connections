# AN11.10 - Moranivāpa - At The Peacocks’ Feeding Ground
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Possessing various sets of qualities, a bhikkhu is best among gods and men

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Adept’s ethics, immersion, and wisdom, demonstration of psychic power,  of revealing, and of instruction; right view, right knowledge, and right freedom, knowledge and conduct
> 
> A bhikkhu with these two qualities has reached the ultimate end, the ultimate sanctuary, the ultimate spiritual life, the ultimate goal. They are best among gods and humans
>
