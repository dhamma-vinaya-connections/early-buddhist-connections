# AN10.215 - Nirayasagga - Heaven And Hell

> [!info] Summary
> The ten kinds of bad deeds lead you to hell; the ten good deeds to heaven

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Killing living, stealing, lying, sexual misconduct, harsh, divisive and non sense speech, covetousness, ill will and wrong views
