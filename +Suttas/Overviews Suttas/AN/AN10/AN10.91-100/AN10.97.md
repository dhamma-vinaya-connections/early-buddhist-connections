# AN10.97 - Āhuneyya - Worthy Of Offerings
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> With ten qualities, a bhikkhu is worthy

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> bhikkhu is ethical, learned, have good friends, right views, wield the many kinds of psychic power, clairaudience, understand the minds of others, recollect many kinds of past lives, see sentient beings passing away and being reborn, realize the undefiled freedom
> 
> bhikkhu with these ten factors is worthy of offerings dedicated to the gods, worthy of hospitality, worthy of a religious donation, worthy of veneration with joined palms, and is the supreme field of merit for the world
>
