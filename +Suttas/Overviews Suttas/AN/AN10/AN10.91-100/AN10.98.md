# AN10.98 - Thera - Senior bhikkhu
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> With ten qualities, a senior bhikkhu is at ease wherever they live

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> bhikkhu is ethical, learned, knows monastic code,   realize the undefiled freedom, skilled in raising and settling disciplinary issues, loves dhamma, contentment, well restrained, get 4 jhanas
> 
> Senior bhikkhu with these ten qualities lives comfortably in whatever region they live
>
