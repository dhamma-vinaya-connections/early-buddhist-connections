# AN10.199-210 - Aparapuggala - Should Not Associate
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Should not associate, nor frequent, not pay homage, nor praise, not venerate, nor venerate, nor defer to, someone with these qualities

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Killing living, stealing, lying, sexual misconduct, harsh, divisive and non sense speech, covetousness, ill will and wrong views
> 
> Is not a success, does not win over conceit, doesn't grow in wisdom and makes bad karma and their opposites
