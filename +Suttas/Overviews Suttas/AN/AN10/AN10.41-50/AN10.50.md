# AN10.50 - Bhaṇḍana - Arguments
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Some monks are arguing, so the Buddha  teaches them ten warm-hearted principles

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> A bhikkhu is ethical, learned, has good friends, is easy to admonish, works for the community,  loves the teachings and is a delight to converse with, energetic, is content,  mindful and wise
> 
> Arguing is not appropriate for you gentlemen who have gone forth in faith. ten warm-hearted qualities that make for fondness and respect, conducing to inclusion, harmony, and unity, without quarreling
>
