> [!info] Summary
> Some monks are arguing, so the Buddha  teaches them ten warm-hearted principles

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> A mendicant is ethical, learned, has good friends, is easy to admonish, works for the community,  loves the teachings and is a delight to converse with, energetic, is content,  mindful and wise
>
