# AN10.176 - Cunda - With Cunda
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains to cunda that genuine self-purification comes about not from performing sacred rites, but by cultivating skillfulness in one's thoughts, words, and deeds.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Killing living, stealing, lying, sexual misconduct, harsh, divisive and non sense speech, covetousness, ill will and wrong views
> 
> These ten ways of doing unskillful deeds are impure and make things impure
>
