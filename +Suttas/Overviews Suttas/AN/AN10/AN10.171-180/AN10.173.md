# AN10.173 - Adhamma - Bad Principles
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> One should understand what is the teaching and what is contrary to the teaching

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Killing living, stealing, lying, sexual misconduct, harsh, divisive and non sense speech, covetousness, ill will and wrong views
> 
> You should know bad principles and good principles. And you should know bad results and good results. Knowing these things, your practice should follow the good principles with good results.
>
