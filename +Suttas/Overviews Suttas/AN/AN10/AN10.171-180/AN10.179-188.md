# AN10.179-188 - SÄdhu - Good
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> What is not good, not the teaching of the noble ones, not the dhamma, unskillful, harmful, defiled, blameworthy, mortifying, leading to accumulation, and has suffering as outcome and results

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Killing living, stealing, lying, sexual misconduct, harsh, divisive and non sense speech, covetousness, ill will and wrong views
> 
> Abstaining from these are the opposite qualities
