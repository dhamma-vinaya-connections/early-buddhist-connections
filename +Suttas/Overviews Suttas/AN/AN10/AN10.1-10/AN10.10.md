# AN10.10 - Jhāna - Inspiring All Around:
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ten factors, beginning with faith, that a bhikkhu should fulfill.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Faith, ethics, learned, frequent and teaching Dhamma to assembles, Dhamma speakers, experts in training, 4 jhanas, stay in remote lodging and in the wilderness, 4 immaterial jhanas, 3 higher knowledge and  having realized the ending of defilements
> 
> Who is incomplete in any respect  should fulfill it, thinking: ‘how can I become complete.   and who has these ten qualities is inspiring all around
>
