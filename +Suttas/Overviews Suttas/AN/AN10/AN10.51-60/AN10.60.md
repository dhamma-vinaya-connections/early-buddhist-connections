# AN10.60 - Girimananda - With GirimÄnanda
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  instructs ven. Girimananda, who is ill, on the ten themes of meditation that can heal both mind and bod.  in detail

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Our minds will be consolidated in the perceptions of impermanence, not-self,   ugliness,  drawbacks, giving up, fading away, cessation, dissatisfaction with the whole world, non-desire for all conditions, and mindfulness of breathing.
> 
> Then after girimÄnanda heard these ten perceptions his illness died down on the spot
