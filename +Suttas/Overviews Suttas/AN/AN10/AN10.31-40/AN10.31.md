# AN10.31 - Upāli - With Upali
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  teaches venerable Upāli the ten reasons for establishing the training rules

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Training rules for well being and comfort of the saṅgha; keeping difficult persons in check and for the comfort of good-hearted bhikkhus. For restraining defilements present and future; inspiring and increasing  confidence; continuation of the true teaching and the support of the training
> 
> The realized one laid down training rules for his disciples and recited the monastic code for ten reasons
>
