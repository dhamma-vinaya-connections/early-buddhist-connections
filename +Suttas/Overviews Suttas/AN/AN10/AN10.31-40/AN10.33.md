# AN10.33 - Ubbāhikā - Judge
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ten qualities a bhikkhu should have to be appointed to judge a dispute

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Judge must be: ethical, learned, mastered both monastic codes, firm and unfaltering in the training, they’re able to persuade, skilled in raising and settling disciplinary issue, know what a disciplinary issue is, how originates, how ceases and practical way leading to the cessation of a disciplinary issue
> 
> bhikkhu should have these ten qualities to be agreed on as a judge
>
