# AN10.34 - Upasampadā - Ordination
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ten qualities a bhikkhu should have to give ordination, dependence and to be attended by a novice.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> is ethical … they’re learned … mastered both monastic codes, able to care for the sick, to settle dissatisfaction, dispel remorse, dissuade misconception,  encourage someone in the higher ethics, the higher mind, and the higher wisdom
> 
> They should be able to do so or get someone else to do so
>
