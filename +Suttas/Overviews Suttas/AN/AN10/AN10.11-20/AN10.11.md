# AN10.11 - Senāsana - Lodgings
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Five factors that a bhikkhu should have, and five factors of a lodging that they should occupy

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Noble disciple has faith, is healthy,  not devious or deceitful, live with energy roused up for giving up unskillful qualities and embracing skillful qualities and have the wisdom of arising and passing away
> 
> Lodging is neither too far nor too near, not bothered by people, little disturbance from animals, requisites are easy to come by, and there  are several senior bhikkhus who are very learned
>
