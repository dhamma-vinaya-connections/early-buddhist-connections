# AN10.19 - DutiyaariyÄvÄsa - Abodes Of The Noble Ones
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ten noble abodes in which the noble ones of the past, present and future abide, in detail

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Mendicant has given up five factors, possesses six factors, has a single guard, has four supports, has eliminated idiosyncratic interpretations of the truth, has totally given up searching, has unsullied intentions, has stilled the physical process, and is well freed in mind and well freed by wisdom
> 
> There are these ten noble abodes in which the noble ones of the past, present, and future abide
