# AN10.14 - Cetokhila - Emotional Barrenness
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Five kinds of emotional barrenness and five emotional shackles

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Emotional barrenness has doubts about the Buddha ,  teaching … the saṅgha … the training … a bhikkhu is angry and upset with their spiritual companions
> 
> Emotional shackles: craving for sensual pleasures, greed for the body, for forms, they eat much and then sleep and live the spiritual life wishing to be reborn in one of the orders of gods
>
