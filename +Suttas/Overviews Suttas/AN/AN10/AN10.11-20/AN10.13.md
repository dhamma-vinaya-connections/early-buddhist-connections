> [!info] Summary
> The ten fetters that bind us to the cycle of birth and death

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Lower fetters: identity view, doubt, misapprehension of precepts and observances, sensual desire, and ill will
> 
> Higher fetters: desire for rebirth in the realm of luminous form, desire for rebirth in the formless realm, conceit, restlessness, and ignorance
