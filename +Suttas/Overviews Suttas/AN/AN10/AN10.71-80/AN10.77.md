# AN10.77 - Nigaṇṭha - Jains
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A bad monk has ten bad qualities, like a crow

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Rude and impudent, gluttonous and voracious, cruel and pitiless, weak and raucous, unmindful and acquisitive. A Bad bhikkhu has these ten bad qualities.”
> 
> Jain ascetics are faithless and unethical, without conscience or prudence, and devoted to bad persons. They Glorify themselves, attached to their own views, deceptive, with bad desires and bad friend
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Crows bad qualities, like bad bhikkhus qualities
