# AN10.82 - Ānanda - With Ānanda
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A bhikkhu cannot achieve progress if they have ten bad qualities starting with lack of faith

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Achieve growth have faith, ethics, learned, energy, mindfulness, easy to admonish, with good friends, contented,
Of few desires and right views
> 
> It is possible for a bhikkhu with these ten qualities to achieve growth, improvement, or maturity
>
