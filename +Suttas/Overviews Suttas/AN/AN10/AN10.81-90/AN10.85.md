# AN10.85 - AdhimÄna - Overestimation
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> When a mendicant declares enlightenment or brags about attainments , the Buddha  or another accomplished meditator examines them

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> When someone  is unethical, unfaithful, uneducated, unmindful, hard to admonish, has bad friends, lazy,  is hard to look after and witless that means decline
> 
> When someone overestimates because of they are learned, or they are covetous, with ill will, dullness and drowsiness, restless, doubts, relishes work, talk, company or sleep, that means decline
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Someone lying to someone else
