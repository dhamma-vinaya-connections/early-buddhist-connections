# AN10.70 - Kathavatthu - Discussion Topics

> [!info] Summary
> The monks are sitting around chatting about this and that. The Buddha  admonishes them and teaches ten topics of conversation suitable for mendicants

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Talk on modesty, on contentment, on seclusion, on non-entanglement, on arousing persistence, on virtue, on concentration, on discernment, on release, and on the knowledge & vision of release.
> 
> If you were to engage repeatedly in these ten topics of conversation, you would outshine even the sun & moon, so mighty, so powerful â€” to say nothing of the wanderers of other sects."
