# AN10.135-144 - SÄdhu - Good
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> What is good, the teaching of the noble ones, skillful, beneficial, the Dhamma is the noble path

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Right view, right thought, right speech, right action, right livelihood, right effort, right mindfulness, right immersion, right knowledge, and right freedom
> 
> The wrong path is defiled, blameworthy, mortifying, leads to accumulation and suffering
