# AN10.113 - Adhamma - Bad Principles
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> One should understand what is the teaching and what is contrary to the teaching

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Right view gives rise to right thought, gives rise to right speech, gives rise to right action, gives rise to right livelihood, livelihood gives rise to right effort gives rise to right mindfulness, gives rise to right immersion gives rise to right knowledge, gives rise to right freedom
> 
> You should know bad principles with bad results. And you should know good principles with good results. Knowing these things, your practice should follow the good principles with good results
