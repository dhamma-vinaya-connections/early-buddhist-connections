# AN10.28 - Mahapañha Sutta - Great Questions 2nd
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Some lay people ask the nun from kajaṅgalā regarding the ten “great questions”. Though she has not heard this teaching herself, she explains from her own understanding, and is praised by the Buddha .

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> 1 all sentient beings are sustained by food, 2 name and form, 3 types of feelings, 4 kinds of mindfulness meditation 5 faculties, 6 elements of escape, 7 awakening factors, 8 noble eightfold path, 9 abodes of sentient beings, 10 ways of performing skillful deeds
> 
> Becoming completely disillusioned, dispassionate, and freed regarding this one thing, seeing its limits and fully comprehending its meaning, a bhikkhu makes an end of suffering in this very life. ‘
>
