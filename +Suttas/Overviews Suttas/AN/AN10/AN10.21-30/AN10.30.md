> [!info] Summary
> King pasenadi returns victorious from battle and immediately goes to see the Buddha , bowing reverentially to his feet, and declaring ten reasons for his love and devotion to the Buddha

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The Buddha  is practicing for the welfare and happiness of the people, is ethical, secluded, is content, worthy of offerings, teaches Â self-effacement, gets 4 jhanas, recollects past lives, sees sentient beings passing away and being reborn, has realized the undefiled freedom of heart and freedom by wisdom
