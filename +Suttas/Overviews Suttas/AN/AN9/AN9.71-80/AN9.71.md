# AN9.71 - Cetokhila - Emotional Barrenness
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> How to overcome emotional barrenness and shackles

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Emotional barrenness are doubts in the teacher, dhamma, Sangha, training and resentment to spiritual companions and shackles are craving for sensual pleasures, greed for the body and forms, overeating and desire to reborn in higher realms
> 
> To give up these fetters  you should develop the four kinds of mindfulness meditation.
