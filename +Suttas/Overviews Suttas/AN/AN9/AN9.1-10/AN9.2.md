# AN9.2 - Nissaya - Support
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The things that a mendicant relies on as supports in spiritual practice

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Faith, conscience, prudence, energy, and wisdom, after reflection, use, endures, avoids, and gets rid of some things
> 
> Supported by these things  gives up the unskillful and develops the skillful, the unskillful is actually given up by them
