# AN9.41 - Tapussa - Householder Tapussa
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The householder tapussa reflects that it is renunciation that distinguishes lay from monastic. The Buddha  responds by giving a long account of his practice of absorption before awakening

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Renunciation, 4 jhanas, dimension of infinite space, infinite consciousness, nothingness, neither perception nor non-perception, cessation of perception and feeling
> 
> Having seen the drawback of lesser attainments  I pursue the next ; and having understood the reward, I develop that next attainment
