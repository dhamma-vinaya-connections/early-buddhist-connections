> [!info] Summary
> I do not see a single thing that prevents specific hindrance from arising, or, when it has arisen, abandons it

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Ugliness vs ensual desire, love vs ill will, being energetic vs dullness and drowsiness, peace of mind vs restlessness and remorse, proper attention vs doubts
> 
> When you don't attend to antidotes, hindrances  arise, and once arisen they increase and grow
