# AN1.333-346 - Catuttha - Fourth
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The rare conditions for being able to practice the dhamma

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Beings reborn as humans are few, in civilized countries less, able to distinguish what is well said less, who have the noble eye of wisdom less, get to see a realized one or hear his teachings and training even less and who get the essence of the meaning, the essence of the teaching, and the essence of freedom
> 
> Of those beings who remember the teachings they hear are few, who examine the meaning less, who  understand the meaning and the teaching and practice accordingly less, who strive effectively less, who relying on letting go, gain samadhi less,
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Delightful parks, and places are few like those who get the essence of the dhamma
