# AN1.306-315 - Dutiya - Second
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> When a person has wrong view, all their path development is wrong. But when they have right view, everything good follows

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Proper attention gives rise to right view, it gives rise and increase skillful qualities, that causes to be reborn  in a good place
> 
> When an individual has right view, whatever bodily, verbal, or mental deeds they undertake in line with that view, their intentions, aims, wishes, and choices all lead to what is likable
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Seed of sugar cane lead to its sweet taste, like right views leat to beneficial effects
