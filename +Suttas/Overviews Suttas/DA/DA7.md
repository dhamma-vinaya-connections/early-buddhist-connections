## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> This is a long and entertaining debate between a monk and a skeptic, who went to elaborate and bizarre lengths to prove that there is no such thing as an afterlife. The discourse contains a colorful series of parables and examples

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> How to refute wrong view of nihilism and to let go of wrong views
How to get the highest merits of giving
> 
> Proves of afterlife, the harm & suffering of not letting go misconception about it and how to abandon it
Give gifts carefully, thoughtfully, with your own hands, not giving the dregs
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Moon & sun, the bandit, the sewer, gods of 33, blind from birth, the pregnant woman, the dream, the hot iron ball, the horn blower and the fire worshiper ascetic like not believing in  rebirth. the 2 caravan leaders, the dung carrier, the gamblers and  the man who carried hemp like don't letting go wrong views
