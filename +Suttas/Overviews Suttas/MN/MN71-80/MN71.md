# MN71 - Tevijjavaccha Sutta - Vacchagotta on the Three Knowledges
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  denies being omniscient, and sets forth the three higher knowledges that form the core of his awakened insight

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The ascetic gotama has the three knowledges
> 
> Whenever I want, I recollect my many kinds of past lives, whenever I want, with clairvoyance that is purified and superhuman, I see sentient beings passing away and being reborn, and I have realized the undefiled freedom of heart and freedom by wisdom in this very life due Â to the ending of defilements
