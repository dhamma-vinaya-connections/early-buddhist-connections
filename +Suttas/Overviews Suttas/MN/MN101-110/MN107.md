> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  compares the training of an accountant with the step by step spiritual path of his followers. But even with such a well explained path, the Buddha  can only show the way, and it is up to us to walk it.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> possible to similarly describe a gradual training, gradual progress, and gradual practice in this teaching and training
> 
> Ethics under pattimokka; restraint 6 senses, eat moderately and reflect on it, practice wakefulness,  keep mindful in every situation ; seclusion; cleanse 5 hindrances; 4 jhannas
>

> Training of an accountant like the gradual training
