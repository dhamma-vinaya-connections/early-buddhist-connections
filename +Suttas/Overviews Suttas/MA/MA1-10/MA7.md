## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ven Cunda ask the Buddha about the sources for worldly and transcendent merits

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> 7 way for worldly merits: offering dwelling, bedding, robes, food, medicines, other things to the Sangha
> 
> 7 ways for transcend merits: hear about, go see, pay respect,  offering, to the Buddha, take refuge and precepts
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Ocean and river can not be measured, like immeasurable merits
