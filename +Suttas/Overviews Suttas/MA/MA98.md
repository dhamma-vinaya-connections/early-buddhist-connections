> [!info] Summary
> Here the Buddha  details the seventh factor of the noble eightfold path, mindfulness meditation. This collects many of the meditation teachings found throughout the canon, especially the foundational practices focusing on the body, and is regarded as one of the most important meditation discourses

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> All Buddhas have attained realization of unsurpassable awakening by abandoning the five hindrances 
by dwelling with the mind well settled in the four establishments of mind-fulness, and by developing the seven factors of awakening
Feelings pleasant, painful, neutral, bodily, mental 
worldly, unworldly,  
sensual, non-sensual,
