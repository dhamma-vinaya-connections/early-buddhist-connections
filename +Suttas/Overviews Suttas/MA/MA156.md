> [!info] Summary
> Difference between ancient and misbehaving brahmins

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Ancient brahmins practiced the pure holy life. 
Practicing celibacy and morality 
Their minds shone with forgiveness and harmlessness. 
Patient and forbearing, they guarded their thoughts
> 
> The subtle sensual pleasures of society 
[Became] for brahmins a constant source of longing
custom of undertaking sacrifices 
Was the principal transgression. 
There is no reason to kill.
