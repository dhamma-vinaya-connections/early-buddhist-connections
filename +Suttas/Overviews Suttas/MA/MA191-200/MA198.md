# MA198 - 調御地 - Stages of Taming
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> By analogy with the taming of an elephant, the Buddha  explains how he tames his disciples.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> If a bhikkhu passes away having ended the defilements—whether as a senior, middle, or junior—they’re considered as a bhikkhu who passed away tamed.                  This should be expanded as in mn 107
> 
> Listen to the Buddha  dhamma, conviction, give away posessions, renounce sensual pleasures, good conduct body speech and mind, sati sampajjano of body, felling , mind & mental contents, 4 jhannas, imperturbability , gaining ultimate freedom
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Taming of an elephant like the gradual training
