# MA26 - 瞿尼師 - With Gulissāni
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A monk comes down to the community from the wilderness, but doesn’t behave properly. Venerable Sāriputta explains how a bhikkhu should behave, whether in forest or town

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> These things should be undertaken and followed by wilderness monks, and still more by those who live in the neighborhood of a village
> 
> Have respect for his spiritual companions, shouldn’t enter the village too early or return too late in the day, shouldn’t socialize with families before or after the meal, shouldn’t be restless, shouldn’t be gossipy and loose-tongued, be easy to admonish, with good friends, guard the sense doors, eat in moderation, be committed to wakefulness, be energetic, mindful, with samadhi, wise, make an effort to learn the teaching and training, practice meditation to realize the peaceful liberations that are formless and practice meditation to realize the superhuman state,
>
