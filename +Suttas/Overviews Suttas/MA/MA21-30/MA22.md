# MA22 - 成就戒 - Perfecting the Precepts
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Qualities should a senior bhikkhu have to be dear and beloved to their spiritual companions

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Ethical, learned, good speaker , get 4 jhanas and undefiled freedom
> 
> bhikkhu who is accomplished in ethics, immersion, and wisdom. They might enter into and emerge from the cessation of perception and feeling
>
