> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha explains which states can be extinguish by body, speech and by wisdom and seeing

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Why do you adhere to body and verbal unwholesome actions?  abandon
unwholesome bodily and verbal action and cultivate wholesome bodily action,
> 
> Covetousness, disputatiousness, . . . anger, . . . ill-will, . . . uncom-municativeness, . . . avarice, . . . envy, . . . deceit, . . . flattery, . . . lack of 
shame and scruples, . . . evil desires and evil views are extinguished only by wisdom and vision
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Lotus is born in water but rises above and is not attached 
to the water, like arahants
