> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha , speaking in reference to the monk kalandaka, explains ten qualities that lead to discord or unity

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Who likes disputes, evil desires, breaks precepts, ill-will, . . .uncommunicativenes, avarice, . . . envy, . . . flattery, . . . deceit, . . . lacks shame and scruples
> 
> He is not likeable, cannot be respected. It is not conducive to training,
to restraint, to being a renunciant, to attaining 
mental unification, not conducive to attaining nirvana
>
