## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  explains to a brahmin how your deeds in past lives affect you in this life

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> It is the way people live that makes them how they are. Sentient beings are the owners of their deeds and heir to their deeds. Deeds are their womb, their relative, and their refuge.Â It Is deeds that divide beings intoinferior and superior
> 
> The way leading to a short life: to be a killer of living beings, to a long life: to have abandoned the killing, to sickliness: to be one who harms beings, to health: not to be one who harms beings, to ugliness: to be ill-tempered & easily upset, to beauty: not to be ill-tempered or easily upset; not being influential: to be envious, being influential: not to be envious, to poverty: not to be a giver, great wealth: to be a giver,  a low birth: to be obstinate & arrogant, to a high birth: not to obstinate or arrogant, to stupidity: when visiting a  contemplative, not to ask, to discernment asking
