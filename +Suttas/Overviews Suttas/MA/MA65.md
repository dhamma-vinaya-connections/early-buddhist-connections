## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha explains different types of indulgences for bhikkhus  similar to some animals

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Bhikkhu who beg for food, without protecting himself, without guarding
his sense faculties, without establishing right mindfulness. he obtains requisites and becomes attached to them, seduced by them, not seeing the danger in them
> 
> Bhikkhu who beg for food,  protecting himself, guarding
his sense faculties, establishing right mindfulness. he obtains requisites and he eliminates covetousness and has no avarice in his mind
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Simile of otter, vulture, bird that feeds on vomit, jackal, raven and ape, like different types of bhikkhus
