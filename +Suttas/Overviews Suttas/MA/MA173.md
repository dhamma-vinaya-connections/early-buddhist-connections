> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Does the desire for awakening get in the way of awakening? According to this discourse, the question of desiring or not desiring is irrelevant as long as one develops the appropriate qualities that constitute the path to awakening. The discourse is also very clear on the point that there are right and wrong paths of practice
