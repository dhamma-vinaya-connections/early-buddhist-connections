## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Anuruddha ask for help to Ananda to make new robes. The Buddha and the Sangha also came to help. In gratitude Anuruddha explained the gradual training.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Detailed explanation on sila, contentment, restraint of the senses, mindfulness on activities, abandoning the hindrances, 4 jhanas, psychic powers, 3 knowledges and liberation
> 
> Relying on the precepts, estab-lished in the precepts, and using the precepts as his ladder he wishes to 
ascend to the hall of unsurpassable wisdom, to the pavilion of the true 
Dharma, that is certainly possible.
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Stairs to second floor of a hall, like steps in the gradual training
