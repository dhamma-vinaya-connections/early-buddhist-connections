## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> If deeds were experienced exactly as performed, there could be no spiritual practice

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Person has not cultivated the body, virtue, mind, wisdom,
having done unwholesome actions, must experience suffering as their fruit by
[being reborn in] hell as a result
> 
> Person has cultivated the body, virtue, mind, wisdom,
having done unwholesome actions, must experience little suffering as their fruit in this life
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Similes of lump of sal,  rich and poor thieves and sheep dealer
