# MA143 - 傷歌邏 - With Saṅgārava
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> A brahmin argues that a sacrifice is less selfish than the bhikkhu’s life. And the king’s men are deploring the decline in psychic abilities among the saṅgha.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Miracles of psychic power, of reading others minds, and of instruction.
> 
> Miracle of instruction “Come, all of you, and walk by yourselves a path, a track such that, you will eradicate all 
taints and attain the taintless liberation
>
