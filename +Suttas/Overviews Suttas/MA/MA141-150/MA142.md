# MA142 - 雨勢 - To Vassakāra
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  teaches the seven principles of non-decline to the licchavis at the sārandada shrine near vesālī
The Buddha  teaches the seven principles of non-decline to the bhikkhus
Seven qualities leading to the decline of a bhikkhu who is a trainee

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Meet frequently, live in harmony, follow the traditional principles, honor elders, don’t rape or abduct women, venerate the shrines and give shelter to arahants
Meet frequently, live in harmony, follow the traditional rules, honor elders, don’t craving for future lives, live in wilderness lodgings, individually establish mindfulness, so that more good-hearted spiritual come
Not relishing work, talk, sleep, and company, being easy to admonish, and having good friends, don’t have wicked desires, and don’t stop half-way, guard the sense doors and don’t they eat too much, respect elders
> 
> Meet frequently, live in harmony, follow the traditional rules, honor elders, don’t craving for future lives, live in wilderness lodgings, individually establish mindfulness, so that more good-hearted spiritual come
As long as these seven principles that prevent decline last among bhikkhus they can expect growth, not decline
Faithful … conscientious …prudent … learned … energetic … mindful … wise and practice the 7 awakening factors
>
