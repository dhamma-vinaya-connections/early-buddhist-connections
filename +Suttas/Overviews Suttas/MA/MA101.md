> [!info] Summary
> In a practical meditation teaching, the Buddha  describes five different approaches to stopping thoughts

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The Buddha  offers five practical methods of responding wisely to unskillful thoughts (thoughts connected with desire, aversion, or delusion).
> 
> Substitute for skillful thoughts, examine the drawbacks, ignore and forget about them, focus on stopping the formation of thoughts and subduing the evil mind by the good mind
