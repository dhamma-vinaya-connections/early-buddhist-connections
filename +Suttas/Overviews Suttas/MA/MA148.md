# MA148 - ä½•è‹¦ - What Is Suffering
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha explains happiness and unhappiness of lay, bhikkhus and devas to brahmin Janussoni
The Buddha explains how to regard good and bad friends to brahmin Janussoni

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> For one who lives at home, 
constraint is suffering; for one who has gone forth to train in the path, lack of constraint is suffering and opposites are happiness 
Who has bad friends lose their 
faith, lose their keeping of the precepts, their wide learning, their generosity, and their wisdom
> 
> When devas and human beings are able to engage in acts that are in accordance 
with the Dharma, acts that are not evil, this is certain to lead to their flourishing, and to their happiness
Who has good friends gain faith, devotion and respect. Their practice is appropriate. 
They establish right comprehension and they progress in the Dharma in 
accordance with the Dharma
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Moon waxing like good friends and wining like bad friends
