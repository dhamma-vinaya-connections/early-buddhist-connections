# EA46.9 - EA 46.9 - 9. Ten Concepts
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Ten meditative perceptions that lead to the deathless

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Perceptions of ugliness, death, repulsiveness of food, dissatisfaction with the whole world, impermanence, suffering in impermanence, and not-self in suffering, giving up, fading away, and cessation
> 
> Perceptions of impermanence, not-self, death, repulsiveness of food, dissatisfaction with the whole world, a skeleton, a worm-infested corpse, a livid corpse, a split open corpse, and a bloated corpse.
