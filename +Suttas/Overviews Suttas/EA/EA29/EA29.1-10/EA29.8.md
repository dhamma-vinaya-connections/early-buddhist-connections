# EA29.8 - EA 29.8 - 8. ?
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Sources of craving for bhikkhus

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Robes, alms-food, lodgings, or rebirth in this or that state.
> 
> Knowing this drawback—
That craving is the cause of suffering—
Rid of craving, by not grasping
>
