# EA44.1 - EA 44.1 - 1. Nine Abodes of Sentient Beings
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Karma thatâ€™s dark, bright, both, and neither, explained with the grave violations and the paths of wholesome deeds

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Dark deeds with dark results;
Bright deeds with bright results;
Dark and bright deeds with dark and bright results; and
Neither dark nor bright deeds with neither dark nor bright results, which lead to the ending of deeds.
> 
> Dark are hurtful, and reborn in bad place, bright are pleasing and reborn in heavens, doing both are reborn humans and neither is the noble path and awakening factors
