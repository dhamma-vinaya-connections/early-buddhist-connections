# EA17.1 - EA 17.1 - Rāhula
## 🌐 Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Breath meditation is very beneficial. It is developed by going into seclusion, establishing mindfulness on the breath, and proceeding through sixteen stages

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Mindfulness of breathing is developed and cultivated in this way you can expect one of two results: enlightenment in the present life, or if there’s something left over, non-return.”
> 
> bhikkhu develops mindfulness of breathing together with the awakening factors
>
