# EA49.3 - EA 49.3 - 3. ?
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> People of poor character associate together, while people of good character associate together.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> In present, past and future, too, sentient beings will come together and converge because of their properties
> 
> Lacking faith and faithful; those unafraid and, those afraid of wrongdoing; the unlearned, the learned;  the lazy, the energetic;  the muddle-minded, the mindful concentrated and dispersed; in right and wrong paths
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Milk comes together with milk, excrement comes together  with excrement
