# EA31.11 - EA 31.11 - 11. No Conflict
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> The Buddha  invites Kassapa to teach the mendicants, but he is reluctant, saying that the mendicants have become stubborn and their good qualities are declining

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Don't compete in studies to see who can recite more and better and longer
> 
> Whoever has faith, conscience, prudence, energy, and wisdom when it comes to skillful qualities can expect growth, not decline,
> 

> [!info] Similes
> Moon waxes, like skillful qualities grow
