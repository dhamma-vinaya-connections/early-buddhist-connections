# EA24.5 - EA 24.5 - Miraculous Transformations
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> What are you searching for? Are you looking for happiness in all the wrong places? Are you looking for a lasting, noble happiness?

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Ignoble is  someone liable to grow old, get sick, die and is defiled searches for things with the same nature
> 
> Noble is  someone liable to grow old, get sick, die and is defiled, knowing the drawback in what is defiled, searches for the undefiled supreme sanctuary, extinguishment
