# ITI73 - Santatara - More Peaceful
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Three properties for escape and degrees of peace

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Renunciation from sensuality, formless from form, cessation and from whatever is fabricated and dependently co-arisen.
> 
> Formless phenomena are more peaceful than forms; cessation, more peaceful than formless phenomena.
