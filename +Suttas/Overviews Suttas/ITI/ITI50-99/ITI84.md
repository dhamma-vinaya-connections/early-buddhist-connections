# ITI84 - Bahujanahita - For Welfare of People
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Three people who appear for the benefit of the world.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> The Teacher, Great Seer,
is first in the world;
following him, the disciple
with mind composed;
and then the erudite one, who follows the practice for one in training,
> 
> These three, chief
among beings divine &human,
giving light, proclaiming the Dhamma,
throw open the door to the Deathless,
>
