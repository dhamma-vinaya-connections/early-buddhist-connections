# ITI98 - Dana - Giving
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Two kinds of gifts, sharing, and assistance: in material things and in Dhamma.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Of these two kinds of gifts, this is supreme: a gift of the Dhamma.
> 
> Both for those who proclaim it
and those who listen,
confident in the message of the One Well-Gone:
it purifies their foremost benefit
