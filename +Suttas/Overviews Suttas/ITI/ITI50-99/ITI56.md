# ITI56 - Asava - Defilements
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Defilements of sensuality, of becoming, of ignorance.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> His effluent of sensuality
ended,
his ignorance
faded away,
his effluent of becoming
exhausted
> 
> With the ending of effluents, a monk
free from hunger
is totally unbound.
