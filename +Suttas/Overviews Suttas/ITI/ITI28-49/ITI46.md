> [!info] Summary
> Live enjoying aloofness, delighting in aloofness, inwardly committed to awareness-tranquility, not neglecting jhÄna, endowed with clear-seeing insight, and frequenting empty buildings.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Live with the trainings as your reward, with discernment uppermost, with release the essence, and with mindfulness the governing principle
