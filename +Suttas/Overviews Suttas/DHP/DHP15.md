# DHP15 - Sukhavagga - Happiness
## ğŸŒ Overview

> [!info] Summary
> Victory begets enmity; the defeated dwell in pain. Happily the peaceful live, discarding both victory and defeat.

> [!info] Key Excerpts 
> Hunger is the worst illness, 
conditions are the worst suffering, 
For one who truly knows this, 
extinguishment is the ultimate happiness
> 
> Health is the most precious gain and contentment the greatest wealth. A trustworthy person is the best kinsman, Nibbana the highest happiness
