## SN45-56 - Great Book - Mahāvagga

### 📂 SN46 - Bojjhaṅgasaṁyutta - Awakening Factors

> [!example]- 🌐[[SN46.1]] - 📜[Available texts](obsidian://search?query=file:sn46.1-) - Himavantasutta – Himalaya
> Dragons nurture their strength in the himalayas, then enter the rivers and reach the ocean. So too, a bhikkhu nurtures ethics and then develops the seven awakening factors


> [!example]- 🌐[[SN46.2]] - 📜[Available texts](obsidian://search?query=file:sn46.2-) - Kāyasutta – Body
> Just as the body depends on food, the awakening factors depend on nutriment. The Buddha  gives specific conditions for each of the factors


> [!example]- 🌐[[SN46.3]] - 📜[Available texts](obsidian://search?query=file:sn46.3-) - Sīlasutta – Ethics
> Here the awakening factors are described in the context of hearing the teachings and reflecting on them. This leads to full enlightenment, or at least to some lesser attainment


> [!example]- 🌐[[SN46.4]] - 📜[Available texts](obsidian://search?query=file:sn46.4-) - Vatthasutta – Clothes
