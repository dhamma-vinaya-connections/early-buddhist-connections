#  THAG - Theragāthā

### THAG1 - Ekakanipātavagga - Ones Book
> [!example]- 🌐[[THAG1]] - 📜[Available texts](obsidian://search?query=file:thag1-) - Ekakanipātavagga – Ones Book 
>  Deceived by conceit,
>  Defiled by conditions,
>  Distressed by gain and loss,
>  They don’t realise samādhi.
