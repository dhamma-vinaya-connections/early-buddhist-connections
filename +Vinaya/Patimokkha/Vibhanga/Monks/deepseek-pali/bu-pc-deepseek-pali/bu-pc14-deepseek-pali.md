# BU-PC 14

## 4. Paṭhamasenāsanasikkhāpadaṃ

## 4.The First Training Rule on Lodgings.

108. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhū hemantike kāle ajjhokāse senāsanaṃ paññapetvā kāyaṃ otāpentā kāle ārocite taṃ pakkamantā neva uddhariṃsu na uddharāpesuṃ, anāpucchā pakkamiṃsu. Senāsanaṃ ovaṭṭhaṃ hoti. Ye te bhikkhū appicchā…pe… te ujjhāyanti khiyyanti vipācenti – ‘‘kathaṃ hi nāma bhikkhū ajjhokāse senāsanaṃ paññapetvā taṃ pakkamantā neva uddharissanti na uddharāpessanti, anāpucchā pakkamissanti, senāsanaṃ ovaṭṭha’’nti! Atha kho te bhikkhū te anekapariyāyena vigarahitvā bhagavato etamatthaṃ ārocesuṃ…pe… saccaṃ kira, bhikkhave, bhikkhū ajjhokāse…pe… evañca pana, bhikkhave, imaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ uddiseyyātha –

108.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī in Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. Now at that time, during the winter season, the bhikkhus prepared lodgings in the open air, warmed themselves in the sun, and when the time was announced, they departed without removing it or having it removed, and they left without asking permission. The lodging was rained on. The bhikkhus who had few desires … complained, grumbled, and criticized: “How indeed can these bhikkhus prepare lodgings in the open air and then depart without removing it or having it removed, and leave without asking permission, so that the lodging is rained on?” Then those bhikkhus criticized the offending bhikkhus in many ways and reported the matter to the Blessed One. … “Is it true, bhikkhus, that the bhikkhus did this?” … “And thus, bhikkhus, you should recite this training rule:

109. ‘‘Yo pana bhikkhu saṅghikaṃ mañcaṃ vā pīṭhaṃ vā bhisiṃ vā kocchaṃ vā ajjhokāse santharitvā vā santharāpetvā vā taṃ pakkamanto neva uddhareyya na uddharāpeyya, anāpucchaṃ vā gaccheyya, pācittiya’’nti.

109.“If a bhikkhu, having spread out or having had spread out a Sangha-owned bed, stool, mattress, or pillow in the open air, then departing, neither removes it nor has it removed, or departs without asking permission, it is an offense entailing confession.”

Evañcidaṃ bhagavatā bhikkhūnaṃ sikkhāpadaṃ paññattaṃ hoti.

Thus was this training rule laid down for the bhikkhus by the Blessed One.

110. Tena kho pana samayena bhikkhū ajjhokāse vasitvā kālasseva senāsanaṃ abhiharanti. Addasā kho bhagavā te bhikkhū kālasseva senāsanaṃ abhiharante. Disvāna etasmiṃ nidāne etasmiṃ pakaraṇe dhammiṃ kathaṃ katvā bhikkhū āmantesi – ‘‘anujānāmi, bhikkhave, aṭṭha māse avassikasaṅkete [vasituṃ avassikasaṃkete (itipi)] maṇḍape vā rukkhamūle vā yattha kākā vā kulalā vā na ūhadanti tattha senāsanaṃ nikkhipitu’’nti.

110.At that time bhikkhus, having dwelt in the open air, would remove their lodgings very early in the morning. The Blessed One saw those bhikkhus removing their lodgings early in the morning. Having seen this, on this occasion and in this connection, he gave a Dhamma talk and addressed the bhikkhus: “bhikkhus, I allow you to place lodgings for eight months in a place designated as non-rainy, in a pavilion or at the foot of a tree where crows or ospreys do not defile it.”

111. Yo panāti yo yādiso…pe… bhikkhūti…pe… ayaṃ imasmiṃ atthe adhippeto bhikkhūti.

111.“Yo panāti” means whoever, of whatever sort… “bhikkhūti”… in this connection, the term “bhikkhu” is intended.

Saṅghikaṃ nāma saṅghassa dinnaṃ hoti pariccattaṃ.

What is given to the Sangha, relinquished, is called Sangha-owned.

Mañco nāma cattāro mañcā – masārako, bundikābaddho, kuḷīrapādako, āhaccapādako.

A bed means four kinds of beds: one with crossbars, one with joined crossbars, one with crab-like legs, and one with tenoned legs.

Pīṭhaṃ nāma cattāri pīṭhāni – masārakaṃ, bundikābaddhaṃ, kuḷīrapādakaṃ, āhaccapādakaṃ.

A stool is of four kinds: a stool with crossbars, a stool with joined crossbars, a stool with crab-like legs, and a stool with tenoned legs.

Bhisi nāma pañca bhisiyo – uṇṇabhisi, coḷabhiti, vākabhisi, tiṇabhisi, paṇṇabhisi.

A mattress means five kinds of mattresses: a wool mattress, a cloth mattress, a bark mattress, a grass mattress, and a leaf mattress.

Kocchaṃ nāma – vākamayaṃ vā usīramayaṃ vā muñjamayaṃ vā pabbajamayaṃ [babbajamayaṃ (sī.)] vā anto saṃveṭhetvā baddhaṃ hoti.

A pillow means one made of bark or of fragrant grass, or of muñja grass, or of rushes, bound together inside and tied.

Santharitvāti sayaṃ santharitvā.

Having spread it out: Having spread it out oneself.

Santharāpetvāti aññaṃ santharāpetvā. Anupasampannaṃ santharāpeti, tassa palibodho. Upasampannaṃ santharāpeti, santhārakassa [santhatassa (itipi)] palibodho.

Having had it spread out means having had another spread it out. If he has it spread out by one who is not fully ordained, it is an impediment for the one who spread it out. If he has it spread out by one who is fully ordained, it is an impediment for the one who spread it out.

Taṃ pakkamanto neva uddhareyyāti na sayaṃ uddhareyya.

When departing, he should not remove it, meaning he should not remove it himself.

Na uddharāpeyyāti na aññaṃ uddharāpeyya.

Nor should he have it removed, meaning he should not cause another to remove it.

Anāpucchaṃ vā gaccheyyāti bhikkhuṃ vā sāmaṇeraṃ vā ārāmikaṃ vā anāpucchā majjhimassa purisassa leḍḍupātaṃ atikkamantassa āpatti pācittiyassa.

Or he should depart without asking permission: If he departs without asking permission of a bhikkhu, a novice, or a monastery attendant, going beyond the distance a man can throw a clod of dirt, he commits an offense entailing confession.

112. Saṅghike saṅghikasaññī ajjhokāse santharitvā vā santharāpetvā vā taṃ pakkamanto neva uddhareyya na uddharāpeyya anāpucchaṃ vā gaccheyya, āpatti pācittiyassa. Saṅghike vematiko…pe… saṅghike puggalikasaññī ajjhokāse santharitvā vā santharāpetvā vā taṃ pakkamanto neva uddhareyya na uddharāpeyya, anāpucchaṃ vā gaccheyya, āpatti pācittiyassa.

112.If, perceiving it as Sangha-owned, he has spread out or had spread out a Sangha-owned lodging in the open air, when departing he neither removes it himself nor has it removed, or departs without asking permission, he commits an offense entailing confession. If he is in doubt [whether it is Sangha-owned], he commits an offense entailing confession. If, when it is Sangha-owned, he perceives it as belonging to an individual, having spread out or had spread out [a Sangha-owned lodging] in the open air, when departing he neither removes it himself nor has it removed, or departs without asking permission, he commits an offense entailing confession.

Cimilikaṃ vā uttarattharaṇaṃ vā bhūmattharaṇaṃ vā taṭṭikaṃ vā cammakhaṇḍaṃ vā pādapuñchaniṃ vā phalakapīṭhaṃ vā ajjhokāse santharitvā vā santharāpetvā vā taṃ pakkamanto neva uddhareyya na uddharāpeyya, anāpucchaṃ vā gaccheyya, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Puggalike saṅghikasaññī, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Puggalike vematiko, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Puggalike puggalikasaññī aññassa puggalike, āpatti dukkaṭassa. Attano puggalike anāpatti.

Having spread out or had spread out in the open air a figured rug, an upper-sheet, a floor covering, a mat, a piece of leather, a foot-wiper, or a wooden stool, when departing he neither removes it himself nor has it removed, or departs without asking permission, he commits an offense of wrong conduct. If, when it belongs to an individual, he perceives it as belonging to the Sangha, he commits an offense of wrong conduct. If he is in doubt [whether it belongs to an individual], he commits an offense of wrong conduct. If, when it belongs to another individual, he perceives it as belonging to an individual, he commits an offense of wrong conduct. If it belongs to himself, there is no offense.

113. Anāpatti uddharitvā gacchati, uddharāpetvā gacchati, āpucchaṃ gacchati, otāpento gacchati, kenaci palibuddhaṃ hoti, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

113.There is no offense if he departs having removed it, departs having had it removed, departs having asked permission, departs while warming it in the sun, if there is an obstruction by something, in emergencies, for one who is insane, for the first offender.

Paṭhamasenāsanasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ catutthaṃ.

The first training rule on lodgings, the fourth, is finished.
