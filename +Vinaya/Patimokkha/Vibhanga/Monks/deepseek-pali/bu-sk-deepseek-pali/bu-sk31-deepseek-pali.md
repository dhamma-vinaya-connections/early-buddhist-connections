# BU-SK 31

## 605. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū thūpīkataṃ piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhanti…pe….

## 605.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Savatthi, in Jeta Grove, Anathapindika's Monastery. At that time the group-of-six bhikkhus were receiving almsfood piled up high...and so on.

‘‘Samatittikaṃ [samatitthikaṃ (ka.)] piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggahessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will receive almsfood filled to the proper level”—this is a training to be observed.

Samatittiko [samatitthikaṃ (ka.)] piṇḍapāto paṭiggahetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca thūpīkataṃ piṇḍapātaṃ paṭiggaṇhāti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

Almsfood filled to the proper level should be received. Whoever, being disrespectful, receives almsfood piled up high, commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional, unmindful, unknowing, in times of danger, if one is insane, or if one is the first offender.

Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The tenth training rule is concluded.

Khambhakatavaggo tatiyo.

The Chapter on Arms Akimbo is the third.
