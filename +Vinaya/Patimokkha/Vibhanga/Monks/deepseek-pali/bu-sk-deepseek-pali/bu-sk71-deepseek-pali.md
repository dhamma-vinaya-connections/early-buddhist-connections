# BU-SK 71

## 645. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū chamāya nisīditvā āsane nisinnassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe….

## 645.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī in Jeta's Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika's Monastery. Now at that time the group of six bhikkhus, sitting on the ground, were teaching the Dhamma to one seated on a seat [...].

‘‘Na chamāyaṃ nisīditvā āsane nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

‘I will not, sitting on the ground, teach the Dhamma to one seated on a seat who is not ill’—this is a training to be observed.

Na chamāyaṃ nisīditvā [nisinnena (aṭṭhakathā)] āsane nisinnassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca chamāyaṃ nisīditvā āsane nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

The Dhamma should not be taught, sitting on the ground, to one seated on a seat who is not ill. Whoever, out of disrespect, sits on the ground and teaches the Dhamma to one seated on a seat who is not ill commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if unintentional [...] for the first offender.

Aṭṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The eighth training rule is concluded.
