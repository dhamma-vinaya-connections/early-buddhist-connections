# BU-SK 74

## 648. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ṭhitā nisinnassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe….

## 648.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī in Jeta’s Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Park. Now at that time the group-of-six bhikkhus, while standing, were teaching the Dhamma to one who was seated….

‘‘Na ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

‘I will not, while standing, teach the Dhamma to one who is seated and not ill’: thus is the training to be observed.”

Na ṭhitena nisinnassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ṭhito nisinnassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not, while standing, teach the Dhamma to one who is seated and not ill. Whoever, out of disrespect, while standing, teaches the Dhamma to one who is seated and not ill, incurs an offense of wrong-doing.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional… or for the first offender.

Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The tenth training rule is concluded.
