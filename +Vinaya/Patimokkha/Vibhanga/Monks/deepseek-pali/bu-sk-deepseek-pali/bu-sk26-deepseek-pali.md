# BU-SK 26

## 600. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchanti…pe….

## 600.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Savatthi, in Jeta Grove, Anathapindika's Monastery. At that time the group-of-six bhikkhus were going indoors squatting...and so on.

‘‘Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not go indoors squatting”—this is a training to be observed.

Na ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ukkuṭikāya antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not go indoors squatting. Whoever, being disrespectful, goes indoors squatting, commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional...or if one is the first offender.

Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The fifth training rule is concluded.
