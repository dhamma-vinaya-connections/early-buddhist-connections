# BU-SK 16

## 590. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchanti kāyaṃ olambentā…pe….

## 590.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī, in Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. At that time the group-of-six bhikkhus were going into inhabited areas swaying their bodies, letting their bodies droop... and so on.

‘‘Na kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not go into inhabited areas swaying my body”—this is a training to be observed.

Na kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Kāyaṃ paggahetvā gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca kāyappacālakaṃ antaraghare gacchati kāyaṃ olambento, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not go into inhabited areas swaying the body. One should go with the body held steady. Whoever, out of disrespect, goes into inhabited areas swaying the body, letting the body droop, incurs an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if unintentional... or if one is a beginner.

Pañcamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The fifth training rule is concluded.
