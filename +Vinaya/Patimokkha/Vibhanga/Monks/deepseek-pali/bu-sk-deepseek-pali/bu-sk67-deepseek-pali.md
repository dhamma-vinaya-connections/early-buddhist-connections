# BU-SK 67

## 641. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sayanagatassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe….

## 641.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī in Jeta's Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika's Monastery. Now at that time the group of six bhikkhus were teaching the Dhamma to one lying down [...].

‘‘Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

‘I will not teach the Dhamma to one lying down who is not ill’—this is a training to be observed.

Na sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca antamaso chamāyampi nipannassa sayanagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

The Dhamma should not be taught to one lying down who is not ill. Whoever, out of disrespect, teaches the Dhamma to one lying down who is not ill—even if lying on the ground—commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if unintentional [...] for the first offender.

Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The fourth training rule is concluded.
