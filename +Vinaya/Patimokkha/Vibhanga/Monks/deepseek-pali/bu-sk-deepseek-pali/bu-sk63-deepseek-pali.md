# BU-SK 63

## 637. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū āvudhapāṇissa dhammaṃ desenti…pe….

## 637.At that time the Blessed One, the Buddha, was dwelling at Savatthi, in Jeta Grove, Anathapindika's Monastery. At that time the group-of-six bhikkhus were teaching the Dhamma to one holding a weapon…

‘‘Na āvudhapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not teach the Dhamma to one who is not ill and is holding a weapon,” this is a training to be observed.

Āvudhaṃ nāma cāpo kodaṇḍo.

A weapon means a bow or a crossbow.

Na āvudhapāṇissa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca āvudhapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

Dhamma should not be taught to one who is not ill and is holding a weapon. Whoever, through disrespect, teaches the Dhamma to one who is not ill and is holding a weapon incurs an offense of wrong-doing.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional… or for the first offender.

Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The tenth training rule is completed.

Surusuruvaggo chaṭṭho.

The sixth section, the Surusuru Division, is completed.
