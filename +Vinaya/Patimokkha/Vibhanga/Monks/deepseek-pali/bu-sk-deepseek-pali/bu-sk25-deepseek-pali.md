# BU-SK 25

## 599. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sasīsaṃ pārupitvā antaraghare nisīdanti…pe….

## 599.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Savatthi, in Jeta Grove, Anathapindika's Monastery. Now at that time the group-of-six bhikkhus sat indoors with their heads covered...and so on.

‘‘Na oguṇṭhito antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not sit indoors with my head covered”—this is a training to be observed.

Na oguṇṭhitena antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sasīsaṃ pārupitvā antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not sit indoors with the head covered. Whoever, being disrespectful, sits indoors fully covered with the head, commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, vāsūpagatassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional, unmindful, unknowing, if one is sick, if one has entered a dwelling, in times of danger, if one is insane, or if one is the first offender.

Catutthasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The fourth training rule is concluded.
