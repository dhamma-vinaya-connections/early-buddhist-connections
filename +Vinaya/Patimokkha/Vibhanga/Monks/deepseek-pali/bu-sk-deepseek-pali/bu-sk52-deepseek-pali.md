# BU-SK 52

## 626. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjanti…pe….

## 626.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī in Jeta's Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika's Park. Now at that time the group of six bhikkhus were eating making smacking sounds…and so on.

‘‘Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not eat making smacking sounds”—this is a training to be observed.

Na capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjitabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca capucapukārakaṃ bhuñjati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not eat making smacking sounds. Whoever, through lack of respect, eats making smacking sounds, incurs an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if unintentional…or if one is a first offender.

Dasamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The tenth training rule is concluded.

Kabaḷavaggo pañcamo.

The fifth chapter is the Chapter on Morsels.

6. Surusuruvaggo

6.The Chapter on Slurping.
