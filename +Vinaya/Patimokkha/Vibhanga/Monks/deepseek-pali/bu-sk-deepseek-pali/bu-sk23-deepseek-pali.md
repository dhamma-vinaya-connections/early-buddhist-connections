# BU-SK 23

## 597. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū khambhakatā antaraghare nisīdanti…pe….

## 597.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Savatthi, in Jeta Grove, Anathapindika's Monastery. Now at that time the group-of-six bhikkhus were sitting indoors with their arms akimbo...and so on.

‘‘Na khambhakato antaraghare nisīdissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not sit indoors with arms akimbo”—this is a training to be observed.

Na khambhakatena antaraghare nisīditabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā khambhaṃ katvā antaraghare nisīdati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not sit indoors with arms akimbo. Whoever, being disrespectful, sits indoors with one arm akimbo or both arms akimbo, commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca, assatiyā, ajānantassa, gilānassa, vāsūpagatassa, āpadāsu, ummattakassa, ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional, unmindful, unknowing, if one is sick, if one has entered a dwelling, in times of danger, if one is insane, or if one is the first offender.

Dutiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The second training rule is concluded.
