# BU-SK 22

## 596. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū khambhakatā antaraghare gacchanti…pe….

## 596.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Savatthi, in Jeta Grove, Anathapindika's Monastery. At that time the group-of-six bhikkhus were going indoors with their arms akimbo...and so on.

‘‘Na khambhakato antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not go indoors with arms akimbo”—this is a training to be observed.

Na khambhakatena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca ekato vā ubhato vā khambhaṃ katvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not go indoors with arms akimbo. Whoever, being disrespectful, goes indoors with one arm akimbo or both arms akimbo, commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional...or if one is the first offender.

Paṭhamasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The first training rule is concluded.
