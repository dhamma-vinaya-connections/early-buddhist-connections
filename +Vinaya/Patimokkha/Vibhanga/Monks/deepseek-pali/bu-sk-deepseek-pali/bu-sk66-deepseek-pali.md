# BU-SK 66

## 640. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū yānagatassa dhammaṃ desenti…pe….

## 640.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Sāvatthī in Jeta's Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika's Monastery. Now at that time the group of six bhikkhus were teaching the Dhamma to one in a vehicle [...].

‘‘Na yānagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

‘I will not teach the Dhamma to one in a vehicle who is not ill’—this is a training to be observed.

Yānaṃ nāma vayhaṃ ratho sakaṭaṃ sandamānikā sivikā pāṭaṅkī.

A vehicle means a conveyance, a chariot, a cart, a sedan chair, a litter, or a palanquin.

Na yānagatassa agilānassa dhammo desetabbo. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca yānagatassa agilānassa dhammaṃ deseti, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

The Dhamma should not be taught to one in a vehicle who is not ill. Whoever, out of disrespect, teaches the Dhamma to one in a vehicle who is not ill commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if unintentional [...] for the first offender.

Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The third training rule is concluded.
