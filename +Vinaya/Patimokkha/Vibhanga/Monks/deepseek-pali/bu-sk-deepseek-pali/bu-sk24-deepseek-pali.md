# BU-SK 24

## 598. Tena samayena buddho bhagavā sāvatthiyaṃ viharati jetavane anāthapiṇḍikassa ārāme. Tena kho pana samayena chabbaggiyā bhikkhū sasīsaṃ pārupitvā antaraghare gacchanti…pe….

## 598.At that time the Blessed One was dwelling at Savatthi, in Jeta Grove, Anathapindika's Monastery. At that time the group-of-six bhikkhus were going indoors with their heads covered...and so on.

‘‘Na oguṇṭhito antaraghare gamissāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā’’ti.

“I will not go indoors with my head covered”—this is a training to be observed.

Na oguṇṭhitena antaraghare gantabbaṃ. Yo anādariyaṃ paṭicca sasīsaṃ pārupitvā antaraghare gacchati, āpatti dukkaṭassa.

One should not go indoors with the head covered. Whoever, being disrespectful, covers the head completely and goes indoors, commits an offense of wrong conduct.

Anāpatti asañcicca…pe… ādikammikassāti.

There is no offense if it is unintentional...or if one is the first offender.

Tatiyasikkhāpadaṃ niṭṭhitaṃ.

The third training rule is concluded.
