## Vinaya Piṭaka - Monastic Rules

## Bhikkhunī Vibhaṅga - Sekhiya 48:  i.e. The bhikkhus' Sekhiya 48 Not to scatter the rice when eating.
### B. Brahmali

- Translated from the Pali by Bhikkhu Brahmali.   

#### Origin

1At one time the Buddha was staying at Sāvatthī in the Jeta Grove, Anāthapiṇḍika’s Monastery. At that time the bhikkhus from the group of six scattered rice while eating. …

#### Final ruling

2“‘I will not eat scattering rice,’ this is how you should train.”

3One should not scatter rice while eating. If a bhikkhu, out of disrespect, eats scattering rice, he commits an offense of wrong conduct.

#### Non-offenses

4There is no offense: if it is unintentional; if he is not mindful; if he does not know; if he is sick; if he discards rice while discarding trash; if there is an emergency; if he is insane; if he is the first offender.


5The eighth training rule is finished.
