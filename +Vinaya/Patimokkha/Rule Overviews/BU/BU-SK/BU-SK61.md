## BU-SK61 - Pāduka - Shoes

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| 61. na pādukāruḷhassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā | I will not give a teaching to anyone wearing shoes who is not sick,’ this is how you should train |
