## BU-SK72 - Uppathenagamana - Off the Path

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| 72. na uppathena gacchanto pathena gacchantassa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā | I will not give a teaching while walking next to the path to anyone walking on the path who is not sick,’ this is how you should train |
