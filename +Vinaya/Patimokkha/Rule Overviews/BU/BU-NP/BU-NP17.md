# bhikkhus - Nissaggiya Pacittiya - Relinquish with confession
## Kosiyavagga - Silk
## BU-NP17 - Eḷakalomadhovāpana - Bhikkhuni Made to Wash Wool

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Yo pana bhikkhu aññātikāya bhikkhuniyā eḷakalomāni dhovāpeyya vā rajāpeyya vā vijaṭāpeyya vā, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ | If any bhikkhu should have sheep’s wool washed, dyed, or carded by an unrelated bhikkhunī, [this is a case] involving expiation with forfeiture |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1 Object. wool that has not been made into cloth or yarn. 2 Effort. One tells an unrelated bhikkhunī to wash, dye, or beat the wool. 3 Result. The bhikkhunī washes, dyes, or beats the robe as requested
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > When she washes or dyes or combs the wool on her own initiative. bhikkhu who gets a bhikkhunī unrelated to him to wash woolen cloth or yarn that has not yet been used
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > The reason because the bhikkhunīs, washing, dyeing, and carding wool, neglect the training
> 
>
