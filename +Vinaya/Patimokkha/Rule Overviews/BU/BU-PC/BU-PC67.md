# bhikkhus - Pacittiya - Confession
## 7 Sappāṇaka-Vaggo - 7 Animals
## BU-PC67 - Saṁvidhāna - Travelling by Arrangement with Woman

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Yo pana bhikkhu mātugāmena saddhiṃ saṃvidhāya ekaddhānamaggaṃ paṭipajjeyya antamaso gāmantarampi, pācittiyaṃ | If a monk travels by arrangement with a woman, even just to the next village, he commits an offense entailing confession |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: a woman or women 2) Effort: (a) Having made an arrangement together with the woman to travel together, (b) one actually travels together with them as arranged (c) from one village to another
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If the bhikkhu and woman happen to travel together without having made an arrangement; if the woman proposes an arrangement, while the bhikkhu does not give his verbal assent; if either party leaves (or, apparently, both leave together) not as specified in the arrangement; if there are dangers; if he is mad
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Private transport, though—such as automobiles, trucks and vans—is an area that different Communities treat in differing ways. Some treat it under Pc 44. This rule thus forbids a bhikkhu from traveling together with a woman, by prior arrangement, on the same public transport
>
