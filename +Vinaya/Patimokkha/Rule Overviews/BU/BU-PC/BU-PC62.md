> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1 Object: Water containing living creatures. 2 Perception: One knows that they are there 3 Effort: all cases of using water containing living beings that are not covered by Pc 20
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If he makes use of it not knowing that it contains living things, knowing that it does not contain living things, knowing that they will not die from this use; if he is mad, if he is the first wrongdoer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Non-offense clauses here make no exception for a bhikkhu who uses water containing living beings either unthinkingly or unintentionally. Do not include the act of getting other people to use such water under this rule. Result is not a factor here
