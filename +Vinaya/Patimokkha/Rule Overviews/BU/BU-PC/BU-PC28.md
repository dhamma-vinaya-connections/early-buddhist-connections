# bhikkhus - Pacittiya - Confession
## 3 Bhikkhunovāda-Vaggo - 3 Instruction
## BU-PC28 - Nāvābhiruhana - Travelling on a Boat With a Nun

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Yo pana bhikkhu bhikkhuniyā saddhiṃ saṃvidhāya ekaṃ nāvaṃ abhiruheyya uddhaṃgāminiṃ vā adhogāminiṃ vā aññatra tiriyaṃ taraṇāya, pācittiyaṃ | If a monk boards a boat by arrangement with a nun, either to go upstream or downstream, except for the purpose of crossing, he commits an offense entailing confession |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: a bhikkhunī. 2) Effort: (a) Having made an arrangement together with her to get in a boat together, (b) one actually travels together with her as arranged,, except for crossing the river
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If it is for crossing over to the other bank; if they embark not having arranged; if the nun arranges (and) the monk does not arrange; if they embark without (making) a appointment; if there are dangers; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Bhikkhu traveling by arrangement with a bhikkhunī in a boat on the ocean incurs a dukkaṭa
> 
>
