# bhikkhus - Pacittiya - Confession
## 1 Musāvāda-Vaggo - 1 Lies
## BU-PC8 - Bhūtārocana - Disclose Attainment

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Yo pana bhikkhu anupasampannassa uttarimanussadhammaṃ āroceyya, bhūtasmiṃ pācittiyaṃ | If a monk truthfully tells a person who is not fully ordained of a superhuman quality, he commits an offense entailing confession |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Effort: One reports one's actual attainment of a superior human state 2) Object: to an unordained person, i.e., any human being who is not a bhikkhu or bhikkhunī
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If he speaks of what is a fact to one who is ordained; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
>
