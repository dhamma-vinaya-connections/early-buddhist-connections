> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: staple or non-staple food that is not leftover. 2) Effort: chew or consume staple or non-staple food at the wrong time
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > Offense if, having a reason, one consumes juice drinks, any of the five tonics, medicine, or water after noon or before dawnrise; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > No exception is granted to an ill bhikkhu, because there are a number of edibles an ill bhikkhu may consume at the wrong time without involving an offense: juice drinks, the five tonics, and medicines
