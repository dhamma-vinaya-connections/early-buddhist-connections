## BNI-PJ7 - Ukkhittānuvattikā - Siding with One Ejected

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ukkhittānuvattikā: Yā pana bhikkhunī samaggena saṅghena ukkhittaṃ bhikkhuṃ... anuvatteyya... ayampi pārājikā hoti asaṃvāsā. | 7. Siding with a Suspended Monk: Should any bhikkhunī follow (side with) a bhikkhu who has been suspended by a united Community... and she persists after being admonished three times, she is defeated and no longer in communion. |
