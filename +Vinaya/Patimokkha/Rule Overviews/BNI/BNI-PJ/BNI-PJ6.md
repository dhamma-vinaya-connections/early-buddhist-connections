## BNI-PJ6 - Vajjappaṭicchādikā - Conceal Offenses

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Vajjappaṭicchāditā: Yā pana bhikkhunī jānaṃ pārājikaṃ dhammaṃ ajjhāpannaṃ bhikkhuniṃ neva attanā paṭicodeyya na gaṇassa āroceyya... ayampi pārājikā hoti asaṃvāsā. | 6. Concealing a Defeat Offense: Should any bhikkhunī, knowing that another bhikkhunī has fallen into a Defeat offense, neither accuse her herself nor inform the group... she is defeated and no longer in communion. |
