# Nuns - Sekhiya - Training
## Surusuruvaggo - Slurping sound
## BNI-SK59 - Satthapāṇi - Holding Knife

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| 59. na satthapāṇissa agilānassa dhammaṃ desessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā | I shall not teach Dhamma to one who has a knife in [his] hand [and] who is not ill,” thus the training is to be done |
