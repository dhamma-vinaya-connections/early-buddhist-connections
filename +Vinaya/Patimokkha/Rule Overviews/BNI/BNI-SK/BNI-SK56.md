## BNI-SK56 - Sasitthaka - Washing Water with Rise

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| 56. na sasitthakaṃ pattadhovanaṃ antaraghare chaḍḍessāmīti sikkhā karaṇīyā | I shall not throw away bowl-washing water which has rice-grains [in it] in an inhabited area,” thus the training is to be done |
