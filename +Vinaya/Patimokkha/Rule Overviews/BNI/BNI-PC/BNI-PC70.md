# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 7 Gabbhinivaggo - 7 Pregnant Woman
## BNI-PC70 - Sahajīvinī-avūpakāsana - Not Departing With Disciple

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Apakaḍḍhana: Yā pana bhikkhunī sahajīviniṃ apakaḍḍheyya vā apakaḍḍhāpeyya vā, pācittiyaṃ. | 70. Dismissing: Should any bhikkhunī engage a companion and then dismiss her without reason... it is to be confessed. |
