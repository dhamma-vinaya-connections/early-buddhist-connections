# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 13 Cārittavaggo - 13 Go-calling
## BNI-PC135 - Anādariya - Disrespect

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Bhiṁsāpanā: Yā pana bhikkhunī bhikkhuniṁ bhiṁsāpeyya, pācittiyaṁ. | 135. Frightening: Should any bhikkhunī attempt to frighten another bhikkhunī, it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Effort: Having been admonished by a fellow bhikkhu who cites a rule formulated in the Vinaya is Paccitiya; or for sake of being “self-effacing, scrupulous, inspiring; for lessening (defilement) or arousing energy” that are not formulated is wrong doing if one shows disrespect 2) Object: for the bhikkhu or for the rule
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If, being admonished, one states simply that one was taught differently by one’s teachers.; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Wise policy would be to avoid showing disrespect for another person, regardless of the situation. if one expresses disrespect behind his or her back, it would count as offense here
> 
>
