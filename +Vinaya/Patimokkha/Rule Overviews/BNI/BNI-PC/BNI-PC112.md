# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 11 Bhūtagāma-Vaggo - 11 Plants
## BNI-PC112 - Anupakhajja - Encroaching

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Anupakhajja: Yā pana bhikkhunī jānaṁ saṅghike vihāre pubbupagataṁ bhikkhuniṁ anupakhajja seyyaṁ kappeyya... pācittiyaṁ. | 112. Intruding: Should any bhikkhunī knowingly lie down in a dwelling belonging to the Community so as to intrude on a bhikkhunī who arrived there first... it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: a bhikkhu who should not be forced to move. 2) Perception: One perceives him as such. 3) Effort: One intrudes on his space in a dwelling belonging to the Community 4) Intention: with the sole purpose of forcing him out
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If an ill man enters, if one pressed by cold or by heat enters, if there are accidents; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer. dukkaṭa in telling someone else to evict the bhikkhu—
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Commentary defines as anywhere within 75 cm. of the sleeping or sitting place where one knows, “This bhikkhu shouldn’t be forced to move.”
>
