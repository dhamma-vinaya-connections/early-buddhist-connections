# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 6 Ārāmavaggo - 6 Monastery
## BNI-PC57 - Apavāraṇā - Not Inviting

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Pavāraṇā: Yā pana bhikkhunī vassaṃ vutthā ubhatosaṅghe tisso vācāyo na pavāreyya diṭṭhena vā sutena vā parisaṅkāya vā, pācittiyaṃ. | 57. Pavarana: Should any bhikkhunī not invite the Community (Pavarana) at the end of the Rains... it is to be confessed. |
