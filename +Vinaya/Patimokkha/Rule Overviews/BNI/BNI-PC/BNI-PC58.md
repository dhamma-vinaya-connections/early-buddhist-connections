# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 6 Ārāmavaggo - 6 Monastery
## BNI-PC58 - Ovāda - Instruction

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ovāda: Yā pana bhikkhunī ovādāya vā saṃvāsāya vā na gaccheyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 58. Ovada (Advice): Should any bhikkhunī not go for the Ovadā (instruction) or the Uposatha... it is to be confessed. |
