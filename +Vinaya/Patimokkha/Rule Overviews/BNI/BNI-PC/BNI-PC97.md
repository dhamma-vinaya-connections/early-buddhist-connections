# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 10 Musāvāda-Vaggo - 10 Lies
## BNI-PC97 - Musāvāda - Lying

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Musāvāda: Sampajānamusāvāde pācittiyaṃ. | 97. Lying: (Start of Common Rules) Should any bhikkhunī tell a conscious lie... it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Intention: the aim to misrepresent the truth; and 2) Effort: the effort to make another individual know whatever one wants to communicate based on that aim
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > Who misrepresents the truth unintentionally like speaking quickly and saying one thing while meaning another
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Falsehoods conveyed not only by speech but also by writing or gesture or silence
> 
> > [!info]-  Formulas 
> > >If applicable from BMC1 Appendices  [[bmc1#^ch13]]
> > 
> > Cv 4 14.30 Senior confesses : C: Ahaṁ āvuso sambahulā nānā-vatthukāyo dukkaṭāyo āpattiyo āpanno. Tā paṭidesemi. A: Passatha bhante? C: Āma āvuso, passāmi. A: Āyatiṁ bhante saṁvareyyātha. C: Sādhu suṭṭhu āvuso saṁvarissāmi
>
