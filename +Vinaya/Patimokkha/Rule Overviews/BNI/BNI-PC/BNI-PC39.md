# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 4 Tuvaṭṭavaggo - 4 Sharing
## BNI-PC39 - Antovassa - During the Rainy Season

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Cārikā: Yā pana bhikkhunī cārikaṃ pakkameyya... pācittiyaṃ. | 39. Staying During Rains: Should any bhikkhunī go on a journey during the Winter/Summer (outside Rains) without reason... it is to be confessed. |
