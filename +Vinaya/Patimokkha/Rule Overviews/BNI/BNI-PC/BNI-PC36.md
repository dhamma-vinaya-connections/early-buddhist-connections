# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 4 Tuvaṭṭavaggo - 4 Sharing
## BNI-PC36 - Saṁsaṭṭha - Socializing

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Sansaṭṭhā: Yā pana bhikkhunī saṃsaṭṭhā vihareyya gahapatinā vā gahapatiputtena vā, pācittiyaṃ. | 36. Moving w/ Husband: Should any bhikkhunī associate closely with a householder or son... it is to be confessed. |
