## 7 Gabbhinivaggo - 7 Pregnant Woman
## BNI-PC63 - Asikkhita-sikkhamānā - Ordaining Untrained Candidate

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Sikkhamānā: Yā pana bhikkhunī dve vassāni chasu dhammesu asikkhitasikkhaṃ sikkhamānaṃ vuṭṭhāpeyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 63. Two Years Training: Should any bhikkhunī ordain a probationer (Sikkhamana) who has not trained for two years in the six rules... it is to be confessed. |
