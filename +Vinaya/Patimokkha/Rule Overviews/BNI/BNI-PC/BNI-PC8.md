# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 1 Lasuṇavaggo - 1 Garlic
## BNI-PC8 - Tirokuṭṭuccārachaḍḍana - Disposing Feces

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Uccāra: Yā pana bhikkhunī uccāraṃ vā passāvaṃ vā saṅkāraṃ vā vighāsaṃ vā tirokuṭṭe vā tiropākāre vā chaḍḍeyya vā chaḍḍāpeyya vā, pācittiyaṃ. | 8. Throwing Waste (Wall): Should any bhikkhunī throw feces/urine/trash over a wall or fence... it is to be confessed. |
