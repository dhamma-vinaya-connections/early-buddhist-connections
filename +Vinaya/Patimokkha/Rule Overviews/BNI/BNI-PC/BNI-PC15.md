# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 2 Rattandhakāravaggo - 2 Darkness
## BNI-PC15 - Anāpucchāpakkamana - Departing Without Taking Leave

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Kula-upagamana: Yā pana bhikkhunī kulasūpagatā kulesu... asāmike anāpucchā nikkhameyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 15. Leaving Before Master: Should any bhikkhunī leave a family home without asking the owner, after entering... it is to be confessed. |
