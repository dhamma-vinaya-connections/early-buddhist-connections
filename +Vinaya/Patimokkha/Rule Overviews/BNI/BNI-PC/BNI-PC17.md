# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 2 Rattandhakāravaggo - 2 Darkness
## BNI-PC17 - Anāpucchāsantharaṇa - Laying Bedding Without Permission

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Kula-sayana: Yā pana bhikkhunī kulasūpagatā kulesu... asāmike anāpucchā āsane nisīdeyya vā seyyaṃ vā kappeyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 17. Sleeping in Family House: Should any bhikkhunī enter a family house and sit or lie down without asking the owner... it is to be confessed. |
