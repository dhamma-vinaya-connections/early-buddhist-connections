## BNI-PC35 - Nikkaḍḍhana - Throwing Out a Monastic

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Uyyojana: Yā pana bhikkhunī bhikkhuniyā upassayaṃ datvā... kupitā anattamanā nikaḍḍheyya... pācittiyaṃ. | 35. Evicting: Should any bhikkhunī give a lodging to another and then angrily evict her... it is to be confessed. |
