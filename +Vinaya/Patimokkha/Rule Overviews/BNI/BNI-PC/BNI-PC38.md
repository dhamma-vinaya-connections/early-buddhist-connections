# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 4 Tuvaṭṭavaggo - 4 Sharing
## BNI-PC38 - Tiroraṭṭha - Outside Their Own Country

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Antoraṭṭhacārikā: Yā pana bhikkhunī antoraṭṭhaṃ cārikaṃ na careyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 38. Post-Rains Journey: Should any bhikkhunī fail to go on a journey after the Rains... it is to be confessed. |
