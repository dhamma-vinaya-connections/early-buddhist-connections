# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 12 Bhojana-Vaggo - 12 Food
## BNI-PC125 - Rahopaṭicchanna - Sitting in Concealed Place

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Paṇītabhojana: Yā pana bhikkhunī... paṇītabhojanāni attanā viññāpetvā bhuñjeyya, pācittiyaṁ. | 125. Fine Foods: Should any bhikkhunī... ask for fine foods (ghee, butter, oil, honey, molasses, fish, meat, milk, curds) for her own sake... it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Effort: One sits with her alone—without another person present—in a place private to the ear and to the eye, and secluded. 2) Object: woman is defined as female human being who knows what is properly an what is lewd. Paṇḍakas, female petas, female yakkhas, and animals in the form of a woman are grounds for a dukkaṭa. 3) Intention: One must be aiming at privacy for this factor to be fulfilled
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If some learned friend comes to be (present); if he stands, does not sit; if he is not desirous of a private place; if he sits down thinking about something else; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Woman here includes women as well. In other words, even if one is sitting with many women in the secluded area. even when his motives are pure, he is inviting the suspicions of others. the woman, may make any claims she likes about what went on while they were alone together
>
