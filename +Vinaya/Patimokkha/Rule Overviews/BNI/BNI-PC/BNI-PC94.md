# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 9 Chattupāhanavaggo - 9 Sunshade and Leather Footwear
## BNI-PC94 - Anāpucchā - Not Asking Permission

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Nahāna: Yā pana bhikkhunī... nahāyeyya... pācittiyaṃ. | 94. Bathing Frequency: Should any bhikkhunī bathe more frequently than every half-month... it is to be confessed. |
