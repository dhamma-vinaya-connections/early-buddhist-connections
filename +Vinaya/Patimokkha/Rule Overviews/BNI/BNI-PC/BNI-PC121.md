> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: staple or non-staple food that is not leftover. Stored-up means formally accepted by a bhikkhu on one day and eaten on the next or a later day. 2) Effort: chew or consume staple or non-staple food at the wrong time
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If , having stored (food) for the time being, he eats it in that time; if, having stored (food to be eaten) during a watch of the night, he eats it in a watch of the night; if, having stored (food) to be eaten during seven days, he eats it in seven days; if, when there is a reason, he uses (food to be eaten) during life; if he is mad; if he is the first wrong-doer, in the mere act of storing food
>
