# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 8 Kumāribhūtavaggo - 8 Maiden
## BNI-PC71 - Ūnavīsativassa-kumāribhūta - Ordaining Married Girls Under Twenty

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Kumāribhūta: Yā pana bhikkhunī ūnavīsativassaṃ kumāribhūtaṃ vuṭṭhāpeyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 71. Under 20: Should any bhikkhunī ordain a maiden under twenty years of age... it is to be confessed. |
