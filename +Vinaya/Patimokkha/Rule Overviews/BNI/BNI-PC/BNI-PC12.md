# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 2 Rattandhakāravaggo - 2 Darkness
## BNI-PC12 - Paṭicchannokāsa - Concealed Places

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Paṭicchanna: Yā pana bhikkhunī paṭicchanne okāse bhikkhunā saddhiṃ ekena ekā santiṭṭheyya vā sallapeyya vā, pācittiyaṃ. | 12. Secluded Place (Monk): Should any bhikkhunī stand or talk with a monk alone in a concealed place... it is to be confessed. |
