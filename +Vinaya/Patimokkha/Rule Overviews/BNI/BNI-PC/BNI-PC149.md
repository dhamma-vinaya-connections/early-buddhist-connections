> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: One has been admonished by a fellow bhikkhu who cites a rule formulated in the Vinaya. 2) Intention: One does not want to train oneself in line with the rule. 3) Effort: As a ploy to excuse oneself, one says something to the effect that one will not train in line with the rule
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > To avoid an offense in situations like this is to say that one will learn about the rule and train in line with it.
