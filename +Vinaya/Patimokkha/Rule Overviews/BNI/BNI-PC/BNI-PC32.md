## BNI-PC32 - Ekattharaṇatuvaṭṭana - Lying on the Same Sheet

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ekattharaṇa: Dve pana bhikkhuniyo ekattharaṇā tuvaṭṭeyyuṃ, pācittiyaṃ. | 32. Sleeping Two (Cover): Should two bhikkhunīs share one cover/blanket... it is to be confessed. |
