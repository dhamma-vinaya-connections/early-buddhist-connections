# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 10 Musāvāda-Vaggo - 10 Lies
## BNI-PC98 - Omasavāda - Speech Abusive

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Omasavāda: Omasavāde pācittiyaṃ. | 98. Insulting: Should any bhikkhunī speak abusively... it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Effort: One insults a person directly to his face.. 2) Object: The person is a bhikkhu. 3) Intention: One's motive is to humiliate him
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If he is “aiming at Dhamma, aiming at (the person’s) benefit, aiming at teaching, if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Must be made directly to the listener. It may be phrased either as sarcastic praise or as out-and-out abuse. Indirect insults are wrong doing
> 
> > [!info]-  Formulas 
> > >If applicable from BMC1 Appendices  [[bmc1#^ch13]]
> > 
> > S. “I, friend, having many times fallen into grave offences with different bases, these I confess J. Do you see, ven. sir? S. Yes, friend, I see J. In future, ven. sir, you should be restrained S. It is well indeed, friend. I shall be restrained
>
