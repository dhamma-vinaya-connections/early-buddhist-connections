# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 2 Rattandhakāravaggo - 2 Darkness
## BNI-PC14 - Dutiyikauyyojana - Dismissing a Companion

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Rathikā: Yā pana bhikkhunī rathikāya vā byūhe vā siṅghāṭake vā bhikkhunā saddhiṃ ekena ekā santiṭṭheyya vā sallapeyya vā... pācittiyaṃ. | 14. On a Road (Monk): Should any bhikkhunī stand or talk with a monk alone on a road or cul-de-sac... it is to be confessed. |
