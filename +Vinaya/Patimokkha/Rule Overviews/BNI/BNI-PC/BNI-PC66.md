# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 7 Gabbhinivaggo - 7 Pregnant Woman
## BNI-PC66 - Paripuṇṇadvādasavassa-asikkhita-gihigata - Ordaining Untrained Married Girls

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Dutiya-gihigatā: Yā pana bhikkhunī paripruṇṇadvādasavassaṃ gihigataṃ dve vassāni chasu dhammesu asikkhitasikkhaṃ vuṭṭhāpeyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 66. Married Girl (Training): Should any bhikkhunī ordain a married girl of twelve years who has not trained for two years... it is to be confessed. |
