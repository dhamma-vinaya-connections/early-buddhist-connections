# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 9 Chattupāhanavaggo - 9 Sunshade and Leather Footwear
## BNI-PC87 - Itthālaṅkāra - Jewellery

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Sāmaṇerī-ummaddāpanā: Yā pana bhikkhunī sāmaṇeriyā ummaddāpeyya vā parimaddāpeyya vā, pācittiyaṃ. | 87. Rubbing (Novice): Should any bhikkhunī have herself rubbed by a novice... it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > Jewellery: what goes on the head, what goes around the neck, what goes on the hands, what goes on the feet, what goes around the hips
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If she does it because she is sick; if she is insane; if she is the first offender
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Applicable to Bhikkhu as dukkata offences
>
