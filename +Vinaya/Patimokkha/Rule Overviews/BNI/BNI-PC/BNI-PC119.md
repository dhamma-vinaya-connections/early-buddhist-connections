# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 12 Bhojana-Vaggo - 12 Food
## BNI-PC119 - Dvittipattapūrapaṭiggahaṇa - Accepting Two or Three Bowls Full

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Paramparabhojana: Paramparabhojane aññatra samayā pācittiyaṁ. | 119. Turn-by-Turn: An out-of-turn meal is to be confessed, except on the proper occasions. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Effort: Receiving more than three bowlfuls 2) Object: of cakes or cooked grain-meal (sattu), use as presents or as provisions for a journey
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If he accepts two or three bowlfuls or less; if they give what is prepared neither as a present nor as provisions for a journey; if they give the remainder of what is prepared either as a present or as provisions for a journey; if they give because the journey is given up; if they belong to relations; if they are invited; if they are for the good of another; if it is by means of his own property; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > The purpose of this rule is to prevent bhikkhus from abusing a donor’s generosity and good faith
> 
>
