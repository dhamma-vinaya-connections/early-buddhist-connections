# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 5 Cittāgāravaggo - 5 Picture gallery
## BNI-PC41 - Rājāgāra - Royal Houses

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Rājāgāra: Yā pana bhikkhunī rājāgāraṃ vā cittāgāraṃ vā ārāmaṃ vā uyyānaṃ vā pokkharaṇiṃ vā dassanāya gaccheyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 41. Royal Pleasure House: Should any bhikkhunī go to see a royal pleasure house or picture gallery... it is to be confessed. |
