# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 4 Tuvaṭṭavaggo - 4 Sharing
## BNI-PC31 - Ekamañcatuvaṭṭana - Lying on the Same Bed

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ekamañca: Dve pana bhikkhuniyo ekamañce tuvaṭṭeyyuṃ, pācittiyaṃ. | 31. Sleeping Two (Bed): Should two bhikkhunīs share one bed... it is to be confessed. |
