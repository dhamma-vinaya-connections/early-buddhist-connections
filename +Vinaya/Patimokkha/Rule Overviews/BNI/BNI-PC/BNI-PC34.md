# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 4 Tuvaṭṭavaggo - 4 Sharing
## BNI-PC34 - Naupaṭṭhāpana - Not Nursing

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Dukkhita: Yā pana bhikkhunī dukkhitaṃ sahajīvaniṃ na upaṭṭhāpeyya na upaṭṭhāpāpeyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 34. Family Issues: Should any bhikkhunī hide the family problems of a student... it is to be confessed. |
