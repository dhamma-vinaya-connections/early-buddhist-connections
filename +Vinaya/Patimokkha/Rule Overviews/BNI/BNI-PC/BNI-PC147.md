# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 15 Diṭṭhivaggo - 15 Views
## BNI-PC147 - Ukkhittasambhoga - Living With an Ejected Bhikkhu

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ukkhitta: Yā pana bhikkhunī jānaṃ tathāvādiniṁ bhikkhuniṁ... sambhuñjeyya vā... pācittiyaṁ. | 147. Associating: Should any bhikkhunī knowingly eat/stay with a bhikkhunī who talks that way (and hasn't given up the view)... it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: a bhikkhu who has been suspended by a Community transaction and has not yet been restored. 2) Perception: One knows that he has been suspended and has not yet been restored 3) Effort: One communes with him, affiliates with him, or lies down in the same dwelling with him
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If he knows, “He is not suspended”; if he knows, “He was suspended, he is restored”; if he knows, “He has given up that view”; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > According to Cv.I.25-35, a bhikkhu may be suspended for: 1 He refuses to relinquish an evil view, as in the preceding rule; 2 he refuses to see an offense; or 3 he refuses to make amends for an offense
> 
> > [!info]-  Formulas 
> > >If applicable from BMC1 Appendices  [[bmc1#^ch13]]
> > 
> > Suṇātu me bhante saṅgho. Itthannāmassa bhikkhuno evarūpaṁ pāpakaṁ diṭṭhigataṁ uppannaṁ, “Tathāhaṁ bhagavatā dhammaṁ desitaṁ ājānāmi, yathā ye’me antarāyikā dhammā vuttā bhagavatā, te paṭisevato nālaṁ antarāyāyā” ti. So taṁ diṭṭhiṁ nappaṭinissajjati. Yadi saṅghassa pattakallaṁ, saṅgho Itthannāmaṁ bhikkhuṁ samanubhāseyya tassā diṭṭhiyā paṭinissaggāya. Esā ñatti
>
