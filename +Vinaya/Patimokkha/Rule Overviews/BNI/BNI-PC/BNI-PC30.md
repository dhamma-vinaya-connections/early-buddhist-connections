## BNI-PC30 - Kathinuddhāra - Ending the Robe-Making Season

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Kathinuddhāra: Yā pana bhikkhunī dhammikena kathinuddhārena kathinaṃ paṭibāheyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 30. Preventing Robe Settling: Should any bhikkhunī prevent the legal settling of robe distribution... it is to be confessed. |
