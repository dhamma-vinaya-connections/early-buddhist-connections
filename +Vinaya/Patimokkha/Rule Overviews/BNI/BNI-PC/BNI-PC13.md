# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 2 Rattandhakāravaggo - 2 Darkness
## BNI-PC13 - Ajjhokāsasallapana - Talking In the Open

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ajjhokāsa: Yā pana bhikkhunī ajjhokāse bhikkhunā saddhiṃ ekena ekā santiṭṭheyya vā sallapeyya vā, pācittiyaṃ. | 13. Open Air (Monk): Should any bhikkhunī stand or talk with a monk alone in the open air... it is to be confessed. |
