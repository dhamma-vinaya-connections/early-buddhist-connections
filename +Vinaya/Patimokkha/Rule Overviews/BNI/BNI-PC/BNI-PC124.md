# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 12 Bhojana-Vaggo - 12 Food
## BNI-PC124 - Sabhojana - Intruding on a Couple Enjoying Themselves

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Sannidhikāraka: Yā pana bhikkhunī sannidhikārakaṁ khādanīyaṁ vā bhojanīyaṁ vā khādeyya vā bhuñjeyya vā, pācittiyaṁ. | 124. Stored Food: Should any bhikkhunī eat staple or non-staple food that has been stored, it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1 Object: a couple together in their private quarters, with at least one of them desiring sexual intercourse with the other. 2 Effort: to sit—without another bhikkhu present—in the private area of the house,
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If both the man and woman have left the bedroom/private area; if neither of them is sexually aroused; if the building is not a “sleeping building”; if the bhikkhu is not in the private area; if he has a companion.; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > A bhikkhu should not sit intruding on a couple unless they both make him 100% certain that he is welcome
>
