# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 14 Jotivaggo - 14 Fire
## BNI-PC139 - Dubbaṇṇakaraṇa - Stains Making

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Upanandha: Yā pana bhikkhunī bhikkhuniyā pattaṁ vā cīvaraṁ vā... apanidheyya vā... pācittiyaṁ. | 139. Hiding Requisites: Should any bhikkhunī hide the bowl, robe, etc. of another bhikkhunī... it is to be confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1 Object:a new robe; 2 Effort: one makes use of it without first marking it
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If, having taken, he makes use of it; if what is allowable becomes destroyed; if what made the appearance allowable becomes worn away; if what was not made allowable becomes sewn together with what was made allowable; if there is a patch; if there is a braiding; if there is a binding hem; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > A bhikkhu should wear robes only that have been marked with an identifying mark. This doesn't include sitting cloths, handkerchiefs, or shoulder bags. The Commentary details and prohibitions have no basis in the Canon or the Great Standards, they are not binding
>
