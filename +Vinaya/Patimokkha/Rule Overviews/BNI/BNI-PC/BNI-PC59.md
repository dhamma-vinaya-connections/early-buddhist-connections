# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 6 Ārāmavaggo - 6 Monastery
## BNI-PC59 - Ovādūpasaṅkamana - Going to the Instruction

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Uposathapucchana: Anvaddhamāsaṃ bhikkhuniyā bhikkhusaṅghato dve dhammā paccāsiṃsitabbā: uposathapucchakañca ovādūpasaṅkamanañca. Taṃ atikkāmentiyā pācittiyaṃ. | 59. Asking for Ovada: Should any bhikkhunī not ask the date of the Uposatha or the teaching... it is to be confessed. |
