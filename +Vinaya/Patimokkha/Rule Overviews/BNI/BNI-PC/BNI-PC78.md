# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 8 Kumāribhūtavaggo - 8 Maiden
## BNI-PC78 - Anubandha-sikkhamānana-vuṭṭhāpana - Not Ordaining Trainees After She Follows

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Anuvasa: Yā pana bhikkhunī anuvassaṃ vuṭṭhāpeyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 78. Every Year: Should any bhikkhunī ordain a student every year (should be every other year)... it is to be confessed. |
