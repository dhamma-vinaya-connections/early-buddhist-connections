# Nuns - Pacittiya - Confession
## 12 Bhojana-Vaggo - 12 Food
## BNI-PC118 - Gaṇabhojana - Eating in Group

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Gaṇabhojana: Gaṇabhojane aññatra samayā pācittiyaṁ. | 118. Group Meal: A group meal is to be confessed, except on the proper occasions. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1 Object: group meal consist of any of the five types of staple foods to which four or more bhikkhus are invited. 2 Effort: applies exclusively to invitations to specific groups—not Communities—of four or more bhikkhus regardless of whether the invitation mentions the word “food” or “meal.” 3 Intention: specify the names or groups of bhikkhus to be invited
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If it is at a right time; if two or three eat together; if having walked one by one for alms, they eat having assembled together; if it is the regular supply of food; if it is food (allowed by) ticket; if it is (food given) on a day of the waxing or waning of the moon,if it is (given) on an Observance day, or the day after; setting aside the five (kinds of) meals, there is no offence in (eating) any other; if donor is from other religions; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Purposes are “For the restraint of evil-minded individuals, for the comfort of well-behaved bhikkhus, so that those with evil desires will not split the Community by (forming) a faction, and out of compassion for families.”
>
