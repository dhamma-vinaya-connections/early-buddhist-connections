## BNI-PC44 - Gihiveyyāvacca - Serving Householders

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Gihiveyyāvacca: Yā pana bhikkhunī gihiveyyāvaccaṃ kareyya, pācittiyaṃ. | 44. Household Work: Should any bhikkhunī do household work (cooking/serving) for laypeople... it is to be confessed. |
