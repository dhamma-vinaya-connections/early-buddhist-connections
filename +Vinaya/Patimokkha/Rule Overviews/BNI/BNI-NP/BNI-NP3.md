# Nuns - Nissaggiya Pacittiya - Relinquish with confession
## Pattavagga - Bowl
## BNI-NP3 - Cīvaraparivattana - Swapping Robes

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Cīvaraparivattana: Yā pana bhikkhunī bhikkhuniyā saddhiṃ cīvaraṃ parivattetvā sā pacchā kupitā anattamanā acchindeyya vā acchindāpeyya vā... nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. | 3. Exchanging Robes: Should any bhikkhunī exchange robes with another bhikkhunī and then later, angry and displeased, snatch it back or have it snatched back... it is to be forfeited and confessed. |
