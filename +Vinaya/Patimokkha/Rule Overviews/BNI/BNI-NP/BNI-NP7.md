# Nuns - Nissaggiya Pacittiya - Relinquish with confession
## Pattavagga - Bowl
## BNI-NP7 - Dutiyasaṁghikacetāpana - Exchanging Saṅgha Property 2nd

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Aññaviññāpana: Yā pana bhikkhunī aññātakaṃ gahapatiṃ vā gahapatāniṃ vā cīvaraṃ viññāpeyya... nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. | 7. Unrelated Donors (I): Should a bhikkhunī ask for a robe from an unrelated householder... (except at the right time)... it is to be forfeited and confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > Belonging to the Sangha, not to a group, not to an individual nun. Exchanges it for something else: if, apart from the purpose for which it was given, she exchanges it for something else, then for the effort there is an act of wrong conduct. When she gets it, it becomes subject to relinquishment
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If she uses the remainder; if she uses it after getting permission from the owners; if there is an emergency; if she is insane; if she is the first offender
> 
>
