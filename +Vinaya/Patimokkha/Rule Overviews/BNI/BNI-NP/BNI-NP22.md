> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object, money is any type of items used as currency. 2) Effort here includes any exchange in which the bhikkhu ends up with gold or money as a result of the exchange
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > By arranging a trade, one is accepting responsibility for the fairness of the trade. Relinquish to Sangha and not to be returned or benefit the offender, but used by Sangha through lay follower or thrown away by a selected bhikkhu. bhikkhu may not express a desire to his steward that he/she sell something belonging to him or take funds dedicated for his use and invest them for monetary return. Money obtained improperly, the object bought with the money is unallowable for all bhikkhus. But if obtained obtained properly under the preceding rule, the item bought is unallowable only for the bhikkhu buyer, and allowed to the rest
