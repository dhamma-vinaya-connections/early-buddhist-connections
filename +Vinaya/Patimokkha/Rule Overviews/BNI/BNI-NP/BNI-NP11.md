# Nuns - Nissaggiya Pacittiya - Relinquish with confession
## Jātarūparajata - Robes
## BNI-NP11 - Garupāvuraṇa - Heavy Cloaks

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Garupāvuraṇa: Yā pana bhikkhunī garupāvuraṇaṃ cetāpeyya catukkaṃsaparamā, tato ce uttari cetāpeyya, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. | 11. Heavy Cloth Price: Should any bhikkhunī obtain a heavy winter cloak for a price exceeding four 'kamsas'... it is to be forfeited and confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > A heavy cloak: whatever cloak is used in cold weather. Carries out an exchange to get: asks for
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If she carries out an exchange for one worth at most four kaṁsa coins; if she carries out an exchange for one worth less than four kaṁsa coins; if it is from relatives; if it is from those who have given an invitation; if it is for the benefit of someone else; if it is by means of her own property; if she gets one in exchange that has little value from someone who wants to exchange one of great value; if she is insane; if she is the first offender
>
