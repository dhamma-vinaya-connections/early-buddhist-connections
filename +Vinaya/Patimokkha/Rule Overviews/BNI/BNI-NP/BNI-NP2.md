# Nuns - Nissaggiya Pacittiya - Relinquish with confession
## Pattavagga - Bowl
## BNI-NP2 - Akālacīvara - Sharing Out-of-Season Robe Cloth

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Akālacīvara: Yā pana bhikkhunī akālacīvaraṃ kālacīvaranti adhiṭṭhaheyya bhājāpeyya vā, nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. | 2. Wrong Season Robes: Should any bhikkhunī distribute robe-cloth out of season (thinking it is in season), it is to be forfeited and confessed. |
