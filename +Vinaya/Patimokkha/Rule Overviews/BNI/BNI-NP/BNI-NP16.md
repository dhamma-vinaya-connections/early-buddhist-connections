> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: a piece of any of the six suitable kinds of robe-cloth, measuring at least four by eight fingerbreadths. 2) Effort: One asks, except at the proper time, for such cloth from a lay person who is not related back through one's great x 7 grandfathers. Even if one perceives the lay person to be related when in fact he/she isn't, that fulfills the factor here. 3) Result: One receives the cloth
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If it is at the right time; if they belong to relations; if they are invited; if it is for another; if it is by means of his own property; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
>
