# Nuns - Nissaggiya Pacittiya - Relinquish with confession
## Jātarūparajata - Robes
## BNI-NP16 - Aññātakaviññatti - Robe Asking to Unrelated People

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Nisīdanasanthata: Yā pana bhikkhunī nisīdanasanthataṃ kārāpayamānā purāṇasanthatassa sāmantā sugatavidatthi āḍātabbā... nissaggiyaṃ pācittiyaṃ. | 16. Sitting Rug: Should any bhikkhunī have a new sitting rug made without taking a piece of the old one... it is to be forfeited and confessed. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1) Object: a piece of any of the six suitable kinds of robe-cloth, measuring at least four by eight fingerbreadths. 2) Effort: One asks, except at the proper time, for such cloth from a lay person who is not related back through one's great x 7 grandfathers. Even if one perceives the lay person to be related when in fact he/she isn't, that fulfills the factor here. 3) Result: One receives the cloth
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If it is at the right time; if they belong to relations; if they are invited; if it is for another; if it is by means of his own property; if he is mad, if he is the first wrong-doer
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > If all of a bhikkhu's robes are snatched away or destroyed, should not approach other people while naked. he is permitted to take any cloth he finds there — robes, sheets, mats, pillow cases, or whatever — to wear as a makeshift robe as long as he has the intention of returning it when he obtains a proper robe
> 
> > [!info]-  Formulas 
> > >If applicable from BMC1 Appendices  [[bmc1#^ch13]]
> > 
> > Idaṃ me bhante cīvaraṃ aññātakaṃ gahapatikaṃ aññatra samayā viññāpitaṃ nissaggiyaṃ. Imāhaṃ āyasmato nissajjāmi, which means, \"This robe (robe-cloth) of mine, venerable sir, requested from an unrelated householder at other than the proper occasion, is to be forfeited. I forfeit it to you.\" For more than one robe: Imāni me bhante cīvarāni aññātakaṃ gahapatikaṃ aññatra samayā viññāpitāni nissaggiyāni. Imānāhaṃ āyasmato nissajjāmi
>
