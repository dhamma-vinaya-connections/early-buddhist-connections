# Nuns - Sanghadisesa - Suspension
##  - 
## BNI-SS4 - Ukkhittakaosāraṇa - Reinstating One Ejected

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ukkhittānussāpanā: Yā pana bhikkhunī samaggena saṅghena ukkhittaṃ bhikkhuniṃ dhammena vinayena satthusāsanena anapaloketvā kārakasaṅghaṃ anaññāya gaṇacchandaṃ ossāpeyya... ayaṃ paṭhamāpattikaṃ dhammamāpannā nissāraṇīyaṃ saṅghādisesaṃ. | 4. Reinstating a Suspended Nun: Should any bhikkhunī, without the approval of the Community of bhikkhunīs, reinstate a bhikkhunī who has been suspended by the Community... she commits a Saṅghādisesa offense. |
