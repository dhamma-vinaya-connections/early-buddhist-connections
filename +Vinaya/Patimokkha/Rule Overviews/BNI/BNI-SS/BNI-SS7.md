# Nuns - Sanghadisesa - Suspension
##  - 1 Admonition
## BNI-SS7 - Sañcaritta - Matchmaking

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Sañcarittā: Yā pana bhikkhunī sañcarittaṃ samāpajjeyya itthiyā vā purisa-matiṃ aroceyya purisassa vā itthi-matiṃ... saṅghādisesaṃ. | 7. The Go-Between: Should any bhikkhunī engage in conveying a man’s intentions to a woman or a woman’s intentions to a man, proposing marriage or an affair... she commits a Saṅghādisesa offense. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1. They are human being 2. They are unmarried 3. Accept the proposal from one party 4. Make an inquiry to the other party 5. Report back the findings to the first party
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If it is for the Order, or for a shrine, or if he is ill; if he is going on business, if he is mad, if he is a beginner
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > Includes helping to arrange not only marriages and affairs but also anything from appointments with a prostitute to arrangements for X to be Y’s date. Also if the bhikkhu gets someone else to act for him. Simply carrying a letter, not knowing its contents, would not fulfill the factor of effort. no offense for effect a reconciliation between an estranged couple who are not divorced, but a full offense for a couple who are divorce. Not involving any sort of romantic liaison—is also exempt from penalty as long as it is not a form of subservience to lay people Ss 13
>
