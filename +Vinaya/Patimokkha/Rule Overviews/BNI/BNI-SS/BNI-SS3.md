# Nuns - Sanghadisesa - Suspension
##  - 
## BNI-SS3 - Ekagāmantara - Walking Alone to the Next Village

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ekāgamanā: Yā pana bhikkhunī ekā vā gāmantaraṃ gaccheyya, ekā vā nadiṃ tareyya, ekā vā rattiṃ vippavaseyya, ekā vā gaṇamhā ohīyeyya... ayaṃ paṭhamāpattikaṃ dhammamāpannā nissāraṇīyaṃ saṅghādisesaṃ. | 3. Going Alone: Should any bhikkhunī go alone to a village, cross a river alone, stay away for a night alone, or lag behind her companion... she commits a Saṅghādisesa offense. |
