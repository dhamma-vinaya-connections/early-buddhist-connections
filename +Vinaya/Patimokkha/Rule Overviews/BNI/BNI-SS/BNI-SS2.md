## BNI-SS2 - Corīvuṭṭhāpikā - Ordaining a Criminal

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Corivuṭṭhāpanā: Yā pana bhikkhunī jānaṃ coriṃ vajjhābhisammataṃ anapaloketvā rājānaṃ vā saṅghaṃ vā gaṇaṃ vā pūgaṃ vā seṇiṃ vā vuṭṭhāpeyya... ayaṃ paṭhamāpattikaṃ dhammamāpannā nissāraṇīyaṃ saṅghādisesaṃ. | 2. Ordaining a Thief: Should any bhikkhunī knowingly give the full ordination to a woman thief who has been condemned to death... without obtaining permission from the king or the Order... she commits a Saṅghādisesa offense. |
