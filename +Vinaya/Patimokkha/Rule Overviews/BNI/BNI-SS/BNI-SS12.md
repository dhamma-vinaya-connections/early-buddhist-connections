## BNI-SS12 - Saṁsaṭṭhā - Socializing

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Samsaṭṭhā: Dve pana bhikkhuniyo saṃsaṭṭhā viharanti pāpācārā pāpasaddā pāpasilokā aññamaññissā vajjappaṭicchādikā... saṅghādisesaṃ. | 12. Improper Association: Should bhikkhunīs live in close association, entangled with one another, engaging in bad behavior and concealing each other's faults... they commit a Saṅghādisesa offense. |
