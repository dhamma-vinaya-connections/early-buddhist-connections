# Nuns - Sanghadisesa - Suspension
##  - 
## BNI-SS1 - Ussayavādikā - Litigious

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Ussayavādikā: Yā pana bhikkhunī gahapatinā vā gahapatiputtena vā dāsena vā kammakārena vā antamaso samaṇaparibbājakenapi saddhiṃ ussayavādikā vihareyya... ayaṃ paṭhamāpattikaṃ dhammamāpannā nissāraṇīyaṃ saṅghādisesaṃ. | 1. The Litigant: Should any bhikkhunī engage in a lawsuit (legal dispute) against a householder, a householder's son, a slave, or a worker... she commits a Saṅghādisesa offense. |
