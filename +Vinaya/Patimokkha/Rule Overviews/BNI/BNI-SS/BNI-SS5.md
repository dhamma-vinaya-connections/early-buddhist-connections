# Nuns - Sanghadisesa - Suspension
##  - 
## BNI-SS5 - Avassutāavassutassa - Receiving Food with Lust

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Avassutabhojanā: Yā pana bhikkhunī avassutā avassutassa purisapuggalassa hatthato khādanīyaṃ vā bhojanīyaṃ vā sahatthā paṭiggahetvā khādeyya vā bhuñjeyya vā... ayaṃ paṭhamāpattikaṃ dhammamāpannā nissāraṇīyaṃ saṅghādisesaṃ. | 5. Consenting to Food (Lust): Should any bhikkhunī, filled with desire, receive staple or non-staple food from the hand of a lustful man... she commits a Saṅghādisesa offense. |
