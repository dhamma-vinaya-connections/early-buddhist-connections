# Nuns - Sanghadisesa - Suspension
##  - 3 Admonitions
## BNI-SS15 - Bhedānuvattaka - Schismatic Followers

### 🌐Overview

| Rule in Pali  | Rule in English  |
| :-----------: | :--------------: |
| Bhedānuvattakā: Tassāyeva kho pana bhikkhuniyā bhikkhuniyo honti anuvattikā... saṅghādisesaṃ. | 15. Siding with a Schismatic: Should any bhikkhunī support a bhikkhunī who is causing a schism... after being admonished three times... she commits a Saṅghādisesa offense. |


> [!Info] Analisis of the rule
> 
> >[!info]- Offence Factors 
> > 
> > > From BMC1
> > 
> > 1. Supporting the schismatic 2. Being admonished in legal Vinaya-kamma 3. Completion of kamma-vàca 4. He does not give up effort and attitude
> 
> > [!info]- No Offence 
> > > From Vibhanga
> > 
> > If they have not been admonished, if they give it up, if they are mad, out of their minds, in pain, beginners
> 
> > [!info]- Useful Notes
> > > From various authors
> > 
> > This rule and the previos one are designed to prevent behavior in the bud before they can become schismatic. If the schismatic mentioned in the preceding rule begins to attract adherents, they are to be treated under this rule — and quickly, before the schismatic gains a fourth adherent because 1 One Community cannot impose a penalty on another Community (four or more bhikkhus) in any one transaction (Mv.IX.2). 2) Penalties of this sort may be imposed only with the unanimous agreement of all the bhikkhus present in the meeting
>
